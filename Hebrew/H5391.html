H5391 נָשַׁךְ / נשך

nâshak

(naw-shak' | naw-SHAHK | na-SHAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to strike with a sting (as a serpent); figuratively, to oppress with interest on a loan; bite, lend upon usury.

  • to bite
    • (Qal) to bite
    • (Piel) to bite
  • to pay, give interest, lend for interest or usury
    • (Qal) to give interest
    • (Hiphil) to make to give interest
