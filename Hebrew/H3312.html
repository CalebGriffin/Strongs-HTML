H3312 יְפֻנֶּה / יפנה

Yᵉphunneh

(yef-oon-neh' | yeh-poon-NEH | yeh-foo-NEH)

Definition

proper masculine noun; He will be facing; from [H6437]; he will be prepared; Jephunneh, the name of two Israelites; Jephunneh.

  • a Kenezite and father of Caleb the spy from the tribe of Judah
  • son of Jether and descendant of Asher
