H4295 מַטָּה / מטה

maṭṭâh

(mat'-taw | maht-TAW | ma-TA)

Definition

adverb; from [H5786] with directive enclitic appended; downward, below or beneath; often adverbially with or without prefixes; beneath, down(-ward), less, very low, under(-neath).

  • downwards, below
    • downwards
    • under (of age)
    • beneath
