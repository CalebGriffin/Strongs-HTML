H6536 פָּרַס / פרס

pâraç

(paw-ras' | paw-RAHS | pa-RAHS)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to break in pieces, i.e., (usually without violence) to split, distribute; deal, divide, have hoofs, part, tear.

  • to divide, break in two
    • (Qal) to break, break up
    • (Hiphil) to be divided (of hoofs)
