H7156 קִרְיָתַיִם / קריתים

Qiryâthayim

(keer-yaw-thah'-yim | kir-yaw-ta-YIM | keer-ya-ta-YEEM)

Definition

proper locative noun; two cities; dual of [H7151]; double city; Kirjathaim, the name of two placed in Palestine; Kiriathaim, Kirjathaim.

  • a city east of the Jordan in Moab
  • a town in Naphtali allotted to the Gershonite Levites
