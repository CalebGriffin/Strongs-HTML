H8252 שָׁקַט / שקט

shâqaṭ

(shaw-kat' | shaw-KAHT | sha-KAHT)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to repose (usually figurative); appease, idleness, (at, be at, be in, give) quiet(-ness), (be at, be in, give, have, take) rest, settle, be still.

  • to be quiet, be tranquil, be at peace, be quiet, rest, lie still, be undisturbed
    • (Qal)
      • to be quiet, be undisturbed
        • to be at peace (of land)
      • to be quiet, be inactive
    • (Hiphil)
      • to show quietness
        • quietness, display of quietness (substantive)
      • to quiet, be quiet
      • to cause quietness, pacify, allay
