H5877 עֵין חָצוֹר / עין חצור

ʻÊyn Châtsôwr

(ane khaw-tsore' | ane haw-TSORE | ane ha-TSORE)

Definition

proper locative noun; fount of Hazor; from [H5869] and the same as [H2674]; fountain of a village; En-Chatsor, a place in Palestine; En-hazor.

  • one of the fenced cities in the territory of Naphtali
