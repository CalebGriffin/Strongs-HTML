H5797 עֹז / עז

ʻôz

(oze | ohdz | oze)

Definition

masculine noun; or (fully) עוֹז; from [H5810]; strength in various applications (force, security, majesty, praise); boldness, loud, might, power, strength, strong.

  • might, strength
    • material or physical
    • personal or social or political
