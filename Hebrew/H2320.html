H2320 חֹדֶשׁ / חדש

chôdesh

(kho'-desh | hoh-DESH | hoh-DESH)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H2318]; the new moon; by implication, a month; month(-ly), new moon.

  • the new moon, month, monthly
    • the first day of the month
    • the lunar month
