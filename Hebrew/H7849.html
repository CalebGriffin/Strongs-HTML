H7849 שָׁטַח / שטח

shâṭach

(shaw-takh' | shaw-TA | sha-TAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to expand; all abroad, enlarge, spread, stretch out.

  • to spread, spread abroad, stretch out
    • (Qal)
      • to spread
      • spreading, expanding (participle)
    • (Piel) to spread out
