H3013 יָגָה / יגה

yâgâh

(yaw-gaw' | yaw-ɡAW | ya-ɡA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to grieve; afflict, cause grief, grieve, sorrowful, vex.

  • to afflict, grieve, suffer, cause grief
    • (Niphal) grieved, grieving (participle)
    • (Piel) to grieve
    • (Hiphil) to cause grief, cause sorrow
