H5378 נָשָׁא / נשא

nâshâʼ

(naw-shaw' | naw-SHAW | na-SHA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (perhaps identical with [H5377], through the idea of imposition); to lend on interest; by implication, to dun for debt; [lexicon-key|×] debt, exact, giver of usury.

  • to lend on interest or usury, be a creditor
    • (Qal) creditor (participle)
    • (Hiphil) to act as a creditor
