H6168 עָרָה / ערה

ʻârâh

(aw-raw' | aw-RAW | ah-RA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be (causatively, make) bare; hence, to empty, pour out, demolish; leave destitute, discover, empty, make naked, pour (out), rase, spread self, uncover.

  • to be bare, be nude, uncover, leave destitute, discover, empty, raze, pour out
    • (Piel)
      • to bare, lay bare
      • to lay bare by emptying, empty
      • to pour out
    • (Hiphil)
      • to make naked, strip bare (of sexual offences)
      • to pour out
    • (Niphal) to be poured out, be exposed
    • (Hithpael)
      • to expose oneself, make oneself naked
      • pouring oneself, spreading oneself (participle)
