H6083 עָפָר / עפר

ʻâphâr

(aw-fawr' | aw-PAWR | ah-FAHR)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H6080]; dust (as powdered or gray); hence, clay, earth, mud; ashes, dust, earth, ground, morter, powder, rubbish.

  • dry earth, dust, powder, ashes, earth, ground, mortar, rubbish
    • dry or loose earth
    • debris
    • mortar
    • ore
