H6084 עׇפְרָה / עפרה

ʻOphrâh

(of-raw' | ope-RAW | ofe-RA)

Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; fawn; feminine of [H6082]; female fawn; Ophrah, the name of an Israelite and of two places in Palestine; Ophrah.

  • a Judaite, son of Meonothai (noun proper masculine)
  • a town of Benjamin approximately 5 miles (8 kilometers) east of Bethel (noun proper locative)
  • a place in Manasseh, native place of Gideon; probably located close to Shechem (noun proper locative)
