H8588 תַּעֲנוּג / תענוג

taʻănûwg

(tah-an-oog' | ta-uh-NOOɡ | ta-uh-NOOɡ)

Definition

masculine noun; or תַּעֲנֻג; and (feminine) תַּעֲנֻגָה; from [H6026]; luxury; delicate, delight, pleasant.

  • daintiness, luxury, exquisite delight, pleasantness, delight, comfort
    • luxury
    • dainty
    • delight
