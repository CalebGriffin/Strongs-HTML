H5643 סֵתֶר / סתר

çêther

(say'-ther | say-TER | say-TER)

Definition

masculine noun; or (feminine) סִתְרָה; (Deuteronomy 32:38), from [H5641]; a cover (in a good or a bad, a literal or a figurative sense); backbiting, covering, covert, [lexicon-key|×] disguise(-th), hiding place, privily, protection, secret(-ly) (place).

  • covering, shelter, hiding place, secrecy (noun masculine)
    • covering, cover
    • hiding place, shelter, secret place
    • secrecy
      • secrecy (of tongue being slanderous)
  • shelter, protection (noun feminine)
