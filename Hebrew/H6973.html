H6973 קוּץ / קוץ

qûwts

(koots | koots | koots)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (identical with through the idea of severing oneself from (compare [H6962])); to be (causatively, make) disgusted or anxious; abhor, be distressed, be grieved, loathe, vex, be weary.

  • to be grieved, loathe, abhor, feel a loathing or abhorrence or sickening dread
    • (Qal)
      • to feel a loathing at, abhor
      • to feel a sickening dread
    • (Hiphil)
      • to cause sickening dread
      • to cause loathing
