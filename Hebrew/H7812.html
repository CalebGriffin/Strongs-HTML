H7812 שָׁחָה / שחה

shâchâh

(shaw-khaw' | shaw-HAW | sha-HA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to depress, i.e., prostrate (especially reflexive, in homage to royalty or God); bow (self) down, crouch, fall down (flat), humbly beseech, do (make) obeisance, do reverence, make to stoop, worship.

  • to bow down
    • (Qal) to bow down
    • (Hiphil) to depress (figuratively)
    • (Hithpael)
      • to bow down, prostrate oneself
        • before superior in homage
        • before God in worship
        • before false gods
        • before angel
