H3848 לְבַשׁ / לבש

lᵉbash

(leb-ash' | leh-BAHSH | leh-VAHSH)

Definition

verb; corresponding to [H3847]; properly, wrap around, i.e., (by implication) to put on a garment or clothe (oneself, or another), literally or figuratively; clothe.

  • to be clothed
    • (Peal) to be clothed
    • (Aphel) to clothe someone
