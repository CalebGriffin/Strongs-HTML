H6869 צָרָה / צרה

tsârâh

(tsaw-raw' | tsaw-RAW | tsa-RA)

Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [H6862]; transitively, a female rival; adversary, adversity, affliction, anguish, distress, tribulation, trouble.

  • straits, distress, trouble
  • vexer, rival wife
