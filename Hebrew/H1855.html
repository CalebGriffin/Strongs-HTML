H1855 דְּקַק / דקק

dᵉqaq

(dek-ak' | deh-KAHK | deh-KAHK)

Definition

verb; corresponding to [H1854]; to crumble or (transitive) crush; break to pieces.

  • to break into pieces, fall into pieces, be shattered
    • (Peal) to be shattered
    • (Aphel) to break into pieces
