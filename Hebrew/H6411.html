H6411 פְּלָיָה / פליה

Pᵉlâyâh

(pel-aw-yaw' | peh-law-YAW | peh-la-YA)

Definition

proper masculine noun; Jehovah does wonders; or פְּלָאיָה; from [H6381] and [H3050]; Jah has distinguished; Pelajah, the name of three Israelites; Pelaiah.

  • son of Elioenai and a descendant of David in the royal line
  • a priest who helped Ezra to expound the law to the people and also sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
