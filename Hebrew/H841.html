H841 אֲשַׂרְאֵלָה / אשראלה

ʼĂsarʼêlâh

(as-ar-ale'-aw | uh-sahr-ay-LAW | uh-sahr-ay-LA)

Definition

proper masculine noun; God holds; from the same as [H840]; right toward God; Asarelah, an Israelite; Asarelah.

  • a son of Asaph, appointed sanctuary musician by David

Note

Compare [H3480].
