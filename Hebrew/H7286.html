H7286 רָדַד / רדד

râdad

(raw-dad' | raw-DAHD | ra-DAHD)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to tread in pieces, i.e., (figuratively) to conquer, or (specifically) to overlay; spend, spread, subdue.

  • to beat down, beat out, subdue
    • (Qal) to beat down (figuratively)
    • (Hiphil) to beat out
