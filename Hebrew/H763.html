H763 אֲרַם נַהֲרַיִם / ארם נהרים

ʼĂram Nahărayim

(ar-am' nah-har-ah'-yim | uh-RAHM na-huh-ra-YIM | uh-RAHM na-huh-ra-YEEM)

Definition

proper locative noun; Aram of the two rivers; from [H758] and the dual of [H5104]; Aram of (the) two rivers (Euphrates and Tigris) or Mesopotamia; Aram-naharaim, Mesopotamia.

  • Mesopotamia
