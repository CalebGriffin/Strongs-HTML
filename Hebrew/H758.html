H758 אֲרָם / ארם

ʼĂrâm

(arawm' | uh-RAWM | uh-RAHM)

Definition

masculine noun, proper masculine noun; exalted; from the same as [H759]; the highland; Aram or Syria, and its inhabitants; also the name of the son of Shem, a grandson of Nahor, and of an Israelite; Aram, Mesopotamia, Syria, Syrians.

  • Aram or Syria the nation
  • the Syrian or Aramean people
  • fifth son of Shem
  • a grandson of Nahor
  • a descendant of Asher
