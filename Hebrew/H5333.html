H5333 נְצִיב / נציב

nᵉtsîyb

(nets-eeb' | neh-TSEEB | neh-TSEEV)

Definition

masculine noun; or נְצִב; from [H5324]; something stationary, i.e., a prefect, a military post, a statue; garrison, officer, pillar.

  • set over, something placed, pillar, prefect, garrison, post
    • pillar
    • prefect, deputy
