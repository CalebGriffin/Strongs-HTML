H5496 סוּת / סות

çûwth

(sooth | soot | soot)

Definition

verb; perhaps denominative from [H7898]; properly, to prick, i.e., (figuratively) stimulate; by implication, to seduce; entice, move, persuade, provoke, remove, set on, stir up, take away.

  • to incite, allure, instigate, entice
    • (Hiphil)
      • to incite (to a request)
      • to allure, lure
      • to instigate (bad sense)
