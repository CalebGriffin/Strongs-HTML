H990 בֶּטֶן / בטן

beṭen

(beh'-ten | beh-TEN | beh-TEN)

Definition

feminine noun; from an unused root probably meaning to be hollow; the belly, especially the womb; also the bosom or body of anything; belly, body, [lexicon-key|+] as they be born, [lexicon-key|+] within, womb.

  • belly, womb, body
    • belly, abdomen
      • as seat of hunger
      • as seat of mental faculties
      • of depth of Sheol (figuratively)
    • womb
