H7980 שָׁלַט / שלט

shâlaṭ

(shaw-lat' | shaw-LAHT | sha-LAHT)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to dominate, i.e., govern; by implication, to permit; (bear, have) rule, have dominion, give (have) power.

  • to domineer, exercise power over, dominate, have mastery, be master, lord it over
    • (Qal) to domineer, lord it over, become master
    • (Hiphil)
      • to give power of
      • to get mastery of
