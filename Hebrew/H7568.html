H7568 רֶשֶׁת / רשת

resheth

(reh'-sheth | reh-SHET | reh-SHET)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H3423]; a net (as catching animals); net(-work).

  • net
    • net
      • for catching
      • of judgment (figuratively)
      • of leaders leading people to sin
      • as a trap for man
    • network (brazen - for altar of tabernacle)
