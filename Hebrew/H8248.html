H8248 שָׁקָה / שקה

shâqâh

(shaw-kaw' | shaw-KAW | sha-KA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to quaff, i.e., (causatively) to irrigate or furnish a potion to; cause to (give, give to, let, make to) drink, drown, moisten, water.

  • to give to drink, irrigate, drink, water, cause to drink water
    • (Hiphil)
      • to water, irrigate
      • to water, give drink to
    • (Pual) to be watered
    • (Niphal) variant
