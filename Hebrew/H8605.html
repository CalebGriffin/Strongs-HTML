H8605 תְּפִלָּה / תפלה

tᵉphillâh

(tef-il-law' | teh-pil-LAW | teh-fee-LA)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H6419]; intercession, supplication; by implication, a hymn; prayer.

  • prayer
    • prayer
    • pray a prayer
    • house of prayer
    • hear prayer
    • in Psalm titles (of poetic or liturgical prayer)
