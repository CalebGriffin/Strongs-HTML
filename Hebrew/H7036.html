H7036 קָלוֹן / קלון

qâlôwn

(kaw-lone' | kaw-LONE | ka-LONE)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H7034]; disgrace; (by implication) the pudenda; confusion, dishonour, ignominy, reproach, shame.

  • shame, disgrace, dishonour, ignominy
    • ignominy (of nation)
    • dishonour, disgrace (personal)
