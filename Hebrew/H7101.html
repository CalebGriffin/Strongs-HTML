H7101 קָצִין / קצין

qâtsîyn

(kaw-tseen' | kaw-TSEEN | ka-TSEEN)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H7096] in the sense of determining; a magistrate (as deciding) or other leader; captain, guide, prince, ruler.

  • chief, ruler, commander
    • chief, commander (in war)
    • dictator
    • ruler (of one in authority)

Note

Compare [H6278].
