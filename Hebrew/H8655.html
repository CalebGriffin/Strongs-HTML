H8655 תְּרָפִים / תרפים

tᵉrâphîym

(ter-aw-feme' | teh-raw-PEEM | teh-ra-FEEM)

Definition

masculine noun; plural from [H7495]; a healer; Teraphim (singular or plural) a family idol; idols(-atry), images, teraphim.

  • idolatry, idols, image(s), teraphim, family idol
    • a kind of idol used in household shrine or worship
