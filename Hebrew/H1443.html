H1443 גָּדַר / גדר

gâdar

(gaw-dar' | ɡaw-DAHR | ɡa-DAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to wall in or around; close up, fence up, hedge, inclose, make up (a wall), mason, repairer.

  • to wall up, wall off, close off, build a wall
    • (Qal)
      • to wall up, shut off
      • masons (participle)
