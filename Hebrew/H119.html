H119 אָדַם / אדם

ʼâdam

(aw-dam' | aw-DAHM | ah-DAHM)

Definition

verb; אדם; to show blood (in the face), i.e., flush or turn rosy; be (dyed, made) red (ruddy).

  • to be red, red
    • (Qal) ruddy (of Nazarites)
    • (Pual)
      • to be rubbed red
      • dyed red
      • reddened
    • (Hiphil)
      • to cause to show red
      • to glare
      • to emit (show) redness
    • (Hithpael)
      • to redden
      • to grow red
      • to look red
