H5287 נָעַר / נער

nâʻar

(naw-ar' | naw-AR | na-AR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (probably identical with [H5286], through the idea of the rustling of mane, which usually accompanies the lion's roar); to tumble about; shake (off, out, self), overthrow, toss up and down.

  • to shake, shake out or off
    • (Qal) to shake out, show emptiness
    • (Niphal)
      • to be shaken
      • to shake oneself
    • (Piel) to shake off or out
    • (Hithpael) to shake oneself
