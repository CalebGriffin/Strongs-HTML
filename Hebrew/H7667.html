H7667 שֶׁבֶר / שבר

sheber

(sheh'-ber | sheh-BER | sheh-VER)

Definition

masculine noun; or שֵׁבֶר; from [H7665]; a fracture, figuratively, ruin; specifically, a solution (of a dream); affliction, breach, breaking, broken(-footed, -handed), bruise, crashing, destruction, hurt, interpretation, vexation.

  • breaking, fracture, crushing, breach, crash, ruin, shattering
    • breaking, fracture, shattering, crushing
    • crashing
    • breaking (of a dream), interpretation
    • quarries
