H6817 צָעַק / צעק

tsâʻaq

(tsaw-ak' | tsaw-AK | tsa-AK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to shriek; (by implication) to proclaim (an assembly); [lexicon-key|×] at all, call together, cry (out), gather (selves) (together).

  • to cry, cry out, call, cry for help
    • (Qal)
      • to cry, cry out (for help)
      • to cry, cry out (in distress or need)
      • to make outcry, clamour
    • (Niphal) to be summoned
    • (Piel) to cry aloud (in grief)
    • (Hiphil) to call together
