H2921 טָלָא / טלא

ṭâlâʼ

(taw-law' | taw-LAW | ta-LA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to cover with pieces; i.e., (by implication) to spot or variegate (as tapestry); clouted, with divers colours, spotted.

  • to patch, spot, be spotted, be coloured
    • (Qal)
      • to spot
      • spotted (participle)
    • (Pual) to be patched
