H8454 תּוּשִׁיָּה / תושיה

tûwshîyâh

(too-shee-yaw' | too-shih-YAW | too-shee-YA)

Definition

feminine noun; or תֻּשִׁיָּה; from an unused root probably meaning to substantiate; support or (by implication) ability, i.e., (direct) help, (in purpose) an undertaking, (intellectual) understanding; enterprise, that which (thing as it) is, substance, (sound) wisdom, working.

  • wisdom, sound knowledge, success, sound or efficient wisdom, abiding success
    • sound or efficient wisdom
    • abiding success (of the effect of sound wisdom)
