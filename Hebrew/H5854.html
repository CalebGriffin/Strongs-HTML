H5854 עַטְרוֹת בֵּית יוֹאָב / עטרות בית יואב

ʻAṭrôwth bêyth Yôwʼâb

(at-roth' bayth yoawb' | at-ROTE bate yoh-AWB | at-ROTE bate yoh-AV)

Definition

proper locative noun; the crowns of; from the same as [H5852] and [H1004] and [H3097]; crowns of the house of Joab; Atroth-beth-Joab, a place in Palestine; Ataroth the house of Joab.

  • the house of Joab, a place in Judah
