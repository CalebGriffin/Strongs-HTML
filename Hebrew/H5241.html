H5241 נְמוּאֵל / נמואל

Nᵉmûwʼêl

(nem-oo-ale' | neh-moo-ALE | neh-moo-ALE)

Definition

proper masculine noun; day of God; apparently for [H3223]; Nemuel, the name of two Israelites; Nemuel.

  • the eldest son of Simeon
  • a Reubenite, son of Eliab and brother of Dathan and Abiram in the time of the exodus
