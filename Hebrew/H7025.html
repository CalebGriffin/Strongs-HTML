H7025 קִיר חֶרֶשׂ / קיר חרש

Qîyr Cheres

(keer kheh'-res | keer heh-RES | keer heh-RES)

Definition

proper locative noun; wall of potsherds; or (feminine of the latter word) קִיר חֲרֶשֶׂת; from [H7023] and [H2789]; fortress of earthenware; Kir-Cheres or Kir-Chareseth, a place in Moab; Kir-haraseth, Kir-hareseth, Kirharesh, Kir-heres.

  • one of the two chief strongholds of Moab
