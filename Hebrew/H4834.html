H4834 מָרַץ / מרץ

mârats

(maw-rats' | maw-RAHTS | ma-RAHTS)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to press, i.e., (figuratively) to be pungent or vehement; to irritate; embolden, be forcible, grievous, sore.

  • to be or make sick
    • (Niphal) to be grievous or painful
    • (Hiphil) to make sick
