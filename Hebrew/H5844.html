H5844 עָטָה / עטה

ʻâṭâh

(aw-taw' | aw-TAW | ah-TA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to wrap, i.e., cover, veil, clothe, or roll; array self, be clad, (put a) cover (-ing) (self), fill, put on, [lexicon-key|×] surely, turn aside.

  • to cover, enwrap, wrap oneself, envelop oneself
    • (Qal)
      • to wrap, envelop oneself
      • to cover (the beard in mourning)
      • mourner, one who covers (participle)
    • (Hiphil) to cover, enwrap, envelop
  • to grasp
    • (Qal) to grasp
