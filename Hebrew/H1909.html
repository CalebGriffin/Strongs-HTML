H1909 הֲדַדְעֶזֶר / הדדעזר

Hădadʻezer

(had-ad-eh'-zer | huh-dahd-eh-DZER | huh-dahd-eh-ZER)

Definition

proper masculine noun; Hadad is help; from [H1908] and [H5828]; Hadad (is his) help; Hadadezer, a Syrian king, possibly a royal title; Hadadezer.

  • son of Rehob, a king of Syria-Zobah defeated by David's army

Note

Compare [H1928].
