H6514 פְּרוּדָא / פרודא

Pᵉrûwdâʼ

(per-oo-daw' | peh-roo-DAW | peh-roo-DA)

Definition

proper masculine noun; grain, kernel; or פְּרִידָא; from [H6504]; dispersion; Peruda or Perida, one of 'Solomon's servants'; Perida, Peruda.

  • an ancestor of a family of temple slaves who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
