H6191 עָרַם / ערם

ʻâram

(aw-ram' | aw-RAHM | ah-RAHM)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to be (or make) bare; but used only in the derivative sense (through the idea perhaps of smoothness) to be cunning (usually in a bad sense); [lexicon-key|×] very, beware, take crafty (counsel), be prudent, deal subtilly.

  • to be subtle, be shrewd, be crafty, beware, take crafty counsel, be prudent
    • (Qal) to be crafty, be subtle
    • (Hiphil) to be crafty, be or become shrewd
