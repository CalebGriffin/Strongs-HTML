H894 בָּבֶל / בבל

Bâbel

(baw-bel' | baw-BEL | ba-VEL)

Definition

proper locative noun; confusion (by mixing); from [H1101]; confusion; Babel (i.e., Babylon), including Babylonia and the Babylonian empire; Babel, Babylon.

  • Babel or Babylon, the ancient site and/or capital of Babylonia (modern Hillah) situated on the Euphrates
