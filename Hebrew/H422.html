H422 אָלָה / אלה

ʼâlâh

(aw-law' | aw-LAW | ah-LA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to adjure, i.e., (usually in a bad sense) imprecate; adjure, curse, swear.

  • to swear, curse
    • (Qal)
      • to swear, take an oath (before God)
      • to curse
    • (Hiphil)
      • to put under oath, adjure
      • to put under a curse
