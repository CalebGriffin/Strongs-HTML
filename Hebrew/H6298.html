H6298 פָּגַשׁ / פגש

pâgash

(paw-gash' | paw-ɡAHSH | pa-ɡAHSH)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to come in contact with, whether by accident or violence; figuratively, to concur; meet (with, together).

  • to meet, join, encounter
    • (Qal) to meet, encounter
    • (Niphal) to meet together, meet each other
    • (Piel) to meet, encounter
