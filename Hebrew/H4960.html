H4960 מִשְׁתֶּה / משתה

mishteh

(mish-teh' | mish-TEH | meesh-TEH)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H8354]; drink, by implication, drinking (the act); also (by implication) a banquet or (generally) feast; banquet, drank, drink, feast(-ed, -ing).

  • feast, drink, banquet
    • feast, banquet
    • drink
