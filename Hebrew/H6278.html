H6278 עֵת קָצִין / עת קצין

ʻÊth Qâtsîyn

(ayth kaw-tseen' | ate kaw-TSEEN | ate ka-TSEEN)

Definition

proper locative noun; time of the judge; from [H6256] and [H7011]; time of a judge; Eth-Katsin, a place in Palestine; Ittahkazin (by including directive enclitic).

  • one of the landmarks of the boundary of Zebulun; site unknown
