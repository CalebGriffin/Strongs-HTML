H898 בָּגַד / בגד

bâgad

(baw-gad' | baw-ɡAHD | ba-ɡAHD)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to cover (with a garment); figuratively, to act covertly; by implication, to pillage; deal deceitfully (treacherously, unfaithfully), offend, transgress(-or), (depart), treacherous (dealer, -ly, man), unfaithful(-ly) (man), [lexicon-key|×] very.

  • to act treacherously, deceitfully, deal treacherously
    • (Qal) to act or deal treacherously, faithlessly, deceitfully, offend
