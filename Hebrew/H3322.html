H3322 יָצַג / יצג

yâtsag

(yaw-tsag' | yaw-TSAHɡ | ya-TSAHɡ)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to place permanently; establish, leave, make, present, put, set, stay.

  • to place, set, establish
    • (Hiphil)
      • to set, place
      • to present
      • to exhibit
    • (Hophal) to be stopped, be stayed, be detained
