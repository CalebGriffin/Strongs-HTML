H7814 שְׂחוֹק / שחוק

sᵉchôwq

(sekh-oke' | seh-HOKE | seh-HOKE)

Definition

masculine noun; or שְׂחֹק; from [H7832]; laughter (in merriment or defiance); derision, laughter(-ed to scorn, -ing), mocked, sport.

  • laughter, laughing stock, mocking, derision
    • laughter
      • joyful
      • hollow
    • derision (of object)
    • sport
