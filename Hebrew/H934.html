H934 בֹּהֶרֶת / בהרת

bôhereth

(bo-heh'-reth | boh-heh-RET | boh-heh-RET)

Definition

feminine noun; feminine active participle of the same as [H925]; a whitish spot on the skin; bright spot.

  • white patch of skin, brightness, bright spot (on skin)
  • ([lexicon-key|CLBL]) bright spot, scar, blister, boil
    • resulting from fire
    • resulting from inflammation of the skin
    • possible sign of leprosy
