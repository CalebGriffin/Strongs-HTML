H5275 נַעַל / נעל

naʻal

(nah'-al | na-AL | na-AL)

Definition

feminine noun; or (feminine) נַעֲלָה; from [H5274]; properly, a sandal tongue; by extension a sandal or slipper (sometimes as a symbol of occupancy, a refusal to marry, or of something valueless); dryshod, (pair of) shoe(-latchet, -s).

  • sandal, shoe
