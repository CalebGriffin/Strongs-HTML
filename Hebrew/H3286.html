H3286 יָעַף / יעף

yâʻaph

(yaw-af' | yaw-AP | ya-AF)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to tire (as if from wearisome flight); faint, cause to fly, (be) weary (self).

  • to be or grow weary, be fatigued, be faint
    • (Qal) to be or grow weary, be fatigued, be faint
    • (Hophal) wearied (participle)
