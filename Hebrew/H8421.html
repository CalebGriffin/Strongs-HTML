H8421 תּוּב / תוב

tûwb

(toob | toob | toov)

Definition

verb; corresponding to [H7725], to come back; specifically (transitive and ellip.) to reply; answer, restore, return (an answer).

  • to return, come back
    • (Peal) to return, come back
    • (Aphel)
      • to restore, give back, answer
      • to return
