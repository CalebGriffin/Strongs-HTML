H7292 רָהַב / רהב

râhab

(raw-hab' | raw-HAHB | ra-HAHV)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to urge severely, i.e., (figuratively) importune, embolden, capture, act insolently; overcome, behave self proudly, make sure, strengthen.

  • to behave proudly, act stormily or boisterously or arrogantly
    • (Qal) to act insolently, storm against, beset, importune
    • (Hiphil) to disturb, alarm, awe, confuse, make bold or proud
