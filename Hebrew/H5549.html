H5549 סָלַל / סלל

çâlal

(saw-lal' | saw-LAHL | sa-LAHL)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to mound up (especially a turnpike); figurative, to exalt; reflexively, to oppose (as by a dam); cast up, exalt (self), extol, make plain, raise up.

  • to lift up, cast up, exalt
    • (Qal)
      • to cast up a highway
      • to cast up a way
      • to lift up (of song)
    • (Pilpel) to exalt, esteem highly, prize
    • (Hithpoel) to exalt oneself
