H8407 תִּגְלַת פִּלְאֶסֶר / תגלת פלאסר

Tiglath Pilʼeçer

(tig-lath' pil-eh'-ser | tiɡ-LAHT pil-eh-SER | teeɡ-LAHT peel-eh-SER)

Definition

proper masculine noun; thou will uncover the wonderful bond; or תִּגְלַת פְּלֶסֶר; or תִּלְגַּת פִּלְנְאֶסֶר or תִּלְגַּת פִּלְנֶסֶר; of foreign derivation; Tiglath-Pileser or Tilgath-pilneser, an Assyryrian king; Tiglath-pileser, Tilgath-pilneser.

  • an Assyrian king who attacked Samaria or northern kingdom of Israel in the reign of Pekah
