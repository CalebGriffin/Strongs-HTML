H7160 קָרַן / קרן

qâran

(kaw-ran' | kaw-RAHN | ka-RAHN)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to push or gore; used only as denominative from [H7161]; to shoot out horns; figuratively, rays; have horns, shine.

  • to shine
    • (Qal) to send out rays
    • (Hiphil) to display or grow horns, be horned
