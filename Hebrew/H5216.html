H5216 נִיר / ניר

nîyr

(neer | neer | neer)

Definition

masculine noun; or נִר; also נֵיר; or נֵר; or (feminine) נֵרָה; from a primitive root (see [H5214]; [H5135]) properly, meaning to glisten; a lamp (i.e., the burner) or light (literally or figuratively); candle, lamp, light.

  • lamp
