H5229 נְכֹחָה / נכחה

nᵉkôchâh

(nek-o-khaw' | neh-koh-HAW | neh-hoh-HA)

Definition

feminine adjective; feminine of [H5228]; properly, straightforwardness, i.e., (figuratively) integrity, or (concretely) a truth; equity, right (thing), uprightness.

  • straight in front, be in front of, straight, right, straightness
    • true things (of prophecy)
    • rectitude (substantive)
