H7827 שְׁחֵלֶת / שחלת

shᵉchêleth

(shekh-ay'-leth | sheh-hay-LET | sheh-hay-LET)

Definition

feminine noun; apparently from the same as [H7826] through some obscure idea, perhaps that of peeling off by concussion of sound; a scale or shell, i.e., the aromatic mussel; onycha.

  • onycha
    • an ingredient used in the holy incense
