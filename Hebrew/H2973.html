H2973 יָאַל / יאל

yâʼal

(yaw-al' | yaw-AL | ya-AL)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to be slack, i.e., (figuratively) to be foolish; dote, be (become, do) foolish(-ly).

  • to be foolish, become fools, act foolishly, show wicked folly
    • (Niphal)
      • to show wicked folly
      • to become fools
