H813 אַשְׁכְּנַז / אשכנז

ʼAshkᵉnaz

(ash-ken-az' | ash-keh-NAHDZ | ash-keh-NAHZ)

Definition

proper masculine noun; a man as sprinkled, fire as scattered; of foreign origin; Ashkenaz, a Japhethite, also his descendants; Ashkenaz.

  • a descendant of Japheth
  • a northern people, perhaps of Bithynia
