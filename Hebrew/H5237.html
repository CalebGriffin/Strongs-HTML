H5237 נׇכְרִי / נכרי

nokrîy

(nok-ree' | noke-REE | noke-REE)

Definition

adjective; from [H5235] (second form); strange, in a variety of degrees and applications (foreign, non-relative, adulterous, different, wonderful); alien, foreigner, outlandish, strange(-r) (woman).

  • foreign, alien
    • foreign
    • foreigner (substantive)
    • foreign woman, harlot
    • unknown, unfamiliar (figuratively)
