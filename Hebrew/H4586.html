H4586 מְעוּנִי / מעוני

Mᵉʻûwnîy

(meh-oo-nee' | meh-oo-NEE | meh-oo-NEE)

Definition

proper masculine noun; habitations; or מְעִינִי; probably patrial from [H4584]; a Meunite, or inhabitant of Maon (only in plural); Mehunim(-s), Meunim.
