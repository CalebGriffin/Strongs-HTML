H5486 סוּף / סוף

çûwph

(soof | soop | soof)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to snatch away, i.e., terminate; consume, have an end, perish, [lexicon-key|×] be utterly.

  • to cease, come to an end
    • (Qal) to come to an end
    • (Hiphil) to make an end
