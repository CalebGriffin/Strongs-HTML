H5571 סַנְבַלַּט / סנבלט

Çanballaṭ

(san-bal-lat' | sahn-bahl-LAHT | sahn-va-LAHT)

Definition

proper masculine noun; strength; of foreign origin; Sanballat, a Persian satrap of Samaria; Sanballat.

  • a Moabite of Horonaim and the opponent of Nehemiah and the returned exiles in their goal of rebuilding the walls of Jerusalem
