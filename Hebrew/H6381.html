H6381 פָּלָא / פלא

pâlâʼ

(paw-law' | paw-LAW | pa-LA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, perhaps to separate, i.e., distinguish (literally or figuratively); by implication, to be (causatively, make) great, difficult, wonderful; accomplish, (arise...too, be too) hard, hidden, things too high, (be, do, do a, shew) marvelous(-ly, -els) (things, work), miracles, perform, separate, make singular, (be, great, make) wonderful(-ers, -ly) (things, works), wondrous (things, works, -ly).

  • to be marvellous, be wonderful, be surpassing, be extraordinary, separate by distinguishing action
    • (Niphal)
      • to be beyond one's power, be difficult to do
      • to be difficult to understand
      • to be wonderful, be extraordinary
        • marvellous (participle)
    • (Piel) to separate (an offering)
    • (Hiphil)
      • to do extraordinary or hard or difficult thing
      • to make wonderful, do wondrously
    • (Hithpael) to show oneself wonderful or marvellous
