H8382 תָּאַם / תאם

tâʼam

(taw-am' | taw-AM | ta-AM)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be complete; but used only as denominative from [H8380]; to be (causatively, make) twinned, i.e., (figuratively) duplicate or (architectural) jointed; coupled (together), bear twins.

  • to be double, be joined
    • (Qal) to be double
    • (Hiphil) to bear twins
