H7075 קִנְיָן / קנין

qinyân

(kin-yawn' | kin-YAWN | keen-YAHN)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H7069]; creation, i.e., (concretely) creatures; also acquisition, purchase, wealth; getting, goods, [lexicon-key|×] with money, riches, substance.

  • thing acquired, acquisition, possession, purchased property, wealth
    • thing acquired (by purchase)
    • creatures (more general)
