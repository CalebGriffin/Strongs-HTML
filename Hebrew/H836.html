H836 אָשֵׁר / אשר

ʼÂshêr

(aw-share' | aw-SHARE | ah-SHARE)

Definition

proper noun; happy; from [H833], happy; Asher, a son of Jacob, and the tribe descended from him, with its territory; also a place in Palestine; Asher.

  • son of Jacob and Zilpah
  • the tribe descended from Asher
  • a city east of Shechem in Manasseh
