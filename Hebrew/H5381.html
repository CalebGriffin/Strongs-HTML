H5381 נָשַׂג / נשג

nâsag

(naw-sag' | naw-SAHɡ | na-SAHɡ)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to reach (literally or figuratively); ability, be able, attain (unto), (be able to, can) get, lay at, put, reach, remove, wax rich, [lexicon-key|×] surely, (over-)take (hold of, on, upon).

  • to reach, overtake, take hold upon
    • (Hiphil)
      • to overtake
      • to reach, attain to, cause to reach
      • to be able to secure, reach, have enough
