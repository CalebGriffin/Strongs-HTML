H830 אַשְׁפֹּת / אשפת

ʼashpôth

(ash-pohth' | ash-POTE | ash-POTE)

Definition

masculine noun; or אַשְׁפּוֹת; or שְׁפֹת; (contraction) plural of a noun of the same form as [H827], from [H8192] (in the sense of scraping); a heap of rubbish or filth; dung (hill).

  • ash heap, refuse heap, dung-hill
