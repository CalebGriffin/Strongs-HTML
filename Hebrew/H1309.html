H1309 בְּשׂוֹרָה / בשורה

bᵉsôwrâh

(bes-o-raw' | beh-soh-RAW | beh-soh-RA)

Definition

feminine noun; or (shortened) בְּשֹׂרָה; feminine from [H1319]; glad tidings; by implication, reward for good news; reward for tidings.

  • news, good news, tidings, reward for good news
    • good tidings
    • tidings, news
    • reward for good tidings
