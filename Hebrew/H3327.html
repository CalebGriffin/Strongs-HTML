H3327 יִצְחָק / יצחק

Yitschâq

(yits-khawk' | yits-HAWK | yeets-HAHK)

Definition

proper masculine noun; he laughs; from [H6711]; laughter (i.e., mochery); Jitschak (or Isaac), son of Abraham; Isaac.

  • son of Abraham by Sarah his wife and father of Jacob and Esau

Note

Compare [H3446].
