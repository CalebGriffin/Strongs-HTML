H1941 הוֹדִיָּה / הודיה

Hôwdîyâh

(ho-dee-yaw' | hoh-dih-YAW | hoh-dee-YA)

Definition

proper masculine noun; my majesty is Jehovah; a form of [H1938]; Hodijah, the name of three Israelites; Hodijah.

  • a Levite in the time of Ezra and Nehemiah
  • another Levite at the same time
  • a chief of the people at the same time
