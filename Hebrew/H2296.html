H2296 חָגַר / חגר

châgar

(khaw-gar' | haw-ɡAHR | ha-ɡAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to gird on (as a belt, armor, etc.); be able to put on, be afraid, appointed, gird, restrain, [lexicon-key|×] on every side.

  • to gird, gird on, gird oneself, put on a belt
    • (Qal)
      • to gird
      • to gird on, bind on
      • to gird oneself
