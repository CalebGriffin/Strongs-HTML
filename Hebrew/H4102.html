H4102 מָהַהּ / מהה

mâhahh

(maw-hah' | maw-HA | ma-HA)

Definition

verb; apparently a denominative from [H4100]; properly, to question or hesitate, i.e., (by implication) to be reluctant; delay, linger, stay selves, tarry.

  • (Hithpalpel) to linger, tarry, wait, delay
