H8262 שָׁקַץ / שקץ

shâqats

(shaw-kats' | shaw-KAHTS | sha-KAHTS)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be filthy, i.e., (intensively) to loathe, pollute; abhor, make abominable, have in abomination, detest, [lexicon-key|×] utterly.

  • (Piel) to detest, make abominable, count filthy, make detestable
    • to detest
    • to make detestable
