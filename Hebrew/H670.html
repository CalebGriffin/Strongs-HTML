H670 אֲפָרְסַי / אפרסי

ʼĂphârᵉçay

(af-aw-re-sah'ee | uh-pore-SAI | uh-fore-SAI)

Definition

proper masculine noun; causers of division(?); of foreign origin (only in the plural); an Apherasite or inhabitant of an unknown region of Assyria; Apharsite.

  • ([lexicon-key|CLBL]) an unknown office
  • ([lexicon-key|BDB]) secretaries
  • ([lexicon-key|TWOT]) perhaps a title of an official
