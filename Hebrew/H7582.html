H7582 שָׁאָה / שאה

shâʼâh

(shaw-aw' | shaw-AW | sha-AH)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to rush; by implication, to desolate; be desolate, (make a) rush(-ing), (lay) waste.

  • crash, make a din or crash, crash into ruins, be in ruins, be desolated
    • (Qal) to crash into ruins
    • (Niphal)
      • to be ruined
      • to crash
      • to be left (a desolation)
    • (Hiphil)
      • to cause to crash
      • to cause to be desolated
