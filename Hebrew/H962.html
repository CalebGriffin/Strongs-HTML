H962 בָּזַז / בזז

bâzaz

(baw-zaz' | baw-DZAHDZ | ba-ZAHZ)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to plunder; catch, gather, (take) for a prey, rob(-ber), spoil, take (away, spoil), [lexicon-key|×] utterly.

  • to spoil, plunder, prey upon, seize
    • (Qal) to spoil, plunder, despoil
    • (Niphal) to be spoiled, plundered
    • (Pual) to be taken as spoil
