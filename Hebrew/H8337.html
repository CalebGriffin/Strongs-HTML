H8337 שֵׁשׁ / שש

shêsh

(shaysh | shaysh | shaysh)

Definition

noun; masculine שִׁשָּׁה; a primitive number; six (as an overplus beyond five or the fingers of the hand); as ordinal, sixth; six(-teen, -teenth), sixth.

  • six
    • six (cardinal number)
    • sixth (ordinal number)
    • in combination with other numbers
