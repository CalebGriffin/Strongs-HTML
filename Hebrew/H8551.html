H8551 תָּמַךְ / תמך

tâmak

(taw-mak' | taw-MAHK | ta-MAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to sustain; by implication, to obtain, keep fast; figuratively, to help, follow close; (take) (up-)hold (up), maintain, retain, stay (up).

  • to grasp, hold, support, attain, lay hold of, hold fast
    • (Qal)
      • to grasp, lay hold of, attain
      • to hold up, support
      • to hold, keep
      • to take hold of each other
    • (Niphal) to be seized, be held
