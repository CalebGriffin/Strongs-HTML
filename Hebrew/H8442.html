H8442 תּוֹעָה / תועה

tôwʻâh

(to-aw' | toh-AW | toh-AH)

Definition

feminine noun; feminine active participle of [H8582]; mistake, i.e., (morally) impiety, or (political) injury; error, hinder.

  • error, wandering, impiety, perversions
    • error (in morals and religion)
    • confusion, disturbance
