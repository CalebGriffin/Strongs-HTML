H6636 צְבֹאִים / צבאים

Tsᵉbôʼîym

(tseb-o-eem' | tseh-boh-EEM | tseh-voh-EEM)

Definition

proper locative noun; gazelles; or (more correctly) צְבִיִּים; or צְבִיִּם; plural of [H6643]; gazelles; Tseboim or Tsebijim, a place in Palestine; Zeboiim, Zeboim.

  • one of the 5 cities in the plain which included Sodom and Gomorrah
