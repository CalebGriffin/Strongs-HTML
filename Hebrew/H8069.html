H8069 שָׁמִיר / שמיר

Shâmîyr

(shaw-meer' | shaw-MEER | sha-MEER)

Definition

proper noun; a point, thorn; the same as [H8068]; Shamir, the name of two places in Palestine; Shamir.

  • a town in the mountain district of Judah
  • a town in Ephraim where the judge Tola lived and was buried

Note

Compare [H8053].
