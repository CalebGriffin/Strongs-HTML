H798 אַשְׁדּוֹת הַפִּסְגָּה / אשדות הפסגה

ʼAshdôwth hap-Piçgâh

(ash-doth' hap-pisgaw' | ash-DOTE hahp-pis-ɡAW | ash-DOTE ha-pees-ɡA)

Definition

proper locative noun; slopes of Pisgah; from the plural of [H794] and [H6449] with the article interposed; ravines of the Pisgah; Ashdoth-Pisgah, a place east of the Jordan; Ashdoth-pisgah.

  • the mountains of Pisgah, which include Mt. Nebo
