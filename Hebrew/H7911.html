H7911 שָׁכַח / שכח

shâkach

(shaw-kakh' | shaw-KA | sha-HAHK)

Definition

verb; or שָׁכֵחַ; a primitive root; to mislay, i.e., to be oblivious of, from want of memory or attention; [lexicon-key|×] at all, (cause to) forget.

  • to forget, ignore, wither
    • (Qal)
      • to forget
      • to cease to care
    • (Niphal) to be forgotten
    • (Piel) to cause to forget
    • (Hiphil) to make or cause to forget
    • (Hithpael) to be forgotten
