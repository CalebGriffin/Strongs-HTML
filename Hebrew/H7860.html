H7860 שֹׁטֵר / שטר

shôṭêr

(sho-tare' | shoh-TARE | shoh-TARE)

Definition

masculine noun; active participle of an otherwise unused root probably meaning to write; properly, a scribe, i.e., (by analogy or implication) an official superintendent or magistrate; officer, overseer, ruler.

  • (Qal) official, officer
