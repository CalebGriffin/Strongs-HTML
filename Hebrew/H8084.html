H8084 שְׁמֹנִים / שמנים

shᵉmônîym

(shem-o-neem' | sheh-moh-NEEM | sheh-moh-NEEM)

Definition

adjective; or שְׁמוֹנִים; multiple from [H8083]; eighty, also eightieth; eighty(-ieth), fourscore.

  • eighty, fourscore
    • eighty (as cardinal number)
    • eightieth (as ordinal number)
    • in combination with other numbers
