H7097 קָצֶה / קצה

qâtseh

(kaw-tseh' | kaw-TSEH | ka-TSEH)

Definition

masculine noun; or (negative only) קֵצֶה; from [H7096]; (used in a great variety of applications and idioms; compare [H7093]); an extremity; [lexicon-key|×] after, border, brim, brink, edge, end, (in-)finite, frontier, outmost coast, quarter, shore, (out-)side, [lexicon-key|×] some, ut(-ter-)most (part).

  • end, extremity
    • end, mouth, extremity
    • border, outskirts
    • the whole (condensed term for what is included within extremities)
    • at the end of (a certain time)
