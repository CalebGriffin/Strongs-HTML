H312 אַחֵר / אחר

ʼachêr

(akh-air' | ah-HARE | ah-HARE)

Definition

adjective; from [H309]; properly, hinder; generally, next, other, etc; (an-)other man, following, next, strange.

  • another, other, following
    • following, further
    • other, different
