H8168 שֹׁעַל / שעל

shôʻal

(sho'-al | shoh-AL | shoh-AL)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning to hollow out; the palm; by extension, a handful; handful, hollow of the hand.

  • hollow hand, hollow of hand, handful
    • hollow hand, hollow of hand
    • handful
