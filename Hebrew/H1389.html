H1389 גִּבְעָה / גבעה

gibʻâh

(ghib-aw' | ɡib-AW | ɡeev-AH)

Definition

feminine noun; feminine from the same as [H1387]; a hillock; hill, little hill.

  • hill
    • hill (lower than a mountain)
    • as a place of illicit worship
    • poetic for mountain
    • used in place names
