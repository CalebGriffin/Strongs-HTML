H5291 נַעֲרָה / נערה

naʻărâh

(nah-ar-aw' | na-uh-RAW | na-uh-RA)

Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [H5288]; a girl (from infancy to adolescence); damsel, maid(-en), young (woman).

  • girl, damsel, female servant
    • girl, damsel, little girl
      • of young woman, marriageable young woman, concubine, prostitute
    • maid, female attendant, female servant
