H7112 קָצַץ / קצץ

qâtsats

(kaw-tsats' | kaw-TSAHTS | ka-TSAHTS)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to chop off (literally or figuratively); cut (asunder, in pieces, in sunder, off), [lexicon-key|×] utmost.

  • to cut off
    • (Qal) to cut off
    • (Piel) to cut or hew off, cut in two, cut in pieces
    • (Pual) to be cut or hewn off
