H974 בָּחַן / בחן

bâchan

(baw-khan' | baw-HAHN | ba-HAHN)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to test (especially metals); generally and figuratively, to investigate; examine, prove, tempt, try (trial).

  • to examine, try, prove
    • (Qal)
      • to examine, scrutinise
      • to test, prove, try (of gold, persons, the heart, man of God)
    • (Niphal) to be tried, proved
    • (Pual) to make a trial
