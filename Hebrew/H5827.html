H5827 עֶזֶר / עזר

ʻEzer

(eh'-zer | eh-DZER | eh-ZER)

Definition

proper masculine noun; treasure; from [H5826]; help; Ezer, the name of two Israelites; Ezer.

  • son of Ephraim who was slain by inhabitants of Gath
  • a priest who helped at the dedication of the walls of Jerusalem under Nehemiah

Note

Compare [H5829].
