H1740 דּוּחַ / דוח

dûwach

(doo'-akh | DOO-ah | DOO-ak)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to thrust away; figuratively, to cleanse; cast out, purge, wash.

  • to rinse, cleanse away by rinsing, cast out, purge, wash, washing
    • (Hiphil)
      • to rinse
      • to cleanse (by washing) (figuratively)
