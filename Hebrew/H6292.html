H6292 פִּגּוּל / פגול

piggûwl

(pig-gool' | pig-GOOL | pee-ɡOOL)

Definition

masculine noun; or פִּגֻּל; from an unused root meaning to stink; properly, fetid, i.e., (figuratively) unclean (ceremonially); abominable(-tion) (thing).

  • foul thing, refuse
    • unclean sacrificial flesh (only use)
