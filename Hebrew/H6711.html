H6711 צָחַק / צחק

tsâchaq

(tsaw-khak' | tsaw-HAHK | tsa-HAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to laugh outright (in merriment or scorn); by implication, to sport; laugh, mock, play, make sport.

  • to laugh, mock, play
    • (Qal) to laugh
    • (Piel)
      • to jest
      • to sport, play, make sport, toy with, make a toy of
