H6161 עֲרֻבָּה / ערבה

ʻărubbâh

(ar-oob-baw' | uh-roob-BAW | uh-roo-BA)

Definition

feminine noun; feminine passive participle of [H6048] in the sense of a bargain or exchange; something given as security, i.e., (literally) a token (of safety) or (metaphorically) a bondsman; pledge, surety.

  • pledge, token, bond, surety, thing exchanged
