H7069 קָנָה / קנה

qânâh

(kaw-naw' | kaw-NAW | ka-NA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to erect, i.e., create; by extension, to procure, especially by purchase (causatively, sell); by implication to own; attain, buy(-er), teach to keep cattle, get, provoke to jealousy, possess(-or), purchase, recover, redeem, [lexicon-key|×] surely, [lexicon-key|×] verily.

  • to get, acquire, create, buy, possess
    • (Qal)
      • to get, acquire, obtain
        • of God originating, creating, redeeming His people
          • possessor
        • of Eve acquiring
        • of acquiring knowledge, wisdom
      • to buy
    • (Niphal) to be bought
    • (Hiphil) to cause to possess
