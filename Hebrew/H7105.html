H7105 קָצִיר / קציר

qâtsîyr

(kaw-tseer' | kaw-TSEER | ka-TSEER)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H7114]; severed, a limb (of a tree, or simply foliage); bough, branch, harvest (man).

  • harvest, harvesting
    • process of harvesting
    • crop, what is harvested or reaped
    • time of harvest
  • boughs, branches
