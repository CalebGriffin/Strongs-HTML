H6912 קָבַר / קבר

qâbar

(kaw-bar' | kaw-BAHR | ka-VAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to inter; [lexicon-key|×] in any wise, bury(-ier).

  • to bury
    • (Qal) to bury
    • (Niphal) to be buried
    • (Piel) to bury, bury (in masses)
    • (Pual) to be buried
