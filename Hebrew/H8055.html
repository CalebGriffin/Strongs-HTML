H8055 שָׂמַח / שמח

sâmach

(saw-makh' | saw-MA | sa-MAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; probably to brighten up, i.e., (figuratively) be (causatively, make) blithe or gleesome; cheer up, be (make) glad, (have, make) joy(-ful), be (make) merry, (cause to, make to) rejoice, [lexicon-key|×] very.

  • to rejoice, be glad
    • (Qal)
      • to rejoice
      • to rejoice (arrogantly), exult (at)
      • to rejoice (religiously)
    • (Piel) to cause to rejoice, gladden, make glad
    • (Hiphil) to cause to rejoice, gladden, make glad
