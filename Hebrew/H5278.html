H5278 נֹעַם / נעם

nôʻam

(no'-am | noh-AM | noh-AM)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H5276]; agreeableness, i.e., delight, suitableness, splendor or grace; beauty, pleasant(-ness).

  • kindness, pleasantness, delightfulness, beauty, favour
    • delightfulness
    • symbolic name of one of two staves
    • pleasantness
