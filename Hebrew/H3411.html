H3411 יְרֵכָה / ירכה

yᵉrêkâh

(yer-ay-kaw' | yeh-ray-KAW | yeh-ray-HA)

Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [H3409]; properly, the flank; but used only figuratively, the rear or recess; border, coast, part, quarter, side.

  • flank, side, extreme parts, recesses
    • side
    • sides, recesses (dual)
