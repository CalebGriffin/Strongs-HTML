H980 בַּחֻרִים / בחרים

Bachurîym

(bakh-oo-reem' | ba-hoo-REEM | ba-hoo-REEM)

Definition

proper locative noun; young men's village; or בַּחוּרִים; masculine plural of [H970]; young men; Bachurim, a place in Palestine; Bahurim.

  • a town in Benjamin between Jerusalem and Jericho beyond the Mount of Olives on the way to Jericho
