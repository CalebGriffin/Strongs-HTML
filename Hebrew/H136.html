H136 אֲדֹנָי / אדני

ʼĂdônây

(ad-o-noy' | uh-doh-NAI | uh-doh-NAI)

Definition

masculine noun; an emphatic form of [H113]; the Lord (used as a proper name of God only); (my) Lord.

  • my lord, lord
    • of men
    • of God
  • Lord - title, spoken in place of Yahweh in Jewish display of reverence
