H6677 צַוָּאר / צואר

tsavvâʼr

(tsav-vawr' | tsa-WAWR | tsa-WAHR)

Definition

masculine noun; or צַוָּר; (Nehemiah 3:5), or צַוָּרֹן; (Song of Solomon 4:9), or (feminine) צַוָּארָה; (Micah 2:3), intensively from [H6696] in the sense of binding; the back of the neck (as that on which burdens are bound); neck.

  • neck, back of neck
    • neck, back of neck (of man)
    • neck (of animals)
