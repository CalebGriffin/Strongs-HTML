H7941 שֵׁכָר / שכר

shêkâr

(shay-kawr' | shay-KAWR | shay-HAHR)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H7937]; an intoxicant, i.e., intensely alcoholic liquor; strong drink, [lexicon-key|+] drunkard, strong wine.

  • strong drink, intoxicating drink, fermented or intoxicating liquor
