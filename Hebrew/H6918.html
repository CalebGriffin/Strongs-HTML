H6918 קָדוֹשׁ / קדוש

qâdôwsh

(kaw-doshe' | kaw-DOHSH | ka-DOHSH)

Definition

adjective; or קָדֹשׁ; from [H6942]; sacred (ceremonially or morally); (as noun) God (by eminence), an angel, a saint, a sanctuary; holy (One), saint.

  • sacred, holy, Holy One, saint, set apart
