H340 אָיַב / איב

ʼâyab

(aw-yab' | aw-YAHB | ah-YAHV)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to hate (as one of an opposite tribe or party); hence to be hostile; be an enemy.

  • to be hostile to, to be an enemy to
    • (Qal)
      • to be hostile to
      • to treat as an enemy
