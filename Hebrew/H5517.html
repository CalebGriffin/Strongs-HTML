H5517 סִיעָא / סיעא

Çîyʻâʼ

(see-ah' | see-AW | see-AH)

Definition

proper masculine noun; departing; or סִיעֲהָא; from an unused root meaning to converse; congregation; Sia or Siaha, one of the Nethinim; Sia, Siaha.

  • ancestor of a family of temple slaves who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
