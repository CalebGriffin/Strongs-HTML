H4626 מַעַר / מער

maʻar

(mah'-ar | ma-AR | ma-AR)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H6168]; a nude place, i.e., (literally) the pudenda, or (figuratively) a vacant space; nakedness, proportion.

  • bare, naked place, nakedness
    • bare place
    • nakedness, pudenda (exposed in punishment)
