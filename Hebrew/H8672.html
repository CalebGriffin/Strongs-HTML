H8672 תֵּשַׁע / תשע

têshaʻ

(tay'-shah | tay-SHA | tay-SHA)

Definition

noun; or (masculine) תִּשְׁעָה; perhaps from [H8159] through the idea of a turn to the next or full number ten; nine or (ordinal) ninth; nine, ninth, [lexicon-key|+] nine(-teen(-th)).

  • nine, nonad
    • nine (as cardinal number)
    • ninth (as ordinal number)
    • in combination with other numbers
