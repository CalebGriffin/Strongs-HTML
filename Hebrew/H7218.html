H7218 רֹאשׁ / ראש

rôʼsh

(roshe | rohsh | rohsh)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root apparently meaning to shake; the head (as most easily shaken), whether literal or figurative (in many applications, of place, time, rank, itc.); band, beginning, captain, chapiter, chief(-est place) (man, things), company, end, [lexicon-key|×] every (man), excellent, first, forefront, (be-)head, height, (on) high(-est part) (priest), [lexicon-key|×] lead, [lexicon-key|×] poor, principal, ruler, sum, top.

  • head, top, summit, upper part, chief, total, sum, height, front, beginning
    • head (of man, animals)
    • top, tip (of mountain)
    • height (of stars)
    • chief, head (of man, city, nation, place, family, priest)
    • head, front, beginning
    • chief, choicest, best
    • head, division, company, band
    • sum
