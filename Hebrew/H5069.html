H5069 נְדַב / נדב

nᵉdab

(ned-ab' | neh-DAHB | neh-DAHV)

Definition

verb; corresponding to [H5068]; be (or give) liberal(-ly); (be minded of...own) freewill (offering), offer freely (willingly).

  • to volunteer, offer freely
    • (Ithpael)
      • to volunteer
      • to give freely, offer freely
