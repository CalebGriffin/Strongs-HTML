H1177 בַּעַל חָנָן / בעל חנן

Baʻal Chânân

(bah'-al khaw-nawn' | ba-AL haw-NAWN | ba-AL ha-NAHN)

Definition

proper masculine noun; Baal is gracious; from [H1167] and [H2603]; possessor of grace; Baal-Chanan, the name of an Edomite, also of an Israelite; Baal-hanan.

  • an early king of Edom
  • one of David's officers, a Gederite, who had the superintendence of his olive and sycamore plantations
