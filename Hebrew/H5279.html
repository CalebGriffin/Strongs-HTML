H5279 נַעֲמָה / נעמה

Naʻămâh

(nah-am-aw' | na-uh-MAW | na-uh-MA)

Definition

proper noun; loveliness; feminine of [H5277]; pleasantness; Naamah, the name of a place in Palestine; Naamah.

  • daughter of Lamech by his wife Zillah and sister of Tubal-cain in the days before the flood (noun proper feminine)
  • the Ammonite wife of Solomon and mother of king Rehoboam of Judah (noun proper feminine)
  • a town in the lowlands of Judah in Philistia (noun proper feminine locative)
