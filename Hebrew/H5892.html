H5892 עִיר / עיר

ʻîyr

(eer | eer | eer)

Definition

masculine noun; or (in the plural) עָר; or עָיַר; (Judges 10:4), from [H5782]; a city (a place guarded by waking or a watch) in the widest sense (even of a mere encampment or post); Ai (from margin), city, court (from margin), town.

  • excitement, anguish
    • of terror
  • city, town (a place of waking, guarded)
    • city, town
