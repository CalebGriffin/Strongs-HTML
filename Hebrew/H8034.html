H8034 שֵׁם / שם

shêm

(shame | shame | shame)

Definition

masculine noun; a primitive word [perhaps rather from [H7760] through the idea of definite and conspicuous position; compare [H8064]]; an appellation, as a mark or memorial of individuality; by implication honor, authority, character; [lexicon-key|+] base, (in-)fame(-ous), named(-d), renown, report.

  • name
    • name
    • reputation, fame, glory
    • the Name (as designation of God)
    • memorial, monument
