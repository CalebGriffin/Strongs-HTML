H6470 פָּעַם / פעם

pâʻam

(paw-am' | paw-AM | pa-AM)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to tap, i.e., beat regularly; hence (generally) to impel or agitate; move, trouble.

  • to thrust, impel, push, beat persistently
    • (Qal) to impel
    • (Niphal) to be beaten, be disturbed
    • (Hithpael) to be disturbed
