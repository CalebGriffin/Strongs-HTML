H8271 שְׁרֵא / שרא

shᵉrêʼ

(sher-ay' | sheh-RAY | sheh-RAY)

Definition

verb; a root corresponding to that of [H8293]; to free, separate; figuratively, to unravel, commence; by implication (of unloading beasts) to reside; begin, dissolve, dwell, loose.

  • to loosen, abide, begin
    • (Peal)
      • to loosen
      • to abide (from loosing girths for camp)
    • (Pael) to begin, open
    • (Ithpael) to be loosened
