H5516 סִיסְרָא / סיסרא

Çîyçᵉrâʼ

(see-ser-aw' | see-seh-RAW | see-seh-RA)

Definition

proper masculine noun; battle array; of uncertain derivation; Sisera, the name of a Canaanitish king and of one of the Nethinim; Sisera.

  • the conquering general under king Jabin of Hazor and slain by Jael
  • ancestor of a family of temple slaves who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
