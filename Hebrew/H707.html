H707 אָרַג / ארג

ʼârag

(aw-rag' | aw-RAHɡ | ah-RAHɡ)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to plait or weave; weaver(-r).

  • to weave
    • (Qal)
      • to weave (cloth)
      • to braid (Samson's hair)
      • weaver (substantive)
      • intrigue (figuratively)
