H7563 רָשָׁע / רשע

râshâʻ

(raw-shaw' | raw-SHAW | ra-SHA)

Definition

adjective; from [H7561]; morally wrong; concretely, an (actively) bad person; [lexicon-key|+] condemned, guilty, ungodly, wicked (man), that did wrong.

  • wicked, criminal
    • guilty one, one guilty of crime (substantive)
    • wicked (hostile to God)
    • wicked, guilty of sin (against God or man)
