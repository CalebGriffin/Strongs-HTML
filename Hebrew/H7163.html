H7163 קֶרֶן הַפּוּךְ / קרן הפוך

qeren hap-pûwk

(keh'-ren hap-pook' | keh-REN hahp-POOK | keh-REN ha-POOK)

Definition

proper feminine noun; horn of antimony; from [H7161] and [H6320]; horn of cosmetic; Keren-hap-Puk, one of Job's daughters; Keren-happuch.

  • one of the daughters of Job born after his trial
