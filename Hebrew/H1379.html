H1379 גָּבַל / גבל

gâbal

(gaw-bal' | ɡaw-BAHL | ɡa-VAHL)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; also as a denominative from [H1366]; properly, to twist as a rope; to bound (as by a line); be border, set (bounds about).

  • to bound, border
    • (Qal) to bound, border
    • (Hiphil) to set bounds, set bounds for
