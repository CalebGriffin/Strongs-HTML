H4242 מְחִיר / מחיר

mᵉchîyr

(mekk-eer' | meh-HEER | meh-HEER)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning to buy; price, payment, wages; gain, hire, price, sold, worth.

  • price, hire
