H8062 שְׁמִידָעִי / שמידעי

Shᵉmîydâʻîy

(shem-ee-daw-ee' | sheh-mee-daw-EE | sheh-mee-da-EE)

Definition

proper masculine noun; wise; patronymically from [H8061]; a Shemidaite (collectively) or descendants of Shemida; Shemidaites.

  • descendants of Shemida, the son of Gilead and grandson of Manasseh
