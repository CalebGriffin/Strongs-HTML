H7564 רִשְׁעָה / רשעה

rishʻâh

(rish-aw' | rish-AW | reesh-AH)

Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [H7562]; wrong (especially moral); fault, wickedly(-ness).

  • wickedness, guilt
    • wickedness (in civil relations)
    • wickedness (of enemies)
    • wickedness (ethical and religious)
