H6935 קַדְמֹנִי / קדמני

Qadmônîy

(kad-mo-nee' | kahd-moh-NEE | kahd-moh-NEE)

Definition

adjective; easterners; the same as [H6931]; ancient, i.e., indigenous; Kadmonite (collectively), the name of a tribe in Palestine; Kadmonites.

  • a people who occupied the land of Canaan when God promised it to Abram
