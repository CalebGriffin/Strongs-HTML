H6950 קָהַל / קהל

qâhal

('kaw-hal' | kaw-HAHL | ka-HAHL)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to convoke; assemble (selves) (together), gather (selves) (together).

  • to assemble, gather
    • (Niphal) to assemble
      • for religious reasons
      • for political reasons
    • (Hiphil) to summon an assembly
      • for war, judgment
      • for religious purposes
