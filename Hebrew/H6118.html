H6118 עֵקֶב / עקב

ʻêqeb

(ay'-keb | ay-KEB | ay-KEV)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H6117] in the sense of [H6119]; a heel, i.e., (figuratively) the last of anything (used adverbially, for ever); also result, i.e., compensation; and so (adverb with preposition or relatively) on account of; [lexicon-key|×] because, by, end, for, if, reward.

  • consequence (noun masculine)
    • consequence
    • consequence, gain, reward
    • end
  • as a consequence, because, consequently (adverb)
  • as a consequence of, that, because (conjunction)
