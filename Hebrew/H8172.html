H8172 שָׁעַן / שען

shâʻan

(shaw-an' | shaw-AN | sha-AN)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to support one's self; lean, lie, rely, rest (on, self), stay.

  • to lean on, trust in, support
    • (Niphal) to lean, lean upon, support oneself
      • of trust in God (figuratively)
