H7819 שָׁחַט / שחט

shâchaṭ

(shaw-khat' | shaw-HAHT | sha-HAHT)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to slaughter (in sacrifice or massacre); kill, offer, shoot out, slay, slaughter.

  • to kill, slaughter, beat (verb)
    • (Qal)
      • to slaughter
        • beast for food
        • sacrifice
        • person in human sacrifice
        • beaten, hammered (of shekels)
    • (Niphal) to be slaughtered, be slain (of food or sacrifice)
  • ([lexicon-key|BDB]) slaughtering (noun feminine)
    • word doubtful
