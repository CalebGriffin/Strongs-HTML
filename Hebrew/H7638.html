H7638 שָׂבָךְ / שבך

sâbâk

(saw-bawk' | saw-BOKE | sa-VOKE)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning to intwine; a netting (ornament to the capital of a column); net.

  • network, lattice-work, net, netting
    • lattice
    • net-ornament (on pillars)
    • network, toils (for catching animals)
