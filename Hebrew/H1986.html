H1986 הָלַם / הלם

hâlam

(haw-lam' | haw-LAHM | ha-LAHM)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to strike down; by implication, to hammer, stamp, conquer, disband; beat (down), break (down), overcome, smite (with the hammer).

  • (Qal) to smite, strike, hammer, strike down
