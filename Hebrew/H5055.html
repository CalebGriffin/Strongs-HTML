H5055 נָגַח / נגח

nâgach

(naw-gakh' | naw-ɡA | na-ɡAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to butt with the horns; figuratively, to war against; gore, push (down, -ing).

  • to push, thrust, gore
    • (Qal) to gore
    • (Piel) to push or thrust at
    • (Hithpael) to engage in thrusting, wage war
