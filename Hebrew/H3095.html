H3095 יַהֲלֹם / יהלם

yahălôm

(yah-hal-ome' | ya-huh-LOME | ya-huh-LOME)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H1986] (in the sense of hardness); a precious stone, probably onyx; diamond.

  • a precious stone (known for its hardness)
    • perhaps the jasper, onyx, or diamond
