H4679 מְצַד / מצד

mᵉtsad

(mets-ad' | meh-TSAHD | meh-TSAHD)

Definition

feminine noun; or מְצָד; or (feminine) מְצָדָה; from [H6679]; a fastness (as a covert of ambush); castle, fort, (strong) hold, munition.

  • fort, stronghold, fastness
    • mountain-fastness
    • stronghold
