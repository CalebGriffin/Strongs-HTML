H5922 עַל / על

ʻal

(al | al | al)

Definition

preposition; corresponding to [H5921]; above, over, upon, or against (yet always in this last relation with a downward aspect) in a great variety of applications; about, against, concerning, for, (there-)fore, from, in, [lexicon-key|×] more, of, (there-, up-)on, (in-)to, [lexicon-key|+] why with.

  • upon, over, on account of, above, to, against
    • upon, over, on account of, regarding, concerning, on behalf of
    • over (with verbs of ruling)
    • above, beyond (in comparison)
    • to, against (of direction)
