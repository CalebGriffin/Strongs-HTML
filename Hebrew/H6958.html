H6958 קוֹא / קוא

qôwʼ

(ko | koh | koh)

Definition

verb; or קָיָה; (Jeremiah 25:27), a primitive root; to vomit; spue (out), vomit (out, up, up again).

  • to vomit up, spue out, disgorge
    • (Qal) to vomit
    • (Hiphil) to vomit up
      • of land spewing out its inhabitants (figuratively)
      • vomiting, disgorging
