H8626 תָּקַן / תקן

tâqan

(taw-kan' | taw-KAHN | ta-KAHN)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to equalize, i.e., straighten (intransitive or transitive); figuratively, to compose; set in order, make straight.

  • to equalise, make straight, become straight
    • (Qal) to become straight
    • (Piel) to make straight, put straight, arrange in order, set in order
