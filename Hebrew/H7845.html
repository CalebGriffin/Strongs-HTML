H7845 שַׁחַת / שחת

shachath

(shakh'-ath | sha-HAHT | sha-HAHT)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H7743]; a pit (especially as a trap); figuratively, destruction; corruption, destruction, ditch, grave, pit.

  • pit, destruction, grave
    • pit (for catching lions)
    • pit (of Hell)
