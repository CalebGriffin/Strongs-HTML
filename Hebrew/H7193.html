H7193 קַשְׂקֶשֶׂת / קשקשת

qasqeseth

(kas-keh'-seth | kahs-keh-SET | kahs-keh-SET)

Definition

feminine noun; by reduplication from an unused root meaning to shale off as bark; a scale (of a fish); hence a coat of mail (as composed of or covered with jointed plates of metal); mail, scale.

  • scale (of fish, water animals)
