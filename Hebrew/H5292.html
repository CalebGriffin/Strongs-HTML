H5292 נַעֲרָה / נערה

Naʻărâh

(nah-ar-aw' | na-uh-RAW | na-uh-RA)

Definition

proper noun; maiden; the same as [H5291]; Naarah, the name of an Israelitess; Naarah, Naarath.

  • the 2nd wife of Ashur of the tribe of Judah (noun proper feminine)
  • one of the landmarks on the southern boundary of the territory of Ephraim (noun proper locative)
