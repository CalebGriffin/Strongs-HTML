H8217 שָׁפָל / שפל

shâphâl

(shaw-fawl' | shaw-PAWL | sha-FAHL)

Definition

adjective; from [H8213]; depressed, literally or figuratively; base(-st), humble, low(-er, -ly).

  • low, humble
    • low (in height)
    • low (in station), humble (of condition or spirit)
    • humiliated
    • lowly (as subst)
