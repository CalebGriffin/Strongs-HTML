H7281 רֶגַע / רגע

regaʻ

(reh'-gah | reh-ɡA | reh-ɡA)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H7280]; a wink (of the eyes), i.e., a very short space of time; instant, moment, space, suddenly.

  • a moment (noun masculine)
  • for a moment, at one moment...at another moment (adverb)
  • in a moment (with preposition)
