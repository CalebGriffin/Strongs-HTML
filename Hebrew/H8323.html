H8323 שָׂרַר / שרר

sârar

(saw-rar' | saw-RAHR | sa-RAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to have (transitively, exercise; reflexively, get) dominion; [lexicon-key|×] altogether, make self a prince, (bear) rule.

  • to be or act as prince, rule, contend, have power, prevail over, reign, govern
    • (Qal) to rule over, govern
    • (Hithpael) to lord it over
