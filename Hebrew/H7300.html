H7300 רוּד / רוד

rûwd

(rood | rood | rood)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to tramp about, i.e., ramble (free or disconsolate); have the dominion, be lord, mourn, rule.

  • to wander restlessly, roam
    • (Qal) to roam
    • (Hiphil) to be restless, show restlessness
