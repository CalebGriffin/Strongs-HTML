H5640 סָתַם / סתם

çâtham

(saw-tham' | saw-TAHM | sa-TAHM)

Definition

verb; or שָׂתַם; (Numbers 24:15), a primitive root; to stop up; by implication, to repair; figuratively, to keep secret; closed up, hidden, secret, shut out (up), stop.

  • to stop up, shut up, keep close
    • (Qal)
      • to stop up
      • to shut up, keep close
      • secret (participle)
    • (Niphal) to be stopped up
    • (Piel) to stop up
