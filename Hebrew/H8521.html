H8521 תֵּל חַרְשָׁא / תל חרשא

Têl Charshâʼ

(tale khar-shaw' | tale hahr-SHAW | tale hahr-SHA)

Definition

proper locative noun; mound of the deaf mute; from [H8510] and the feminine of [H2798]; mound of workmanship; Tel-Charsha, a place in Babylonia; Tel-haresha, Tel-harsa.

  • a town of Babylon
    • probably located in the low country near the sea
