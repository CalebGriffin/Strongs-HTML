H1160 בְּעוֹר / בעור

Bᵉʻôwr

(beh-ore' | beh-ORE | beh-ORE)

Definition

proper masculine noun; burning; from [H1197] (in the sense of burning); a lamp; Beor, the name of the father of an Edomitish king; also of that of Balaam; Beor.

  • father of Balaam
  • father of Bela, king of Edom
