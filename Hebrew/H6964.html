H6964 קוֹלָיָה / קוליה

Qôwlâyâh

(ko-law-yaw' | koh-law-YAW | koh-la-YA)

Definition

proper masculine noun; voice of Jehovah; from [H6963] and [H3050]; voice of Jah; Kolajah, the name of two Israelites; Kolaiah.

  • father of the false prophet Ahab
  • a Benjamite whose descendants settled in Jerusalem after the return from captivity
  • a Levite in the time of Ezra
