H8231 שָׁפַר / שפר

shâphar

(shaw-far' | shaw-PAHR | sha-FAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to glisten, i.e., (figuratively) be (causatively, make) fair; [lexicon-key|×] goodly.

  • to be pleasing, be beautiful, be fair, be comely, be bright, glisten
    • (Qal) to be beautiful
