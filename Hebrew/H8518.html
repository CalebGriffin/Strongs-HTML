H8518 תָּלָה / תלה

tâlâh

(taw-law' | taw-LAW | ta-LA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to suspend (especially to gibbet); hang (up).

  • to hang
    • (Qal)
      • to hang
        • to hang up (any object)
        • to put to death by hanging
    • (Niphal) to be hanged
    • (Piel) to hang, hang up (for display)
