H1171 בַּעַל גָּד / בעל גד

Baʻal Gâd

(bah'-al gawd | ba-AL ɡawd | ba-AL ɡahd)

Definition

proper locative noun; lord of fortune; from [H1168] and [H1409]; Baal of Fortune; Baal-Gad, a place in Syria; Baal-gad.

  • a city noted for Baal-worship, located at the most northern or northwestern point to which Joshua's victories extended
