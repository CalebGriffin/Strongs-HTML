H7884 שִׁיחוֹר לִבְנָת / שיחור לבנת

Shîychôwr Libnâth

(shee-khore' lib-nawth' | shee-HORE lib-NAWT | shee-HORE leev-NAHT)

Definition

proper masculine noun; blackness of whiteness; from the same as [H7883] and [H3835]; darkish whiteness; Shichor-Libnath, a stream of Palestine; Shihor-libnath.

  • a stream in Asher
