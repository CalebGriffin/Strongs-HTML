H7990 שַׁלִּיט / שליט

shallîyṭ

(shal-leet' | shahl-LEET | sha-LEET)

Definition

adjective; corresponding to [H7989]; mighty; abstractly, permission; concretely, a premier; captain, be lawful, rule(-r).

  • having mastery, having authority, ruling
    • having or exercising mastery, ruling
    • ruler (substantive)
    • having authority
      • it is authorised
