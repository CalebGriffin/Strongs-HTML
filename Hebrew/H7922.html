H7922 שֶׂכֶל / שכל

sekel

(seh'-kel | seh-KEL | seh-HEL)

Definition

masculine noun; or שֵׂכֶל; from [H7919]; intelligence; by implication, success; discretion, knowledge, policy, prudence, sense, understanding, wisdom, wise.

  • prudence, insight, understanding
    • prudence, good sense
    • insight, understanding
    • cunning, craft (bad sense)
