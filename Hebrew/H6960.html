H6960 קָוָה / קוה

qâvâh

(kaw-vaw' | kaw-WAW | ka-VA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to bind together (perhaps by twisting), i.e., collect; (figuratively) to expect; gather (together), look, patiently, tarry, wait (for, on, upon).

  • to wait, look for, hope, expect
    • (Qal) waiting (participle)
    • (Piel)
      • to wait or look eagerly for
      • to lie in wait for
      • to wait for, linger for
  • to collect, bind together
    • (Niphal) to be collected
