H5899 עִיר הַתְּמָרִים / עיר התמרים

ʻÎyr hat-Tᵉmârîym

(eer hat-tem-aw-reem' | eer haht-teh-maw-REEM | eer ha-teh-ma-REEM)

Definition

proper locative noun; the city of palmtrees; from [H5892] and the plural of [H8558] with the article interpolated; city of the palmtrees; Ir-hat-Temarim, a place in Palestine; the city of palmtrees.

  • another name for 'Jericho'
