H5318 נֶפְתּוֹחַ / נפתוח

Nephtôwach

(nef-to'-akh | nep-TOH-ah | nef-TOH-ak)

Definition

proper locative noun; opening; from [H6605]; opened, i.e., a spring; Nephtoach, a place in Palestine; Neptoah.

  • a spring or source of water on the boundary of the territories of Judah and Benjamin; located northwest of Jerusalem
