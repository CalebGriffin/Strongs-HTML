H784 אֵשׁ / אש

ʼêsh

(aysh | aysh | aysh)

Definition

feminine noun; a primitive word; fire (literally or figuratively); burning, fiery, fire, flaming, hot.

  • fire
    • fire, flames
    • supernatural fire (accompanying theophany)
    • fire (for cooking, roasting, parching)
    • altar-fire
    • God's anger (figuratively)
