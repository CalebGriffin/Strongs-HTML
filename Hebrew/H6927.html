H6927 קַדְמָה / קדמה

qadmâh

(kad-maw' | kahd-MAW | kahd-MA)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H6923]; priority (in time); also used adverbially (before); afore, antiquity, former (old) estate.

  • antiquity, former state or estate or situation, before, origin
    • antiquity, beginning, origin
    • former state or situation
    • before
