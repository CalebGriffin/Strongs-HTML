H2008 הֵנָּה / הנה

hênnâh

(hane'-naw | hane-NAW | hay-NA)

Definition

adverb; from [H2004]; hither or thither (but used both of place and time); here, hither(-to), now, on this (that) side, [lexicon-key|+] since, this (that) way, thitherward, [lexicon-key|+] thus far, to...fro, [lexicon-key|+] yet.

  • here, there, now, hither
