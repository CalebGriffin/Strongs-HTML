H1293 בְּרָכָה / ברכה

Bᵉrâkâh

(ber-aw-kaw' | beh-raw-KAW | beh-ra-HA)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H1288]; benediction; by implication prosperity; blessing, liberal, pool, present.

  • blessing
  • (source of) blessing
  • blessing, prosperity
  • blessing, praise of God
  • a gift, present
  • treaty of peace
