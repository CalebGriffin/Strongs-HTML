H7080 קָסַם / קסם

qâçam

(kaw-sam' | kaw-SAHM | ka-SAHM)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to distribute, i.e., determine by lot or magical scroll; by implication, to divine; divine(-r, -ation), prudent, soothsayer, use (divination).

  • (Qal) to practice divination, divine
    • of diviners of the nations, Balaam
    • of false prophets of Israel
    • prohibited
