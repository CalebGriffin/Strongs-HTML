H6398 פָּלַח / פלח

pâlach

(paw-lakh' | paw-LA | pa-LAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to slice, i.e., break open or pierce; bring forth, cleave, cut, shred, strike through.

  • to cleave, slice
    • (Qal) to cleave, plow
    • (Piel)
      • to cleave open or through
      • to cause to cleave open
      • to slice, pierce
