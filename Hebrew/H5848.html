H5848 עָטַף / עטף

ʻâṭaph

(aw-taf' | aw-TAHP | ah-TAHF)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to shroud, i.e., clothe (whether transitive or reflexive); hence (from the idea of darkness) to languish; cover (over), fail, faint, feebler, hide self, be overwhelmed, swoon.

  • to turn aside
    • (Qal) to turn aside, turn (in order to cover)
  • to envelop oneself
    • (Qal) to envelop oneself, cover
  • to be feeble, be faint, grow weak
    • (Qal) to be feeble, be faint
    • (Niphal) to faint
    • (Hiphil) to show feebleness
    • (Hithpael) to faint, faint away
