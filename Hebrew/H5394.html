H5394 נָשַׁל / נשל

nâshal

(naw-shal' | naw-SHAHL | na-SHAHL)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to pluck off, i.e., divest, eject or drop; cast (out), drive, loose, put off (out), slip.

  • to slip off, drop off, clear away, draw off
    • (Qal)
      • to slip or drop off
      • to draw off
      • to clear away
    • (Piel) to clear out completely, clear entirely
