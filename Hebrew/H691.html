H691 אֶרְאֵל / אראל

ʼerʼêl

(er-ale' | er-ALE | er-ALE)

Definition

masculine noun; probably for [H739]; a hero (collectively); valiant one.

  • ([lexicon-key|CLBL]) hero, valiant one
  • ([lexicon-key|BDB]) form and meaning dubious
  • ([lexicon-key|TWOT]) Ariel or hearth

Note

The form and meaning is considered questionable by some.
