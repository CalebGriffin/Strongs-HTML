H801 אִשָּׁה / אשה

ʼishshâh

(ish-shaw' | ish-SHAW | ee-SHA)

Definition

masculine noun; the same as [H800], but used in a liturgical sense; properly, a burnt-offering; but occasionally of any sacrifice; (offering, sacrifice), (made) by fire.

  • burnt-offering, offering made by fire, fire offering
