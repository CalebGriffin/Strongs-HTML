H6285 פֵּאָה / פאה

pêʼâh

(pay-aw' | pay-AW | pay-AH)

Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [H6311]; properly, mouth in a figurative sense, i.e., direction, region, extremity; corner, end, quarter, side.

  • corner, edge, side, quarter, extremity
    • corner
    • side
