H3524 כַּבִּיר / כביר

kabbîyr

(kab-beer' | kahb-BEER | ka-BEER)

Definition

adjective; from [H3527]; vast, whether in extent (figuratively, of power, mighty; of time, aged), or in number, many; feeble, mighty, most, much, strong, valiant.

  • mighty, great, powerful, many, much
