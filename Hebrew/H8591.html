H8591 תָּעַע / תעע

tâʻaʻ

(taw-ah' | taw-AH | ta-AH)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to cheat; by analogy, to maltreat; deceive, misuse.

  • to deceive, misuse
    • (Pilpel)
      • to be a mocker, mock
      • mocker (substantive)
    • (Hithpalpel)
      • to ridicule
      • mocking (participle)
