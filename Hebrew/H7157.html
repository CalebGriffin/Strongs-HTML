H7157 קִרְיַת יְעָרִים / קרית יערים

Qiryath Yᵉʻârîym

(keer-yath' yeh-aw-reem' | kir-YAHT yeh-aw-REEM | keer-YAHT yeh-ah-REEM)

Definition

proper locative noun; city of forests; or (Jeremiah 26:20) with the article interposed; or (Joshua 18:28) simply the former part of the word; or קִרְיַת עָרִים; from [H7151] and the plural of [H3293] or [H5892]; city of forests, or city of towns; Kirjath-Jearim or Kirjath-Arim, a place in Palestine; Kirjath, Kirjath-jearim, Kirjath-arim.

  • a city on the northern boundary of Judah and on the western and southern boundaries of Benjamin
    • also 'Kirjath-baal' and 'Baalah'
