H1602 גָּעַל / געל

gâʻal

(gaw-al' | ɡaw-AL | ɡa-AL)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to detest; by implication, to reject; abhor, fail, lothe, vilely cast away.

  • to abhor, loathe, be vilely cast away, fall
    • (Qal) to abhor, loathe
    • (Niphal) to be defiled
    • (Hiphil) to reject as loathsome, show aversion
