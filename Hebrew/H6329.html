H6329 פּוּק / פוק

pûwq

(pook | pook | pook)

Definition

verb; a primitive root [rather identical with [H6328] through the idea of dropping out; compare [H5312]]; to issue, i.e., furnish; causatively, to secure; figuratively, to succeed; afford, draw out, further, get, obtain.

  • to bring out, furnish, promote, go out, issue
    • (Hiphil)
      • to produce, furnish
      • to bring out, elicit, obtain, cause to come out from
      • to promote
      • to cause to go out to
