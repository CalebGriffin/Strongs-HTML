H5276 נָעֵם / נעם

nâʻêm

(naw-ame' | naw-AME | na-AME)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be agreeable (literally or figuratively); pass in beauty, be delight, be pleasant, be sweet.

  • (Qal) to be pleasant, be beautiful, be sweet, be delightful, be lovely
