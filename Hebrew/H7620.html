H7620 שָׁבוּעַ / שבוע

shâbûwaʻ

(shaw-boo'-ah | shaw-BOO-ah | sha-VOO-ah)

Definition

masculine noun; or שָׁבֻעַ; also (feminine) שְׁבֻעָה; properly, passive participle of [H7650] as a denominative of [H7651]; literally, sevened, i.e., a week (specifically, of years); seven, week.

  • seven, period of seven (days or years), heptad, week
    • period of seven days, a week
      • Feast of Weeks
    • heptad, seven (of years)
