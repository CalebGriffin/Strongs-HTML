H7186 קָשֶׁה / קשה

qâsheh

(kaw-sheh' | kaw-SHEH | ka-SHEH)

Definition

adjective; from [H7185]; severe (in various applications); churlish, cruel, grievous, hard(-hearted) (thing), heavy, [lexicon-key|+] impudent, obstinate, prevailed, rough(-ly), sore, sorrowful, stiff(necked), stubborn, [lexicon-key|+] in trouble.

  • hard, cruel, severe, obstinate
    • hard, difficult
    • severe
    • fierce, intense, vehement
    • stubborn, stiff of neck, stiff-necked
    • rigorous (of battle)
