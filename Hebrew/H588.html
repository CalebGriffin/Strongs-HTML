H588 אֲנָחֲרָת / אנחרת

ʼĂnâchărâth

(an-aw-kha-rawth' | uh-naw-huh-RAWT | uh-na-huh-RAHT)

Definition

proper locative noun; the groaning of fear; probably from the same root as [H5170]; a gorge or narrow pass; Anacharath, a place in Palestine; Anaharath.

  • ([lexicon-key|BDB]) city in Naphtali
  • ([lexicon-key|CLBL]) city in Issachar
