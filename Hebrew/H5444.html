H5444 סִבְּכַי / סבכי

Çibbᵉkay

(sib-bek-ah'-ee | sib-beh-KAI | see-beh-HAI)

Definition

proper masculine noun; weaver; from [H5440]; copse-like; Sibbecai, an Israelite; Sibbecai, Sibbechai.

  • one of David's guard and 8th captain for the 8th month of 24,000 men of the king's army; a Judaite, descendant of Zerah and called the 'Hushathite'
