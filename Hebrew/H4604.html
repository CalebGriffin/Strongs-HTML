H4604 מַעַל / מעל

maʻal

(mah'-al | ma-AL | ma-AL)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H4603]; treachery, i.e., sin; falsehood, grievously, sore, transgression, trespass, [lexicon-key|×] very.

  • unfaithful or treacherous act, trespass
    • against man
    • against God
