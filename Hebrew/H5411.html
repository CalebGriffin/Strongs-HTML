H5411 נָתִין / נתין

Nâthîyn

(naw-theen' | naw-TEEN | na-TEEN)

Definition

masculine noun; or נָתוּן (Ezra 8:17), (the proper form as passive participle), from [H5414]; one given, i.e., (in the plural only); the Nethinim, or Temple-servants (as given to that duty); Nethinims.

  • Nethinims
    • temple slaves assigned to the Levites and priests for service in the sanctuary
