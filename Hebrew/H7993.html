H7993 שָׁלַךְ / שלך

shâlak

(shaw-lak | shaw-LAHK | sha-LAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to throw out, down or away (literally or figuratively); adventure, cast (away, down, forth, off, out), hurl, pluck, throw.

  • to throw, cast, hurl, fling
    • (Hiphil)
      • to throw, cast, throw away, cast off, shed, cast down
      • to cast (lots) (figuratively)
    • (Hophal)
      • to be thrown, be cast
      • to be cast forth or out
      • to be cast down
      • to be cast (metaphorically)
