H5592 סַף / סף

çaph

(saf | sahp | sahf)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H5605], in its original sense of containing; a vestibule (as a limit); also a dish (for holding blood or wine); bason, bowl, cup, door (post), gate, post, threshold.

  • a spreading out, basin, goblet, bowl
    • basin, goblet
  • threshold, sill
    • threshold, sill, door-keeper
