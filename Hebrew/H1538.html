H1538 גֻּלְגֹּלֶת / גלגלת

gulgôleth

(gul-go'-leth | ɡool-ɡoh-LET | ɡool-ɡoh-LET)

Definition

feminine noun; by reduplication from [H1556]; a skull (as round); by implication, a head (in enumeration of persons); head, every man, poll, skull.

  • head, poll, skull
    • skull
    • head, poll (of census)
