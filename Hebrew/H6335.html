H6335 פּוּשׁ / פוש

pûwsh

(poosh | poosh | poosh)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to spread; figuratively, act proudly; grow up, be grown fat, spread selves, be scattered.

  • to spring about
    • (Qal) to frisk, act proudly (figuratively)
  • (Niphal) to be scattered, be spread
