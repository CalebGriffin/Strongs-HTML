H6424 פָּלַס / פלס

pâlaç

(paw-las' | paw-LAHS | pa-LAHS)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to roll flat, i.e., prepare (a road); also to revolve, i.e., weigh (mentally); make, ponder, weigh.

  • (Piel) to weigh, make level, balance
    • to weigh out
    • to make level or smooth
