H2111 זוּעַ / זוע

zûwaʻ

(zoo'-ah | DZOO-ah | ZOO-ah)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to shake off, i.e., (figuratively) to agitate (as with fear); move, tremble, vex.

  • to tremble, quiver, quake, be in terror
    • (Qal) to tremble, quake
    • (Pilpel)
      • to shake violently
      • to cause to tremble
