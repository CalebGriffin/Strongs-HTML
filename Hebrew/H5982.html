H5982 עַמּוּד / עמוד

ʻammûwd

(am-mood' | am-MOOD | ah-MOOD)

Definition

masculine noun; or עַמֻּד; from [H5975]; a column (as standing); also a stand, i.e., platform; [lexicon-key|×] apiece, pillar.

  • pillar, column
    • pillar
    • column, upright
    • column (of smoke)
