H5513 סִינִי / סיני

Çîynîy

(see-nee' | see-NEE | see-NEE)

Definition

adjective; thorn, clay; from an otherwise unknown name of a man; a Sinite, or descendant of one of the sons of Canaan; Sinite.

  • a tribe of the Canaanites descended from Canaan inhabiting the northern part of the Lebanon district
