H5445 סָבַל / סבל

çâbal

(saw-bal' | saw-BAHL | sa-VAHL)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to carry (literally or figuratively), or (reflexively) be burdensome; specifically, to be gravid; bear, be a burden, carry, strong to labour.

  • to bear, bear a load, drag oneself along
    • (Qal) to bear (a load)
    • (Pual) laden (participle)
    • (Hithpael) to make oneself a burden, drag oneself along
