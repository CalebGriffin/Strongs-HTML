H2756 חָרִיף / חריף

Chârîyph

(khaw-reef' | haw-REEP | ha-REEF)

Definition

proper masculine noun; a plucking off; from [H2778]; autumnal; Chariph, the name of two Israelites; Hariph.

  • head of a family of exiles returning with Zerubbabel
  • a head of the people who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
