H8385 תַּאֲנָה / תאנה

taʼănâh

(tah-an-aw' | ta-uh-NAW | ta-uh-NA)

Definition

feminine noun; or תֹּאֲנָה; from [H579]; an opportunity or (subjectively) purpose; occasion.

  • occasion, time of heat or oestrous or copulation, sexual drive (of animal)
  • occasion, opportunity (for a quarrel)
