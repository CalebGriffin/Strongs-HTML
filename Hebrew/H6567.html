H6567 פָּרָשׁ / פרש

pârâsh

(paw-rash' | paw-ROHSH | pa-ROHSH)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to separate, literally (to disperse) or figuratively (to specify); also (by implication) to wound; scatter, declare, distinctly, shew, sting.

  • to make distinct, declare, distinguish, separate
    • (Qal) to declare, clarify
    • (Pual) to be distinctly declared
  • (Hiphil) to pierce, sting
  • (Niphal) scatter
