H7707 שְׁדֵיאוּר / שדיאור

Shᵉdêyʼûwr

(shed-ay-oor' | sheh-day-OOR | sheh-day-OOR)

Definition

proper masculine noun; darter of light; from the same as [H7704] and [H217]; spreader of light; Shedejur, an Israelite; Shedeur.

  • father of Elizur, chief of the tribe of Reuben at the time of the exodus
