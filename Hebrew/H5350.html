H5350 נִקֻּד / נקד

niqqud

(nik-kood' | nik-KOOD | nee-KOOD)

Definition

masculine noun; from the same as [H5348]; a crumb (as broken to spots); also a biscuit (as pricked); cracknel, mouldy.

  • crumbled thing, thing easily crumbled, crumbs
    • crumbs
    • apparently a kind of hard biscuit or cake
