H8040 שְׂמֹאול / שמאול

sᵉmôʼwl

(sem-ole' | seh-MOH-L | seh-MOVE-L)

Definition

masculine noun; or שְׂמֹאל; a primitive word (rather perhaps from the same as [H8071] (by insertion of the aleph) through the idea of wrapping up); properly, dark (as enveloped), i.e., the north; hence (by orientation), the left hand; left (hand, side).

  • the left, the left hand, the left side
    • left
    • left hand
    • north (as one faces east)
