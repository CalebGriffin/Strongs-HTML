H6113 עָצָר / עצר

ʻâtsâr

(aw-tsar' | aw-TSAWR | ah-TSAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to inclose; by analogy, to hold back; also to maintain, rule, assemble; [lexicon-key|×] be able, close up, detain, fast, keep (self close, still), prevail, recover, refrain, [lexicon-key|×] reign, restrain, retain, shut (up), slack, stay, stop, withhold (self).

  • to restrain, retain, close up, shut, withhold, refrain, stay, detain
    • (Qal)
      • to restrain, halt, stop
      • to retain
    • (Niphal) to be restrained, be stayed, be under restraint
