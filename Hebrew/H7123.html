H7123 קְרָא / קרא

qᵉrâʼ

(ker-aw' | keh-RAW | keh-RA)

Definition

verb; corresponding to [H7121]; to call out to (i.e., properly, address by name, but used in a wide variety of applications); call, cry, read.

  • to call, read aloud, read out, shout
    • (Peal)
      • to call, proclaim
      • to read aloud, read out
    • (Ithpeel) to be summoned, be called
