H3366 יְקָר / יקר

yᵉqâr

(yek-awr' | yeh-KAWR | yeh-KAHR)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H3365]; value, i.e., (concretely) wealth; abstractly, costliness, dignity; honour, precious (things), price.

  • price, value, preciousness, honour, splendour, pomp
    • preciousness
    • price
    • honour, esteem
