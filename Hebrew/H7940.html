H7940 שָׂכָר / שכר

Sâkâr

(saw-kar' | saw-KAWR | sa-HAHR)

Definition

proper masculine noun; wages; the same as [H7939]; recompense; Sakar, the name of two Israelites; Sacar.

  • father of Ahiam, one of David's mighty warriors
  • a Korahite Levite gatekeeper, the 4th son of Obed-edom in the time of David
