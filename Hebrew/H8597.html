H8597 תִּפְאָרָה / תפארה

tiphʼârâh

(tif-aw-raw' | tip-aw-RAW | teef-ah-RA)

Definition

feminine noun; or תִּפְאֶרֶת; from [H6286]; ornament (abstractly or concretely, literally or figuratively); beauty(-iful), bravery, comely, fair, glory(-ious), honour, majesty.

  • beauty, splendour, glory
    • beauty, finery (of garments, jewels)
    • glory
      • of rank, renown
      • as attribute of God
    • honour (or nation Israel)
    • glorying, boasting (of individual)
