H5050 נָגַהּ / נגה

nâgahh

(naw-gah' | naw-ɡA | na-ɡA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to glitter; causatively, to illuminate; (en-)lighten, (cause to) shine.

  • to shine
    • (Qal) to shine
    • (Hiphil)
      • to enlighten
      • to cause to shine
