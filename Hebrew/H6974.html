H6974 קוּץ / קוץ

qûwts

(koots | koots | koots)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (identical with through the idea of abruptness in starting up from sleep (compare [H3364])); to awake (literally or figuratively); arise, (be) (a-)wake, watch.

  • to awake, wake up
    • (Hiphil) to awaken, arouse, show signs of waking, awake
