H5526 סָכַךְ / סכך

çâkak

(saw-kak' | saw-KAHK | sa-HAHK)

Definition

verb; or שָׂכַךְ; (Exodus 33:22), a primitive root; properly, to entwine as a screen; by implication, to fence in, cover over, (figuratively) protect; cover, defence, defend, hedge in, join together, set, shut up.

  • (Qal) to hedge, fence about, shut in
  • to block, overshadow, screen, stop the approach, shut off, cover
    • (Qal)
      • to screen, cover
      • to cover oneself
      • protector (participle)
    • (Hiphil)
      • to screen, cover
      • to cover, defecate (euphemism)
  • (Qal) to cover, lay over
  • to weave together
    • (Qal) to weave together
    • (Pilpel) to weave, weave together
