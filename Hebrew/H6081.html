H6081 עֵפֶר / עפר

ʻÊpher

(ay'-fer | ay-PER | ay-FER)

Definition

proper masculine noun; a calf; probably a variation of [H6082]; gazelle; Epher, the name of an Arabian and of two Israelites; Epher.

  • the 2nd son of Midian
  • a son of Ezra, among the descendants of Judah
  • one of the heads of the families of Manasseh on the east of the Jordan
