H5314 נָפַשׁ / נפש

nâphash

(naw-fash' | naw-PAHSH | na-FAHSH)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to breathe; passively, to be breathed upon , i.e., (figuratively) refreshed (as if by a current of air); (be) refresh(-ed) selves.

  • (Niphal) to take breath, refresh oneself
