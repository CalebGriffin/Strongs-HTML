H5210 נִינְוֵה / נינוה

Nîynᵉvêh

(nee-nev-ay' | nee-neh-WAY | nee-neh-VAY)

Definition

proper locative noun; abode of Ninus; of foreign origin; Nineveh, the capital of Assyria; Nineveh.

  • capital of the ancient kingdom of Assyria; located on the east bank of the Tigris river, 550 miles (880 kilometers) from its mouth and 250 miles (400 kilometers) north of Babylon
