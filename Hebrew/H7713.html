H7713 שְׂדֵרָה / שדרה

sᵉdêrâh

(sed-ay-raw' | seh-day-RAW | seh-day-RA)

Definition

feminine noun; from an unused root meaning to regulate; a row, i.e., rank (of soldiers), story (of rooms); board, range.

  • row, rank (of soldiers)
    • rows, ranks
    • technical term of building
      • meaning unknown
