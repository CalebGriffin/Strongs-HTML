H7843 שָׁחַת / שחת

shâchath

(shaw-khath' | shaw-HAHT | sha-HAHT)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to decay, i.e., (causatively) ruin (literally or figuratively); batter, cast off, corrupt(-er) (thing), destroy(-er, -uction), lose, mar, perish, spill, spoiler, [lexicon-key|×] utterly, waste(-r).

  • to destroy, corrupt, go to ruin, decay
    • (Niphal) to be marred, be spoiled, be corrupted, be corrupt, be injured, be ruined, be rotted
    • (Piel)
      • to spoil, ruin
      • to pervert, corrupt, deal corruptly (morally)
    • (Hiphil)
      • to spoil, ruin, destroy
      • to pervert, corrupt (morally)
      • destroyer (participle)
    • (Hophal) spoiled, ruined (participle)
