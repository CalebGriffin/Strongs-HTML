H7652 שֶׁבַע / שבע

shebaʻ

(sheh'-bah | sheh-BA | sheh-VA)

Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; seven; the same as [H7651]; seven; Sheba, the name of a place in Palestine, and of two Israelites; Sheba.

  • Benjamite, son of Bichri and one who led a rebellion against David (noun proper masculine)
  • Gadite, brother of Michael, Meshullam, Jorai, Jachan, Zia, and Heber (noun proper masculine)
  • a town in Simeon (noun proper locative)
