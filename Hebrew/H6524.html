H6524 פָּרַח / פרח

pârach

(paw-rakh' | paw-RA | pa-RAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to break forth as a bud, i.e., bloom; generally, to spread; specifically, to fly (as extending the wings); figuratively, to flourish; [lexicon-key|×] abroad, [lexicon-key|×] abundantly, blossom, break forth (out), bud, flourish, make fly, grow, spread, spring (up).

  • to bud, sprout, shoot, bloom
    • (Qal) to bud, sprout, send out shoots, blossom
    • (Hiphil)
      • to cause to bud or sprout
      • to show buds or sprouts
  • (Qal) to break out (of leprosy)
  • (Qal) to fly
