H7852 שָׂטַם / שטם

sâṭam

(saw-tam' | saw-TAHM | sa-TAHM)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to lurk for, i.e., persecute; hate, oppose self against.

  • to hate, oppose oneself to, bear a grudge, retain animosity against, cherish animosity against
    • (Qal) to cherish animosity against
