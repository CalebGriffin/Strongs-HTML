H7049 קָלַע / קלע

qâlaʻ

(kaw-lah' | kaw-LA | ka-LA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to sling; also to carve (as if a circular motion, or into light forms); carve, sling (out).

  • to sling, hurl forth
    • (Qal)
      • to sling
      • slinger (participle)
    • (Piel) to sling
  • (Qal) to carve
