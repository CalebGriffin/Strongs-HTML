H5284 נַעֲמָתִי / נעמתי

Naʻămâthîy

(nah-am-aw-thee' | na-uh-maw-TEE | na-uh-ma-TEE)

Definition

adjective; pleasantness; patrial from a place corresponding in name (but not identical) with [H5279]; a Naamathite, or inhabitant of Naamah; Naamathite.

  • an inhabitant of Naamah (site unknown); describes Zophar the friend of Job
