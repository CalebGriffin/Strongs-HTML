H2263 חָבַק / חבק

châbaq

(khaw-bak' | haw-BAHK | ha-VAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to clasp (the hands or in embrace); embrace, fold.

  • to embrace, clasp
    • (Qal)
      • to embrace
      • to fold one's hands in idleness (figuratively)
    • (Piel) to embrace
