H7951 שָׁלָה / שלה

shâlâh

(shaw-law' | shaw-LAW | sha-LA)

Definition

verb; or שָׁלַו; (Job 3:26), a primitive root; to be tranquil, i.e., secure or successful; be happy, prosper, be in safety.

  • to be at rest, prosper, be quiet, be at ease
    • (Qal)
      • to be or have quiet
      • to be at ease, prosper
