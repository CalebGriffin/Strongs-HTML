H7508 רְפִידִים / רפידים

Rᵉphîydîym

(ref-ee-deem' | reh-pee-DEEM | reh-fee-DEEM)

Definition

proper masculine noun; rests, stays, resting places; plural of the masculine of the same as [H7507]; ballusters; Rephidim, a place in the Desert; Rephidim.

  • a station of Israel in the wilderness between Egypt and Sinai
