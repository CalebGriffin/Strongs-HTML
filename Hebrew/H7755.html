H7755 שׂוֹכֹה / שוכה

Sôwkôh

(so-ko' | soh-KOH | soh-HOH)

Definition

proper locative noun; bushy; or שֹׂכֹה; or שׂוֹכוֹ; from [H7753]; Sokoh or Soko, the name of two places in Palestine; Shocho, Shochoh, Sochoh, Soco, Socoh.

  • a town in the lowlands of Judah
  • a town in the mountain district of Judah
