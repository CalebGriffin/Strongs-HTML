H5236 נֵכָר / נכר

nêkâr

(nay-kawr' | nay-KAWR | nay-HAHR)

Definition

masculine noun; ' from [H5234]; foreign, or (concretely) a foreigner, or (abstractly) heathendom; alien, strange, [lexicon-key|+] stranger.

  • foreign, alien, foreignness, that which is foreign
    • foreignness, foreign gods
    • alien, foreigner
    • foreign (vanities)
