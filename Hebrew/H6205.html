H6205 עֲרָפֶל / ערפל

ʻărâphel

(ar-aw-fel' | uh-raw-PEL | uh-ra-FEL)

Definition

masculine noun; probably from [H6201]; gloom (as of a lowering sky); (gross, thick) dark (cloud, -ness).

  • cloud, heavy or dark cloud, darkness, gross darkness, thick darkness
