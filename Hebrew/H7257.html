H7257 רָבַץ / רבץ

râbats

(raw-bats' | raw-BAHTS | ra-VAHTS)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to crouch (on all four legs folded, like a recumbent animal); by implication, to recline, repose, brood, lurk, imbed; crouch (down), fall down, make a fold, lay, (cause to, make to) lie (down), make to rest, sit.

  • to stretch oneself out, lie down, lie stretched out
    • (Qal) to lie down, lie
    • (Hiphil) to cause to lie down
      • laying (stones)
