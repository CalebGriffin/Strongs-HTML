H6007 עֲמַסְיָה / עמסיה

ʻĂmaçyâh

(am-as-yaw' | uh-mahs-YAW | uh-mahs-YA)

Definition

proper masculine noun; Jehovah is strength; from [H6006] and [H3050]; Jah has loaded; Amasjah, an Israelite; Amasiah.

  • son of Zichri and captain of 200,000 warriors of Judah in the reign of king Jehoshaphat of Judah
