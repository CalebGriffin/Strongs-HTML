H518 אִם / אם

ʼim

(eem | im | eem)

Definition

particle of speech; a primitive particle; used very widely as demonstrative, lo!; interrogative, whether?; or conditional, if, although; also Oh that!, when; hence, as a negative, not; (and, doubtless, if, that) (can-)(not), [lexicon-key|+] but, either, [lexicon-key|+] except, [lexicon-key|+] more(-over if) (than), neither, nevertheless, nor, oh that, or, [lexicon-key|+] save (only, -ing), seeing, since, sith, [lexicon-key|+] surely (no more, none, not), though, [lexicon-key|+] of a truth, [lexicon-key|+] unless, [lexicon-key|+] verily, when, whereas, whether, while, [lexicon-key|+] yet.
