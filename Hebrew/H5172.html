H5172 נָחַשׁ / נחש

nâchash

(naw-khash' | naw-HAHSH | na-HAHSH)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to hiss, i.e., whisper a (magic) spell; generally, to prognosticate; [lexicon-key|×] certainly, divine, enchanter, (use) [lexicon-key|×] enchantment, learn by experience, [lexicon-key|×] indeed, diligently observe.

  • to practice divination, divine, observe signs, learn by experience, diligently observe, practice fortunetelling, take as an omen
    • (Piel)
      • to practice divination
      • to observe the signs or omens
