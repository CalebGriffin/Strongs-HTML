H7611 שְׁאֵרִית / שארית

shᵉʼêrîyth

(sheh-ay-reeth' | sheh-ay-REET | sheh-ay-REET)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H7604]; a remainder or residual (surviving, final) portion; that had escaped, be left, posterity, remain(-der), remnant, residue, rest.

  • rest, residue, remainder, remnant
    • rest, what is left
    • remainder, descendants
