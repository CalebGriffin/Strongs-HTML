H5074 נָדַד / נדד

nâdad

(naw-dad' | naw-DAHD | na-DAHD)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to wave to and fro (rarely to flap up and down); figuratively, to rove, flee, or (causatively) to drive away; chase (away), [lexicon-key|×] could not, depart, flee ([lexicon-key|×] apace, away), (re-)move, thrust away, wander (abroad, -er, -ing).

  • to retreat, flee, depart, move, wander abroad, stray, flutter
    • (Qal)
      • to retreat, flee
      • to flee, depart
      • to wander, stray
      • to flutter (of birds)
    • (Poal) to flee away, be chased
    • (Hiphil) to chase away
    • (Hophal) to be chased away
    • (Hithpolel) to flee away
