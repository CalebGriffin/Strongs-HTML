H2945 טַף / טף

ṭaph

(taf | tahp | tahf)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H2952] (perhaps referring to the tripping gait of children); a family (mostly used collectively in the singular); (little) children (ones), families.

  • children, little children, little ones
