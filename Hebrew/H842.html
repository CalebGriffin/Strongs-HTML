H842 אֲשֵׁרָה / אשרה

ʼăshêrâh

(ash-ay-raw' | uh-shay-RAW | uh-shay-RA)

Definition

proper feminine noun; groves (for idol worship); or אֲשֵׁירָה; from [H833]; happy; Asherah (or Astarte) a Phoenician goddess; also an image of the same; grove.

  • a Babylonian (Astarte)-Canaanite goddess (of fortune and happiness), the supposed consort of Baal, her images
    • the goddess, goddesses
    • her images
    • sacred trees or poles set up near an altar

Note

Compare [H6253].
