H57 אָבֵל / אבל

ʼâbêl

(aw-bale' | aw-BALE | ah-VALE)

Definition

adjective; from [H56]; lamenting; mourn(-er, -ing).

  • mourning
    • for the dead
    • because of calamity
    • of rites of mourning
  • mourner (substantive)
    • for the dead
    • for calamity
