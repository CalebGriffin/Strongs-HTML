H8334 שָׁרַת / שרת

shârath

(shaw-rath' | shaw-RAHT | sha-RAHT)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to attend as a menial or worshipper; figuratively, to contribute to; minister (unto), (do) serve(-ant, -ice, -itor), wait on.

  • (Piel) to minister, serve, minister to
