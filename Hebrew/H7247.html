H7247 רִבְלָה / רבלה

Riblâh

(rib-law' | rib-LAW | reev-LA)

Definition

proper locative noun; fertility; from an unused root meaning to be fruitful; fertile; Riblah, a place in Syria; Riblah.

  • a place on the eastern boundary of Israel
  • a town in the land of Hamath on the great road between Babylon and Palestine
