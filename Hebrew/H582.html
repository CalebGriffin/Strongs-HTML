H582 אֱנוֹשׁ / אנוש

ʼĕnôwsh

(en-oshe' | eh-NOHSH | ay-NOHSH)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H605]; properly, a mortal (and thus differing from the more dignified H0120); hence, a man in general (singly or collectively); another, [lexicon-key|×] (blood-)thirsty, certain, chap(-man); divers, fellow, [lexicon-key|×] in the flower of their age, husband, (certain, mortal) man, people, person, servant, some ([lexicon-key|×] of them), [lexicon-key|+] stranger, those, [lexicon-key|+] their trade.

  • man, mortal man, person, mankind
    • of an individual
    • men (collective)
    • man, mankind

Note

It is often unexpressed in the English versions, especially when used in apposition with another word. Compare [H376].
