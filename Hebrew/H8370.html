H8370 שְׁתַר בּוֹזְנַי / שתר בוזני

Shᵉthar Bôwzᵉnay

(sheth-ar' bo-zen-ah'-ee | sheh-TAHR boh-dzeh-NAI | sheh-TAHR boh-zeh-NAI)

Definition

proper masculine noun; star of splendor; of foreign derivation; Shethar-Bozenai, a Persian officer; Shetharboznai.

  • a Persian officer in the reign of Darius
