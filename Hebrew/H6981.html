H6981 קוֹרֵא / קורא

Qôwrêʼ

(ko-ray' | koh-RAY | koh-RAY)

Definition

proper masculine noun; crier; or קֹרֵא; (1 Chronicles 26:1), active participle of [H7121]; crier; Kore, the name of two Israelites; Kore.

  • a Korahite Levite, ancestor of Shallum and Meshelemiah, chief porters in the reign of David
  • a Levite, son of Imnah, in the reign of king Hezekiah of Judah
