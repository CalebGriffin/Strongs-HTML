H7602 שָׁאַף / שאף

shâʼaph

(shaw-af' | shaw-AP | sha-AF)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to inhale eagerly; figuratively, to cover; by implication, to be angry; also to hasten; desire (earnestly), devour, haste, pant, snuff up, swallow up.

  • to gasp, pant, pant after, long for, breathe heavily
    • (Qal)
      • to gasp, pant after, snuff up
      • to gasp or pant (with desire), pant after, be eager for
      • to thirst for one's blood (from actions of animals) (figuratively)
  • to crush, trample, trample upon
    • (Qal) to trample upon, crush
