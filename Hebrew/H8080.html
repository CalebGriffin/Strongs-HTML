H8080 שָׁמַן / שמן

shâman

(shaw-man' | shaw-MAHN | sha-MAHN)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to shine, i.e., (by analogy) be (causatively, make) oily or gross; become (make, wax) fat.

  • to be or become fat, grow fat
    • (Qal) fat (substantive)
    • (Hiphil) to make fat, show fatness
