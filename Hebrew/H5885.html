H5885 עֵין שֶׁמֶשׁ / עין שמש

ʻÊyn Shemesh

(ane sheh'-mesh | ane sheh-MESH | ane sheh-MESH)

Definition

proper locative noun; fountain of the sun; from [H5869] and [H8121]; fountain of the sun; En-Shemesh, a place in Palestine; Enshemesh.

  • a spring on the boundary of Judah on the south and Benjamin on the north
