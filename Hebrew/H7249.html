H7249 רַב־סָרִיס / רבסריס

Rab-Çârîyç

(rab-saw-reece' | rahb saw-REES | rahv sa-REES)

Definition

proper masculine noun; from [H7227] and a foreign word for a eunuch; chief chamberlain; Rab-Saris, a Babylonian official; Rab-saris.

  • chief eunuch
    • Rab-saris, chief eunuch, a high ranking Babylonian official
