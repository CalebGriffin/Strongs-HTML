H7649 שָׂבֵעַ / שבע

sâbêaʻ

(saw-bay'-ah | saw-BAY-ah | sa-VAY-ah)

Definition

adjective; from [H7646]; satiated (in a pleasant or disagreeable sense); full (of), satisfied (with).

  • sated, satisfied, surfeited
    • sated, abounding, satisfied
    • surfeited (bad sense)
