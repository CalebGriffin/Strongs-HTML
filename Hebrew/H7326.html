H7326 רוּשׁ / רוש

rûwsh

(roosh | roosh | roosh)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be destitute; lack, needy, (make self) poor (man).

  • to be poor, be in want, lack
    • (Qal)
      • to be poor
      • to be in want or hunger
      • poor man (substantive)
    • (Hithpolel) one impoverishing himself (participle)
