H7989 שַׁלִּיט / שליט

shallîyṭ

(shal-leet' | shahl-LEET | sha-LEET)

Definition

adjective; from [H7980]; potent; concretely, a prince or warrior; governor, mighty, that hath power, ruler.

  • having mastery, domineering, master
    • having mastery
      • ruler (substantive)
    • domineering, imperious
