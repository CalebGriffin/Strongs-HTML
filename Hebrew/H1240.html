H1240 בְּקַר / בקר

bᵉqar

(bek-ar' | beh-KAHR | beh-KAHR)

Definition

verb; corresponding to [H1239]; properly, to plough, or (generally) break forth, i.e., (figuratively) to inspect, admire, care for, consider; inquire, make search.

  • to seek, enquire
    • (Pael) to enquire
    • (Ithpael) to let search be made
