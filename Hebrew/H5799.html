H5799 עֲזָאזֵל / עזאזל

ʻăzâʼzêl

(az-aw-zale' | uh-dzaw-DZALE | uh-za-ZALE)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H5795] and [H235]; goat of departure; the scapegoat; scapegoat.

  • entire removal, scapegoat
    • refers to the goat used for sacrifice for the sins of the people
    • meaning dubious
