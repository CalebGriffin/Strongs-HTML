H6578 פְּרָת / פרת

Pᵉrâth

(per-awth' | peh-RAWT | peh-RAHT)

Definition

proper masculine noun; fruitfulness; from an unused root meaning to break forth; rushing; Perath (i.e., Euphrates), a river of the East; Euphrates.

  • the largest and longest river of western Asia; rises from two chief sources in the Armenian mountains and flows into the Persian Gulf
