H2290 חֲגוֹר / חגור

chăgôwr

(khag-ore' | huh-ɡORE | huh-ɡORE)

Definition

proper masculine noun; or חֲגֹר; and (feminine) חֲגוֹרָה; or חֲגֹרָה; from [H2296]; a belt (for the waist); apron, armour, gird(-le).

  • girdle, belt
  • girdle, loin-covering, belt, loin-cloth, armour
