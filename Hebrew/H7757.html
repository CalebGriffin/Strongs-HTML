H7757 שׁוּל / שול

shûwl

(shool | shool | shool)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning to hang down; a skirt; by implication, a bottom edge; hem, skirt, train.

  • skirt (of robe)
    • of high priest's robe
    • of God's train, city as woman, ignominy, defilement (figuratively)
