H8535 תָּם / תם

tâm

(tawm | tawm | tahm)

Definition

adjective; from [H8552]; complete; usually (morally) pious; specifically, gentle, dear; coupled together, perfect, plain, undefiled, upright.

  • perfect, complete
    • complete, perfect
      • one who lacks nothing in physical strength, beauty, etc
    • sound, wholesome
      • an ordinary, quiet sort of person
    • complete, morally innocent, having integrity
      • one who is morally and ethically pure
