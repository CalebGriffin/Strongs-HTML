H7626 שֵׁבֶט / שבט

shêbeṭ

(shay'-bet | shay-BET | shay-VET)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root probably meaning to branch off; a scion, i.e., (literally) a stick (for punishing, writing, fighting, ruling, walking, etc.) or (figuratively) a clan; [lexicon-key|×] correction, dart, rod, sceptre, staff, tribe.

  • rod, staff, branch, offshoot, club, sceptre, tribe
    • rod, staff
    • shaft (of spear, dart)
    • club (of shepherd's implement)
    • truncheon, sceptre (mark of authority)
    • clan, tribe
