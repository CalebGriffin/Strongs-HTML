H6957 קַו / קו

qav

(kav | ka | kahv)

Definition

masculine noun; or קָו; from [H6960] (compare [H6961]); a cord (as connecting), especially for measuring; figuratively, a rule; also a rim, a musical string or accord; line.

  • cord, line, measuring-line
    • cord
    • measuring-line
  • ([lexicon-key|BDB]) onomatapoetic mimicry of Isaiah's words, perhaps senseless

Note

Compare [H6978].
