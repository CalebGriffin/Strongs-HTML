H5855 עַטְרוֹת שׁוֹפָן / עטרות שופן

ʻAṭrôwth Shôwphân

(at-roth' sho-fawn' | at-ROTE shoh-PAWN | at-ROTE shoh-FAHN)

Definition

proper locative noun; crowns of their rapine; from the same as [H5852] and a name otherwise unused (being from the same as [H8226]) meaning hidden; crowns of Shophan; Atroth-Shophan, a place in Palestine; Atroth, Shophan (as if two places).

  • a town in the territory of Gad
