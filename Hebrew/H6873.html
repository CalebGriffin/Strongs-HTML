H6873 צָרַח / צרח

tsârach

(tsaw-rakh' | tsaw-RA | tsa-RAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be clear (in tone, i.e., shrill), i.e., to whoop; cry, roar.

  • to cry, roar, make a shrill or clear sound
    • (Qal) to roar
    • (Hiphil) to utter a roar, cry out (a battle cry)
