H769 אַרְנוֹן / ארנון

ʼArnôwn

(ar-nohn' | ar-NONE | ar-NONE)

Definition

proper locative noun; rushing stream; or אַרְנֹן; from [H7442]; a brawling stream; the Arnon, a river east of the Jordan, also its territory; Arnon.

  • a river and surrounding valley in south Palestine, forms the border between Moab and the Amorites
