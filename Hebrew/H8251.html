H8251 שִׁקּוּץ / שקוץ

shiqqûwts

(shik-koots' | shik-KOOTS | shee-KOOTS)

Definition

masculine noun; or שִׁקֻּץ; from [H8262]; disgusting, i.e., filthy; especially idolatrous or (concretely) an idol; abominable filth (idol, -ation), detestable (thing).

  • detestable thing or idol, abominable thing, abomination, idol, detested thing
