H5594 סָפַד / ספד

çâphad

(saw-fad' | saw-PAHD | sa-FAHD)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to tear the hair and beat the breasts (as Middle Easterners do in grief); generally to lament; by implication, to wail; lament, mourn(-er), wail.

  • to wail, lament, mourn
    • (Qal)
      • to wail, lament
      • wailers (participle)
    • (Niphal) to be lamented, be bewailed
