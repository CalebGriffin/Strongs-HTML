H8387 תַּאֲנַת שִׁלֹה / תאנת שלה

Taʼănath Shilôh

(tah-an-ath' shee-lo' | ta-uh-NAHT shih-LOH | ta-uh-NAHT shee-LOH)

Definition

proper locative noun; approach to Shiloh; from [H8385] and [H7887]; approach of Shiloh; Taanath-Shiloh, a place in Palestine; Taanath-shiloh.

  • a landmark on the boundary of Ephraim
