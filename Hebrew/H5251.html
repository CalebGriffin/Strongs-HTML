H5251 נֵס / נס

nêç

(nace | nase | nase)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H5264]; a flag; also a sail; by implication, a flagstaff; generally a signal; figuratively, a token; banner, pole, sail, (en-)sign, standard.

  • something lifted up, standard, signal, signal pole, ensign, banner, sign, sail
    • standard (as rallying point), signal
    • standard (pole)
    • ensign, signal
