H8660 תִּרְשָׁתָא / תרשתא

Tirshâthâʼ

(teer-shaw-thaw' | tir-shaw-TAW | teer-sha-TA)

Definition

masculine noun; of foreign derivation; Tirshatha, the title of a Persian deputy or governor; Tirshatha.

  • Tirshatha - governor
    • a title used by the Persian governor in Judea
      • Nehemiah used this title
