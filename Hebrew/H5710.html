H5710 עָדָה / עדה

ʻâdâh

(aw-daw' | aw-DAW | ah-DA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to advance, i.e., pass on or continue; causatively, to remove; specifically, to bedeck (i.e., bring an ornament upon); adorn, deck (self), pass by, take away.

  • to pass on, advance, go on, pass by, remove
    • (Qal) to pass on, put on
    • (Hiphil) to remove
