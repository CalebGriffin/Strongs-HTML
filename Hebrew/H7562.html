H7562 רֶשַׁע / רשע

reshaʻ

(reh'-shah | reh-SHA | reh-SHA)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H7561]; a wrong (especially moral); iniquity, wicked(-ness).

  • wrong, wickedness, guilt
    • wickedness (as violence and crime against civil law)
    • wickedness (of enemies)
    • wickedness (in ethical relations)
