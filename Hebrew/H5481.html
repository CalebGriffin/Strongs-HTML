H5481 סוּמְפּוֹנְיָה / סומפוניה

çûwmᵉpôwnᵉyâh

(soom-po-neh-yaw' | soo-mep-poh-neh-YAW | soo-meh-poh-neh-YA)

Definition

feminine noun; or סוּמְפֹּנְיָה; (Aramaic), or סִיפֹנְיָא; (Daniel 3:10) (Aramaic), of Greek origin; a bagpipe (with a double pipe); dulcimer.

  • a musical instrument, wind instrument, bagpipe, double pipe, panpipes
    • perhaps dulcimer, panpipes
