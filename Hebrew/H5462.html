H5462 סָגַר / סגר

çâgar

(saw-gar' | saw-ɡAHR | sa-ɡAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to shut up; figuratively, to surrender; close up, deliver (up), give over (up), inclose, [lexicon-key|×] pure, repair, shut (in, self, out, up, up together), stop, [lexicon-key|×] straitly.

  • to shut, close
    • (Qal)
      • to shut
      • to close, close up
      • closed up, closely joined, shut up
    • (Niphal)
      • to be shut up
      • to be shut or closed
    • (Piel) to shut up, deliver up
    • (Pual) to be shut up
    • (Hiphil)
      • to deliver up
      • to shut up, imprison
