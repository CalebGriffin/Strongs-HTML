H5736 עֲדַף / עדף

ʻădaph

(aw-daf' | uh-DAHP | uh-DAHF)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be (causatively have) redundant; be more, odd number, be (have) over (and above), overplus, remain.

  • to remain over, be in excess
    • (Qal)
      • to be in excess
        • excess (substantive)
        • surplus (participle)
    • (Hiphil) to have a surplus
