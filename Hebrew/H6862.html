H6862 צַר / צר

tsar

(tsar | tsahr | tsahr)

Definition

adjective; or צָר; from [H6887]; a pebble (as in H6864); adversary, afflicted(-tion), anguish, close, distress, enemy, flint, foe, narrow, small, sorrow, strait, tribulation, trouble.

  • narrow, tight
  • straits, distress
  • adversary, foe, enemy, oppressor
  • hard pebble, flint
