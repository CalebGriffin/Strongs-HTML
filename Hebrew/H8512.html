H8512 תֵּל אָבִיב / תל אביב

Têl ʼÂbîyb

(tale aw-beeb' | tale aw-BEEB | tale ah-VEEV)

Definition

proper locative noun; mound of the flood; from [H8510] and [H24]; mound of green growth; Tel-Abib, a place in Chaldaea; Tel-abib.

  • a city in Babylon, the home of the prophet Ezekiel, located on the river Chebar which was probably a branch of the Euphrates
