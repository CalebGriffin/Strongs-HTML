H6914 קִבְרוֹת הַתַּאֲוָה / קברות התאוה

Qibrôwth hat-Taʼă-vâh

(kib-roth' hat-tahav-aw' | kib-ROTE haht-ta-uh-WAW | keev-ROTE ha-ta-uh-VA)

Definition

proper noun; graves of lust; from the feminine plural of [H6913] and [H8378] with the article interposed; graves of the longing; Kibroth-hat-Taavh, a place in the Desert; Kibroth-hattaavah.

  • a station of Israel in the wilderness 3 campsites away from Sinai near the gulf of Akabah
