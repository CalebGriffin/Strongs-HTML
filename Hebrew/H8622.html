H8622 תְּקוּפָה / תקופה

tᵉqûwphâh

(tek-oo-faw' | teh-koo-PAW | teh-koo-FA)

Definition

feminine noun; or תְּקֻפָה; from [H5362]; a revolution, i.e., (of the sun) course, (of time) lapse; circuit, come about, end.

  • coming round, circuit of time or space, a turning, circuit
    • at the circuit (as adverb)
