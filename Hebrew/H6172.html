H6172 עֶרְוָה / ערוה

ʻervâh

(er-vaw' | er-WAW | er-VA)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H6168]; nudity, literally (especially the pudenda) or figuratively (disgrace, blemish); nakedness, shame, unclean(-ness).

  • nakedness, nudity, shame, pudenda
    • pudenda (implying shameful exposure)
    • nakedness of a thing, indecency, improper behaviour
    • exposed, undefended (figuratively)
