H3939 לַעֲנָה / לענה

laʻănâh

(lah-an-aw' | la-uh-NAW | la-uh-NA)

Definition

feminine noun; from an unused root supposed to mean to curse; wormwood (regarded as poisonous, and therefore accursed); hemlock, wormwood.

  • wormwood
    • bitterness (metaphorically)
