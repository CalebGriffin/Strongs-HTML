H5741 עַדְרִיאֵל / עדריאל

ʻAdrîyʼêl

(ad-ree-ale' | ad-ree-ALE | ad-ree-ALE)

Definition

proper masculine noun; flock of God; from [H5739] and [H410]; flock of God; Adriel, an Israelite; Adriel.

  • son of Barzillai, and the man to whom Saul gave his daughter Merab in marriage although she was already promised to David
