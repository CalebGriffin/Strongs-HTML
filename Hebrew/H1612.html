H1612 גֶּפֶן / גפן

gephen

(gheh'-fen | ɡeh-PEN | ɡeh-FEN)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning to bend; a vine (as twining), especially the grape; vine, tree.

  • vine, vine tree
    • of Israel (figuratively)
    • of stars fading at Jehovah's judgment (metaphorically)
    • of prosperity
