H8184 שְׂעֹרָה / שערה

sᵉʻôrâh

(seh-o-raw' | seh-oh-RAW | seh-oh-RA)

Definition

feminine noun; or שְׂעוֹרָה (feminine meaning the plant); and (masculine meaning the grain); also שְׂעֹר; or שְׂעוֹר; from [H8175] in the sense of roughness; barley (as villose); barley.

  • barley
    • barley (of the plant)
    • barley (of the meal or grain)
