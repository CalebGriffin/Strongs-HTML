H8664 תִּשְׁבִּי / תשבי

Tishbîy

(tish-bee' | tish-BEE | teesh-BEE)

Definition

proper noun; captivity; patrial from an unused name meaning recourse; a Tishbite or inhabitant of Tishbeh (in Gilead); Tishbite.

  • the home of Elijah
    • site unknown but maybe the town of Tishbe
  • an inhabitant of Tishbe or Tishbi or Tesheb
    • site unknown and name uncertain
