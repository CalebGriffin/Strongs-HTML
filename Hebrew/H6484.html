H6484 פָּצַר / פצר

pâtsar

(paw-tsar' | paw-TSAHR | pa-TSAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to peck at, i.e., (figuratively) stun or dull; press, urge, stubbornness.

  • to press, push
    • (Qal) to push, press
    • (Hiphil) be insolent, display pushing (arrogance, presumption)
