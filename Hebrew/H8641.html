H8641 תְּרוּמָה / תרומה

tᵉrûwmâh

(ter-oo-maw' | teh-roo-MAW | teh-roo-MA)

Definition

feminine noun; or תְּרֻמָה; (Deuteronomy 12:11), from [H7311]; a present (as offered up), especially in sacrifice or as tribute; gift, heave offering (shoulder), oblation, offered(-ing).

  • contribution, offering
    • a heave offering
    • any offering
    • an offering to God
    • an offering (of grain, money, etc)
    • contribution
