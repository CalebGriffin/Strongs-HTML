H6908 קָבַץ / קבץ

qâbats

(kaw-bats' | kaw-BAHTS | ka-VAHTS)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to grasp, i.e., collect; assemble (selves), gather (bring) (together, selves together, up), heap, resort, [lexicon-key|×] surely, take up.

  • to gather, assemble
    • (Qal) to gather, collect, assemble
    • (Niphal)
      • to assemble, gather
      • to be gathered
    • (Piel) to gather, gather together, take away
    • (Pual) to be gathered together
    • (Hithpael) to gather together, be gathered together
