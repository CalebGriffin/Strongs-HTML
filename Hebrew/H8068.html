H8068 שָׁמִיר / שמיר

shâmîyr

(shaw-meer' | shaw-MEER | sha-MEER)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H8104] in the original sense of pricking; a thorn; also (from its keenness for scratching) a gem, probably the diamond; adamant (stone), brier, diamond.

  • thorn(s), adamant, flint
    • thorns, thorn-bushes
    • adamant (as sharp)
    • a sharp stone, flint
      • perhaps a diamond
