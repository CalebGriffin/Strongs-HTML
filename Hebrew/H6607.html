H6607 פֶּתַח / פתח

pethach

(peh'-thakh | peh-TA | peh-TAHK)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H6605]; an opening (literally), i.e., door (gate) or entrance way; door, entering (in), entrance (-ry), gate, opening, place.

  • opening, doorway, entrance
