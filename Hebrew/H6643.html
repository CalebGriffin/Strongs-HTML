H6643 צְבִי / צבי

tsᵉbîy

(tseb-ee' | tseh-BEE | tseh-VEE)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H6638] in the sense of prominence; a gazelle (as beautiful); beautiful(-ty), glorious(-ry), goodly, pleasant, roe(-buck).

  • beauty, glory, honour
    • beauty, decoration
    • honour
  • roebuck, gazelle
    • perhaps an extinct animal, exact meaning unknown
