H8181 שֵׂעָר / שער

sêʻâr

(say-awr' | say-AWR | say-AR)

Definition

masculine noun; or שַׂעַר; (Isaiah 7:20), from [H8175] in the sense of dishevelling; hair (as if tossed or bristling); hair(-y), [lexicon-key|×] rough.

  • hair
    • hair (of animals, man)
    • hair (of garment made of hair)
