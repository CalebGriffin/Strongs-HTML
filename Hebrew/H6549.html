H6549 פַּרְעֹה נְכֹה / פרעה נכה

Parʻôh Nᵉkôh

(par-o' nek-o' | pahr-OH neh-KOH | pahr-OH neh-HOH)

Definition

proper masculine noun; great house-he is smitten; or פַּרְעֹה נְכוֹ; of Egyptian derivation; Paroh-Nekoh (or Paroh-Neko), an Egyptian king; Pharaoh-necho, Pharaohnechoh.

  • the Pharaoh of Egypt who fought king Josiah of Judah at Megiddo and killed him
