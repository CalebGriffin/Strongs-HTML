H2331 חָוָה / חוה

châvâh

(khaw-vah' | haw-WAW | ha-VA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; (compare [H2324], [H2421]); properly, to live; by implication (intensively) to declare or show; show.

  • (Piel) to tell, declare, show, make known
  • ([lexicon-key|CLBL]) to breath
