H6100 עֶצְיוֹן גֶּבֶר / עצין גבר

ʻEtsyôwn Geber

(ets-yone' gheh'ber | ets-YONE ɡeh-BER | ets-YONE ɡeh-VER)

Definition

proper locative noun; backbone of a man; from [H6096] and [H1397]; backbone-like of a man; Etsjon-Geber, a place on the Red Sea; Ezion-geber.

  • the last station during the exodus of the Israelites before they came to the wilderness of Zin; located near Elath at the head of the Gulf of Akaba
