H5766 עֶוֶל / עול

ʻevel

(eh'-vel | eh-WEL | eh-VEL)

Definition

noun; or עָוֶל; and (feminine) עַוְלָה; or עוֹלָה; or עֹלָה; from [H5765]; (moral) evil; iniquity, perverseness, unjust(-ly), unrighteousness(-ly); wicked(-ness).

  • injustice, unrighteousness, wrong
    • violent deeds of injustice
    • injustice (of speech)
    • injustice (generally)
