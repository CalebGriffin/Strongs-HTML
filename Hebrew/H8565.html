H8565 תַּן / תן

tan

(tan | tahn | tahn)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root probably meaning to elongate; a monster (as preternaturally formed), i.e., a sea-serpent (or other huge marine animal); also a jackal (or other hideous land animal); dragon, whale.

  • dragon, maybe the extinct dinosaur the plesiosaurus, whale

Note

Compare [H8577].
