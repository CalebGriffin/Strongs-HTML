H6154 עֵרֶב / ערב

ʻêreb

(ay'-reb | ay-REB | ay-REV)

Definition

masculine noun; or עֶרֶב; (1 Kings 10:15), (with the article prefix), from [H6148]; the web (or transverse threads of cloth); also a mixture, (or mongrel race); Arabia, mingled people, mixed (multitude), woof.

  • woof
    • as mixed, interwoven
    • knitted material
  • mixture, mixed people, mixed company
