H519 אָמָה / אמה

ʼâmâh

(aw-maw' | aw-MAW | ah-MA)

Definition

feminine noun; apparently a primitive word; a maid-servant or female slave; (hand-)bondmaid(-woman), maid(-servant).

  • maid-servant, female slave, maid, handmaid, concubine
    • of humility (figuratively)
