H2931 טָמֵא / טמא

ṭâmêʼ

(taw-may' | taw-MAY | ta-MAY)

Definition

adjective; from [H2930]; foul in a religious sense; defiled, [lexicon-key|+] infamous, polluted(-tion), unclean.

  • unclean, impure
    • ethically and religiously
    • ritually
    • of places
