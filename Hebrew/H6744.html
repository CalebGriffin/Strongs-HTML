H6744 צְלַח / צלח

tsᵉlach

(tsel-akh' | tseh-LA | tseh-LAHK)

Definition

verb; corresponding to [H6743]; to advance (transitive or intransitive); promote, prosper.

  • to prosper
    • (Aphel)
      • to cause to prosper
      • show prosperity, be prosperous, have success, be successful
