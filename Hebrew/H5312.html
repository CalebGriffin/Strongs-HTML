H5312 נְפַק / נפק

nᵉphaq

(nef-ak' | neh-PAHK | neh-FAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to issue; causatively, to bring out; come (go, take) forth (out).

  • to go or come out, bring or come forth
    • (Peal) to go out or forth
    • (Aphel) to bring forth
