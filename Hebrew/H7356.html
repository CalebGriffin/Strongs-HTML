H7356 רַחַם / רחם

racham

(rakh'-am | ra-HAHM | ra-HAHM)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H7355]; compassion (in the plural); bowels, compassion, damsel, tender love, (great, tender) mercy, pity, womb.

  • womb (noun masculine)
  • compassion (noun masculine absolute plural intensive)
