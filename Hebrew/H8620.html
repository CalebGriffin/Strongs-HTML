H8620 תְּקוֹעַ / תקוע

Tᵉqôwaʻ

(tek-o'-ah | teh-KOH-ah | teh-KOH-ah)

Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; a stockade; a form of [H8619]; Tekoa, a place in Palestine; Tekoa, Tekoah.

  • a Judaite, son of Ashur and grandson of Hezron (noun proper masculine)
  • a town in the hill country of Judah near Hebron built by king Rehoboam of Judah; birthplace of Amos (noun proper locative)
  • a wilderness area where king Jehoshaphat of Judah defeated the people of Moab, Ammon, and Mount Seir (noun proper locative)
