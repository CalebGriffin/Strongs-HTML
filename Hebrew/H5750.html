H5750 עוֹד / עוד

ʻôwd

(ode | ode | ode)

Definition

adverb; or עֹד; from [H5749]; properly, iteration or continuance; used only adverbially (with or without preposition), again, repeatedly, still, more; again, [lexicon-key|×] all life long, at all, besides, but, else, further(-more), henceforth, (any) longer, (any) more(-over), [lexicon-key|×] once, since, (be) still, when, (good, the) while (having being), (as, because, whether, while) yet (within).

  • a going round, continuance (substantive)
  • still, yet, again, besides (adverb)
    • still, yet (of continuance or persistence)
    • still, yet, more (of addition or repetition)
    • again
    • still, moreover, besides
