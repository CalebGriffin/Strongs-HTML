H5775 עוֹף / עוף

ʻôwph

(ofe | ope | ofe)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H5774]; a bird (as covered with feathers, or rather as covering with wings), often collectively; bird, that flieth, flying, fowl.

  • flying creatures, fowl, insects, birds
    • fowl, birds
    • winged insects
