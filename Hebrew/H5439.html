H5439 סָבִיב / סביב

çâbîyb

(saw-beeb' | saw-BEEB | sa-VEEV)

Definition

adverb; or (feminine) סְבִיבָה; from [H5437]; (as noun) a circle, neighbour, or environs; but chiefly (as adverb, with or without preposition) around; (place, round) about, circuit, compass, on every side.

  • places round about, circuit, round about (substantive)
  • in a circuit, a circuit, round about (adverb)
  • in the circuit, from every side (preposition)
