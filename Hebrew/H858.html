H858 אָתָה / אתה

ʼâthâh

(aw-thaw' | aw-TAW | ah-TA)

Definition

verb; or אָתָא; (Aramaic), corresponding to [H857]; to come, to bring; (be-)come, bring.

  • to come, arrive
    • (Peal) to come
    • (Aphel) to bring
    • (Hophal) to be brought
  • used in the NT in the phrase "maranatha" - "Lord come"

Note

This word is used in the Greek New Testament in the word "maranatha" (meaning "Lord come").
