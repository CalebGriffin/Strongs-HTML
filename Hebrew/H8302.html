H8302 שִׁרְיוֹן / שריון

shiryôwn

(shir-yone' | shir-YONE | sheer-YONE)

Definition

noun; or שִׁרְיֹן; and שִׁרְיָן; also (feminine) שִׁרְיָה; and שִׁרְיֹנָה; from [H8281] in the original sense of turning; a corslet (as if twisted); breastplate, coat of mail, habergeon, harness.

  • body armour
  • a weapon
    • perhaps a lance, javelin
