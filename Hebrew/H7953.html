H7953 שָׁלָה / שלה

shâlâh

(shaw-law' | shaw-LAW | sha-LA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (rather cognate (by contraction) to the base of [H5394], [H7997] and their congeners through the idea of extracting); to draw out or off, i.e., remove (the soul by death); take away.

  • (Qal) to draw out, extract
