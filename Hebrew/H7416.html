H7416 רִמּוֹן / רמון

rimmôwn

(rim-mone' | rim-MONE | ree-MONE)

Definition

masculine noun; or רִמֹּן; from [H7426]; a pomegranate, the tree (from its upright growth) or the fruit (also an artificial ornament); pomegranate.

  • pomegranate
    • as tree
    • as fruit
    • as pomegranate shaped ornaments in temple
