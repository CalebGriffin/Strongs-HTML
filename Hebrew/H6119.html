H6119 עָקֵב / עקב

ʻâqêb

(aw-kabe' | aw-KABE | ah-KAVE)

Definition

masculine noun; or (feminine) עִקְּבָה; from [H6117]; a heel (as protuberant); hence, a track; figuratively, the rear (of an army); heel, (horse-)hoof, last, lier in wait (by mistake for [H6120]), (foot-)step.

  • heel, rear, footprint, hinder part, hoof, rear of a troop, footstep
    • heel
    • mark of heel, footprint
    • hinder part, rear
