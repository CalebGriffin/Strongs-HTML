H5402 נֶשֶׁק / נשק

nesheq

(neh'-shek | neh-SHEK | neh-SHEK)

Definition

masculine noun; or נֵשֶׁק; from [H5401]; military equipment, i.e., (collectively) arms (offensive or defensive), or (concretely) an arsenal; armed men, armour(-y), battle, harness, weapon.

  • equipment, weapons, armoury
    • equipment, weapons
    • armoury
