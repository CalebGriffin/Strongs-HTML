H8085 שָׁמַע / שמע

shâmaʻ

(shaw-mah' | shaw-MA | sha-MA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to hear intelligently (often with implication of attention, obedience, etc.; causatively, to tell, etc.); [lexicon-key|×] attentively, call (gather) together, [lexicon-key|×] carefully, [lexicon-key|×] certainly, consent, consider, be content, declare, [lexicon-key|×] diligently, discern, give ear, (cause to, let, make to) hear(-ken) (tell), [lexicon-key|×] indeed, listen, make (a) noise, (be) obedient, obey, perceive, (make a) proclaim(-ation), publish, regard, report, shew (forth), (make a) sound, [lexicon-key|×] surely, tell, understand, whosoever (heareth), witness.

  • to hear, listen to, obey (verb)
    • (Qal)
      • to hear (perceive by ear)
      • to hear of or concerning
      • to hear (have power to hear)
      • to hear with attention or interest, listen to
      • to understand (language)
      • to hear (of judicial cases)
      • to listen, give heed
        • to consent, agree
        • to grant request
      • to listen to, yield to
      • to obey, be obedient
    • (Niphal)
      • to be heard (of voice or sound)
      • to be heard of
      • to be regarded, be obeyed
    • (Piel) to cause to hear, call to hear, summon
    • (Hiphil)
      • to cause to hear, tell, proclaim, utter a sound
      • to sound aloud (musical term)
      • to make proclamation, summon
      • to cause to be heard
  • sound (noun masculine)
