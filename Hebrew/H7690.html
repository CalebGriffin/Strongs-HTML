H7690 שַׂגִּיא / שגיא

saggîyʼ

(sag-ghee' | sahg-GEE | sa-ɡEE)

Definition

adjective; corresponding to [H7689]; large (in size, quantity or number, also adverbial); exceeding, great(-ly); many, much, sore, very.

  • great, much (adjective)
    • great
    • much, many
  • exceedingly (adverb)
