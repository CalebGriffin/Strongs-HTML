H7087 קָפָא / קפא

qâphâʼ

(kaw-faw' | kaw-PAW | ka-FA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to shrink, i.e., thicken (as unracked wine, curdled milk, clouded sky, frozen water); congeal, curdle, dark, settle.

  • to thicken, condense, congeal, settle, become dense (verb)
    • (Qal) to be condensed
      • thickening (participle)
    • (Hiphil) to cause to curdle
  • congelation (noun masculine)
