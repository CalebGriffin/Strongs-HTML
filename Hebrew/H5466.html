H5466 סָדִין / סדין

çâdîyn

(saw-deen' | saw-DEEN | sa-DEEN)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning to envelop; a wrapper, i.e., shirt; fine linen, sheet.

  • linen wrapper
    • rectangular piece of fine linen worn as outer, or at night, as a sole garment
