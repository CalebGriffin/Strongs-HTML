H8266 שָׁקַר / שקר

shâqar

(shaw-kar' | shaw-KAHR | sha-KAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to cheat, i.e., be untrue (usually in words); fail, deal falsely, lie.

  • to do or deal falsely, be false, trick, cheat
    • (Piel) to deal falsely
    • (Qal) to deal falsely
