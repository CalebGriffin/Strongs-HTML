H5492 סוּפָה / סופה

çûwphâh

(soo-faw' | soo-PAW | soo-FA)

Definition

feminine noun; honeycomb, overflow; from [H5486]; a hurricane; Red Sea, storm, tempest, whirlwind, Red sea.

  • storm-wind (noun feminine)
  • a place east of the Jordan (noun proper locative)
