H8289 שָׁרוֹן / שרון

Shârôwn

(shaw-rone' | shaw-RONE | sha-RONE)

Definition

proper noun; a plain; probably abridged from [H3474]; plain; Sharon, the name of a place in Palestine; Lasharon, Sharon.

  • plain, level (noun masculine)
  • the district lying between the mountains of central Palestine and the Mediterranean Sea and north of Joppa (noun proper locative)
  • a district on the east of the Jordan around Gilead and Bashan (noun proper locative)
