H7718 שֹׁהַם / שהם

shôham

(sho'-ham | shoh-HAHM | shoh-HAHM)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root probably mean to blanch; a gem, probably the beryl (from its pale green color); onyx.

  • a precious stone or gem
    • probably onyx, chrysoprasus, beryl, malachite
