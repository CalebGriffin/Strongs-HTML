H5371 נֵרְגַּל שַׁרְאֶצֶר / נרגל שראצר

Nêrᵉgal Sharʼetser

(nare-gal' shar-eh'-tser | nay-reg-GAHL shahr-eh-TSER | nay-reh-ɡAHL shahr-eh-TSER)

Definition

proper masculine noun; prince of fire; from [H5370] and [H8272]; Nergal-Sharetser, the name of two Babylonians; Nergal-sharezer.

  • chief soothsayer and a ruler in the army of Nebuchadnezzar
