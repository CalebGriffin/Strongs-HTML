H709 אַרְגֹּב / ארגב

ʼArgôb

(ar-gobe' | ar-ɡOBE | ar-ɡOVE)

Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; heap of clods; from the same as [H7263]; stony; Argob, a district of Palestine; Argob.

  • a district or area in Bashan (noun proper locative)
  • one of king Pekah's officers (noun proper masculine)
