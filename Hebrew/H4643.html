H4643 מַעֲשֵׂר / מעשר

maʻăsêr

(mah-as-ayr' | ma-uh-SARE | ma-uh-SARE)

Definition

masculine noun; or מַעֲשַׂר; and (in plural) feminine מַעַשְׂרָה; from [H6240]; a tenth; especially a tithe; tenth (part), tithe(-ing).

  • tithe, tenth part
    • tenth part
    • tithe, payment of a tenth part
