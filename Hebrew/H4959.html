H4959 מָשַׁשׁ / משש

mâshash

(maw-shash' | maw-SHAHSH | ma-SHAHSH)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to feel of; by implication, to grope; feel, grope, search.

  • to feel, grope
    • (Qal) to feel, grope
    • (Piel) to feel over or through, grope
      • to feel through
      • to grope
    • (Hiphil) to feel
