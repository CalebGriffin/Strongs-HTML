H7138 קָרוֹב / קרוב

qârôwb

(kaw-robe' | kaw-ROBE | ka-ROVE)

Definition

adjective; or קָרֹב; from [H7126]; near (in place, kindred or time); allied, approach, at hand, [lexicon-key|+] any of kin, kinsfold(-sman), (that is) near (of kin), neighbour, (that is) next, (them that come) nigh (at hand), more ready, short(-ly).

  • near
    • of place
    • of time
    • of personal relationship
      • kinship
