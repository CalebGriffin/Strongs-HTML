H6879 צָרַע / צרע

tsâraʻ

(tsaw-rah' | tsaw-RA | tsa-RA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to scourge, i.e., (intransitive and figurative) to be stricken with leprosy; leper, leprous.

  • to be diseased of skin, be leprous
    • (Qal) to be a leper
    • (Pual) to have leprosy
