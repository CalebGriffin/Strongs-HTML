H2883 טָבַע / טבע

ṭâbaʻ

(taw-bah' | taw-BA | ta-VA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to sink; drown, fasten, settle, sink.

  • to sink, sink into, sink down, pierce, settle down, drown, be settled, be planted
    • (Qal) to sink, sink down
    • (Pual) to be sunk
    • (Hophal) to cause to sink
