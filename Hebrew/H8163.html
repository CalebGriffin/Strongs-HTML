H8163 שָׂעִיר / שעיר

sâʻîyr

(saw-eer' | saw-EER | sa-EER)

Definition

masculine noun; or שָׂעִר; from [H8175]; shaggy; as noun, a he-goat; by analogy, a faun; devil, goat, hairy, kid, rough, satyr.

  • hairy (adjective)
  • he-goat, buck (noun masculine)
    • as sacrificial animal
    • satyr, may refer to a demon possessed goat like the swine of Gadara ( Mat 8:30-32 )
