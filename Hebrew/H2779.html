H2779 חֹרֶף / חרף

chôreph

(kho'-ref | hoh-REP | hoh-REF)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H2778]; properly, the crop gathered, i.e., (by implication) the autumn (and winter) season; figuratively, ripeness of age; cold, winter(-house), youth.

  • harvest time, autumn
  • ([lexicon-key|CLBL]) winter
