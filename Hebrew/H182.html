H182 אוֹדוֹת / אודות

ʼôwdôwth

(o-doth' | oh-DOTE | oh-DOTE)

Definition

feminine noun; or (shortened) אֹדוֹת (only thus in the plural); from the same as [H181]; turnings (i.e., occasions); (adverb) on account of; (be-)cause, concerning, sake.

  • cause
    • cause, reason for
    • the occasion of
