H5720 עָדִין / עדין

ʻÂdîyn

(aw-deen' | aw-DEEN | ah-DEEN)

Definition

proper masculine noun; dainty, delicate; the same as [H5719]; Adin, the name of two Israelites; Adin.

  • ancestor of a family of exiles who returned with Zerubbabel
  • a chief among the returned exiles who signed the covenant with Nehemiah
