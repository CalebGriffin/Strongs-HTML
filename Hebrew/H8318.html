H8318 שֶׁרֶץ / שרץ

sherets

(sheh'-rets | sheh-RETS | sheh-RETS)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H8317]; a swarm, i.e., active mass of minute animals; creep(-ing thing), move(-ing creature).

  • teeming or swarming things, creepers, swarmers
    • of insects, animals, small reptiles, quadrupeds
