H5321 נַפְתָּלִי / נפתלי

Naphtâlîy

(naf-taw-lee' | nahp-taw-LEE | nahf-ta-LEE)

Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; wrestling; from [H6617]; my wrestling; Naphtali, a son of Jacob, with the tribe descended from him, and its territory; Naphtali.

  • the 6th son of Jacob and the 2nd by Bilhah the handmaid of Rachel (noun proper masculine)
  • the tribe descended from Naphtali the son of Jacob (noun proper masculine)
  • the territory assigned to the tribe of Naphtali (noun proper locative)
