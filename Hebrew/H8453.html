H8453 תּוֹשָׁב / תושב

tôwshâb

(to-shawb' | toh-SHAWB | toh-SHAHV)

Definition

masculine noun; or תֹּשָׁב; (1 Kings 17:1), from [H3427]; a dweller but not outlandish [H5237]; especially (as distinguished from a native citizen [active participle of H3427] and a temporary inmate [H1616] or mere lodger [H3885]) resident alien; foreigner, inhabitant, sojourner, stranger.

  • sojourner, stranger
