H5606 סָפַק / ספק

çâphaq

(saw-fak' | saw-PAHK | sa-FAHK)

Definition

verb; or שָׂפַק; (1 Kings 20:10; Job 27:23; Isaiah 2:6), a primitive root; to clap the hands (in token of compact, derision, grief, indignation, or punishment); by implication of satisfaction, to be enough; by implication of excess, to vomit; clap, smite, strike, suffice, wallow.

  • to clap, slap
    • (Qal)
      • to slap, clap
      • to slap, chastise
      • to splash, throw up
    • (Hiphil) to cause to clap
