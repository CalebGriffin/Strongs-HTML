H7643 שְׂבָם / שבם

Sᵉbâm

(seb-awm' | seh-BAWM | seh-VAHM)

Definition

proper locative noun; fragrance; or (feminine) שִׂבְמָה; probably from [H1313]; spice; Sebam or Sibmah, a place in Moab; Shebam, Shibmah, Sibmah.

  • one of the towns in the pastoral district on the east of the Jordan in Moab; allotted to the tribes of Reuben and Gad
