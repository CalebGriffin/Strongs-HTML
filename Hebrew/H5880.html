H5880 עֵין מִשְׁפָּט / עין משפט

ʻÊyn Mishpâṭ

(ane mish-pawt' | ane mish-PAWT | ane meesh-PAHT)

Definition

proper locative noun; spring of judgment; from [H5869] and [H4941]; fountain of judgment; En-Mishpat, a place near Palestine; En-mishpat.

  • a place near southern Palestine coincident or the same place as Kadesh
