H7918 שָׁכַךְ / שכך

shâkak

(shaw-kak' | shaw-KAHK | sha-HAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to weave (i.e., lay) a trap; figuratively, (through the idea of secreting) to allay (passions; physically, abate a flood); appease, assuage, make to cease, pacify, set.

  • to subside, abate, decrease
    • (Qal) to abate
    • (Hiphil) to allay
