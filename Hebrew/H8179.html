H8179 שַׁעַר / שער

shaʻar

(shah'-ar | sha-AR | sha-AR)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H8176] in its original sense; an opening, i.e., door or gate; city, door, gate, port, [lexicon-key|×] porter.

  • gate
    • gate (of entrance)
    • gate (of space inside gate, i.e. marketplace, public meeting place)
      • city, town
    • gate (of palace, royal castle, temple, court of tabernacle)
    • heaven
