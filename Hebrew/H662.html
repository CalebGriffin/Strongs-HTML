H662 אָפַק / אפק

ʼâphaq

(aw-fak' | aw-PAHK | ah-FAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to contain, i.e., (reflexive) abstain; force (oneself), restrain.

  • to hold, be strong, restrain, hold back
    • (Hithpael)
      • to restrain oneself, refrain
      • to force, compel oneself
