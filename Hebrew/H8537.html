H8537 תֹּם / תם

tôm

(tome | tome | tome)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H8552]; completeness; figuratively, prosperity; usually (morally) innocence; full, integrity, perfect(-ion), simplicity, upright(-ly, -ness), at a venture.

  • integrity, completeness
    • completeness, fulness
    • innocence, simplicity
    • integrity
