H6312 פּוּאָה / פואה

Pûwʼâh

(poo-aw' | poo-AW | poo-AH)

Definition

proper masculine noun; splendid; or פֻּוָּה; from [H6284]; a blast; Puah or Puvvah, the name of two Israelites; Phuvah, Pua, Puah.

  • son of Issachar
    • also 'Pua' and 'Phuvah'
  • man of Issachar, father of Tola, the judge of Israel after Abimelech
