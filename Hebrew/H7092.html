H7092 קָפַץ / קפץ

qâphats

(kaw-fats' | kaw-PAHTS | ka-FAHTS)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to draw together, i.e., close; by implication, to leap (by contracting the limbs); specifically, to die (from gathering up the feet); shut (up), skip, stop, take out of the way.

  • to draw together, close, shut, shut up, stop up
    • (Qal) to shut
    • (Niphal)
      • to draw oneself together (of death)
      • to be shut up to death
    • (Piel) springing, skipping (participle)
