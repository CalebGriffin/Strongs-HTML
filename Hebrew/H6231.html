H6231 עָשַׁק / עשק

ʻâshaq

(aw-shak' | aw-SHAHK | ah-SHAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (compare [H6229]); to press upon, i.e., oppress, defraud, violate, overflow; get deceitfully, deceive, defraud, drink up, (use) oppress(-ion, -or), do violence (wrong).

  • to press upon, oppress, violate, defraud, do violence, get deceitfully, wrong, extort
    • (Qal)
      • to oppress, wrong, extort
      • to oppress
    • (Pual) to be exploited, be crushed
