H805 אֲשׁוּרִי / אשורי

ʼĂshûwrîy

(ash-oo-ree' | uh-shoo-REE | uh-shoo-REE)

Definition

adjective, proper masculine noun; guided, blessed, steps; or אַשּׁוּרִי; from a patrial word of the same form as [H804]; an Ashurite (collectively) or inhabitant of Ashur, a district in Palestine; Asshurim, Ashurites.

  • a tribe inhabiting the plain of Esdraelon
  • a tribe descended from Abraham and Keturah
