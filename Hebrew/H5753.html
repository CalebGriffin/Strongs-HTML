H5753 עָוָה / עוה

ʻâvâh

(aw-vaw' | aw-WAW | ah-VA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to crook, literally or figuratively; do amiss, bow down, make crooked, commit iniquity, pervert, (do) perverse(-ly), trouble, [lexicon-key|×] turn, do wickedly, do wrong.

  • to bend, twist, distort
    • (Niphal) to be bent, be bowed down, be twisted, be perverted
    • (Piel) to twist, distort
    • (Hiphil) to do perversely
  • to commit iniquity, do wrong, pervert
    • (Qal) to do wrong, commit iniquity
    • (Hiphil) to commit iniquity
