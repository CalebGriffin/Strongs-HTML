H727 אָרוֹן / ארון

ʼârôwn

(aw-rone' | aw-RONE | ah-RONE)

Definition

masculine noun; or אָרֹן; from [H717] (in the sense of gathering); a box; ark, chest, coffin.

  • chest, ark
    • money chest
    • Ark of the Covenant
  • ([lexicon-key|TWOT])coffin
