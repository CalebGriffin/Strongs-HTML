H6926 קִדְמָה / קדמה

qidmâh

(kid-maw' | kid-MAW | keed-MA)

Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [H6924]; the forward part (or relatively) East (often adverbially, on the east or in front); east(-ward).

  • forward, front, east, antiquity, East
    • in front of, over against, front
    • on the east of, east
