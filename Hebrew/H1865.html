H1865 דְּרוֹר / דרור

dᵉrôwr

(der-ore' | deh-RORE | deh-RORE)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root (meaning to move rapidly); freedom; hence, spontaneity of outflow, and so clear; liberty, pure.

  • a flowing, free run, liberty
    • flowing (of myrrh)
    • liberty
