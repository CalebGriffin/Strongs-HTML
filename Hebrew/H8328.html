H8328 שֶׁרֶשׁ / שרש

sheresh

(sheh'-resh | sheh-RESH | sheh-RESH)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H8327]; a root (literally or figuratively); bottom, deep, heel, root.

  • root
    • root (literal)
    • root (of people involving firmness or permanence) (figuratively)
    • root, bottom (as lowest stratum) (figuratively)
