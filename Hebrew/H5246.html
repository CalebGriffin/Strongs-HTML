H5246 נָמֵר / נמר

nâmêr

(naw-mare' | naw-MARE | na-MARE)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning properly, to filtrate, i.e., be limpid (compare [H5247] and [H5249]); and thus to spot or stain as if by dripping; a leopard (from its stripes); leopard.
