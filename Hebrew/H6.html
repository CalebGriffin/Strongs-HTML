H6 אָבַד / אבד

ʼâbad

(aw-bad' | aw-BAHD | ah-VAHD)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to wander away, i.e., lose oneself; by implication to perish (causative, destroy); break, destroy, destruction, [lexicon-key|+] not escape, fail, lose, (cause to, make) perish, spend, [lexicon-key|×] and surely, take, be undone, [lexicon-key|×] utterly, be void of, have no way to flee.

  • perish, vanish, go astray, be destroyed
    • (Qal)
      • perish, die, be exterminated
      • perish, vanish (figuratively)
      • be lost, strayed
    • (Piel)
      • to destroy, kill, cause to perish, to give up
      • to blot out, do away with, cause to vanish, (figuratively)
      • cause to stray, lose
    • (Hiphil)
      • to destroy, put to death
        • of divine judgment
      • object name of kings (figuratively)
