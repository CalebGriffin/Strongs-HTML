H8064 שָׁמַיִם / שמים

shâmayim

(shaw-mah'-yim | shaw-ma-YIM | sha-ma-YEEM)

Definition

masculine noun; dual of an unused singular שָׁמֶה; from an unused root meaning to be lofty; the sky (as aloft; the dual perhaps alluding to the visible arch in which the clouds move, as well as to the higher ether where the celestial bodies revolve); air, [lexicon-key|×] astrologer, heaven(-s).

  • heaven, heavens, sky
    • visible heavens, sky
      • as abode of the stars
      • as the visible universe, the sky, atmosphere, etc
    • Heaven (as the abode of God)
