H7641 שִׁבֹּל / שבל

shibbôl

(shib-bole | shib-BOLE | shee-BOLE)

Definition

feminine noun; or (feminine) שִׁבֹּלֶת; from the same as [H7640]; a stream (as flowing); also an ear of grain (as growing out); by analogy, a branch; branch, channel, ear (of corn), (water-)flood, Shibboleth.

  • flowing stream
  • ear (of grain), head of grain
    • as growing
    • cluster

Note

Compare [H5451].
