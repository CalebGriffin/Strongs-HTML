H8057 שִׂמְחָה / שמחה

simchâh

(sim-khaw' | sim-HAW | seem-HA)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H8056]; blithesomeness or glee, (religious or festival); [lexicon-key|×] exceeding(-ly), gladness, joy(-fulness), mirth, pleasure, rejoice(-ing).

  • joy, mirth, gladness
    • mirth, gladness, joy, gaiety, pleasure
    • joy (of God)
    • glad result, happy issue
