H7617 שָׁבָה / שבה

shâbâh

(shaw-baw' | shaw-BAW | sha-VA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to transport into captivity; (bring away, carry, carry away, lead, lead away, take) captive(-s), drive (take) away.

  • to take captive
    • (Qal)
      • to take captive
      • captive (participle)
      • to lead captive
    • (Niphal) to be taken captive
