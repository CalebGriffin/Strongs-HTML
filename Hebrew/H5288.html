H5288 נַעַר / נער

naʻar

(nah'-ar | na-AR | na-AR)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H5287]; (concretely) a boy (as active), from the age of infancy to adolescence; by implication, a servant; also (by interch. of sex), a girl (of similar latitude in age); babe, boy, child, damsel (from the margin), lad, servant, young (man).

  • a boy, lad, servant, youth, retainer
    • boy, lad, youth
    • servant, retainer
