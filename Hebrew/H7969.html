H7969 שָׁלוֹשׁ / שלוש

shâlôwsh

(shaw-loshe' | shaw-LOHSH | sha-LOHSH)

Definition

noun; or שָׁלֹשׁ; masculine שְׁלוֹשָׁה; or שְׁלֹשָׁה; a primitive number; three; occasionally (ordinal) third, or (multiple) thrice; [lexicon-key|+] fork, [lexicon-key|+] often(-times), third, thir(-teen, -teenth), three, [lexicon-key|+] thrice.

  • three, triad
    • 3, 300, third
