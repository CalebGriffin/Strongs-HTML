H6937 קָדַר / קדר

qâdar

(kaw-dar' | kaw-DAHR | ka-DAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be ashy, i.e., dark-colored; by implication, to mourn (in sackcloth or sordid garments); be black(-ish), be (make) dark(-en), [lexicon-key|×] heavily, (cause to) mourn.

  • to mourn, be dark
    • (Qal) to be dark
    • (Hiphil)
      • to darken
      • to cause to mourn
    • (Hithpael) to grow dark
