H174 אֲהָלִים / אהלים

ʼăhâlîym

(a-haw-leem' | uh-haw-LEEM | uh-ha-LEEM)

Definition

masculine noun; or (feminine) אֲהָלוֹת (only used thus in the plural); of foreign origin; aloe wood (i.e., sticks); (tree of lign-)aloes.

  • aloes, aloe tree
    • aloe tree
    • aloe (perfume)
