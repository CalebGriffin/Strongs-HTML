H6019 עַמְרָם / עמרם

ʻAmrâm

(am-rawm' | am-RAWM | am-RAHM)

Definition

proper masculine noun; exalted people; probably from [H5971] and [H7311]; high people; Amram, the name of two Israelites; Amram.

  • a descendant of Kohath and Levi and father of Moses
  • one of the sons of Bani who had a foreign wife in the time of Ezra
