H6932 קְדֵמוֹת / קדמות

Qᵉdêmôwth

(ked-ay-mothe' | keh-day-MOTE | keh-day-MOTE)

Definition

proper locative noun; eastern; from [H6923]; beginnings; Kedemoth, a place in eastern Palestine; Kedemoth.

  • one of the towns in the district east of the Dead Sea allotted to the tribe of Reuben and given to the Merarite Levites
