H72 אֶבֶן הָעֵזֶר / אבן העזר

ʼEben hâ-ʻÊzer

(eh'-ben haw-e'-zer | eh-BEN haw-ay-DZER | eh-VEN ha-ay-ZER)

Definition

noun; stone of help; from [H68] and [H5828] with the article inserted; stone of the help; Eben-ha-Ezer, a place in Palestine; Ebenezer.

  • memorial stone erected by Samuel to mark where God helped Israel to defeat the Philistines - north of Jerusalem
