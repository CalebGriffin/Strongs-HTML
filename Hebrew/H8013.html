H8013 שְׁלֹמוֹת / שלמות

Shᵉlômôwth

(shel-o-moth' | sheh-loh-MOTE | sheh-loh-MOTE)

Definition

proper masculine noun; peaceful; feminine plural of [H7965]; pacifications; Shelomoth, the name of two Israelites; Shelomith (from the margin), Shelomoth.

  • a Gershonite Levite, son of Shimei in the time of David
  • a Kohathite Levite of the sons of Izhar in the time of David
  • a Gershonite Levite, son of Zichri in the time of David

Note

Compare [H8019].
