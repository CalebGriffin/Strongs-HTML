H8398 תֵּבֵל / תבל

têbêl

(tay-bale' | tay-BALE | tay-VALE)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H2986]; the earth (as moist and therefore inhabited); by extension, the globe; by implication, its inhabitants; specifically, a particular land, as Babylonia, Palestine; habitable part, world.

  • world
