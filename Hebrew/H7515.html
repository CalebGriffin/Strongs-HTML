H7515 רָפַשׂ / רפש

râphas

(raw-fas' | raw-PAHS | ra-FAHS)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to trample, i.e., roil water; foul, trouble.

  • to stamp, tread, foul by stamping or treading
    • (Qal) to tread down, foul by treading, foul with the feet
    • (Niphal) to be fouled
