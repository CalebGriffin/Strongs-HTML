H7857 שָׁטַף / שטף

shâṭaph

(shaw-taf' | shaw-TAHP | sha-TAHF)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to gush; by implication, to inundate, cleanse; by analogy, to gallop, conquer; drown, (over-)flow, overwhelmed, rinse, run, rush, (throughly) wash (away).

  • to wash, rinse, overflow, engulf, rinse or wash off
    • (Qal)
      • to overflow
      • to flow, run
      • to rinse or wash off
    • (Niphal) to be swept away, be rinsed out
    • (Pual) to be rinsed, be scoured and rinsed
