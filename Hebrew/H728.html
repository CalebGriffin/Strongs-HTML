H728 אֲרַוְנָה / ארונה

ʼĂravnâh

(ar-av-naw' | uh-ra-NAW | uh-rahv-NA)

Definition

proper masculine noun; I shall shout for joy, make ye to shine, joyful shouting of Jah (Jehovah); or (by transposition) אוֹרְנָה; or אַרְנִיָּה; all by orthographical variation for [H771]; Aravnah (or Arnijah or Ornah), a Jebusite; Araunah.

  • a Jebusite who sold David the site for an altar
