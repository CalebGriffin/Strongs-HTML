H1234

bâqaʻ

(baw-kah' | baw-KA | ba-KA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to cleave; generally, to rend, break, rip or open; make a breach, break forth (into, out, in pieces, through, up), be ready to burst, cleave (asunder), cut out, divide, hatch, rend (asunder), rip up, tear, win.

  • to split, cleave, break open, divide, break through, rip up, break up, tear
    • (Qal)
      • to cleave, cleave open
      • to break through, break into
    • (Niphal)
      • to be cleft, be rent open, be split open
      • to be broken into
    • (Piel)
      • to cleave, cut to pieces, rend open
      • to break through, break down
    • (Pual)
      • to be ripped open, be torn open
      • to be rent
      • to be broken into
    • (Hiphil)
      • to break into
      • to break through
    • (Hophal) to be broken into
    • (Hithpael) to burst (themselves) open, cleave asunder
