H7324 רוּק / רוק

rûwq

(rook | rook | rook)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to pour out (literally or figuratively), i.e., empty; [lexicon-key|×] arm, cast out, draw (out), (make) empty, pour forth (out).

  • to make empty, empty out
    • (Hiphil)
      • to empty, keep empty or hungry
      • to pour out or down
      • to empty out
    • (Hophal) to be emptied out
