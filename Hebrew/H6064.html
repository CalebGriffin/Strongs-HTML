H6064 עָנַשׁ / ענש

ʻânash

(aw-nash' | aw-NAHSH | ah-NAHSH)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to urge; by implication, to inflict a penalty, specifically, to fine; amerce, condemn, punish, [lexicon-key|×] surely.

  • to fine, amerce, punish, condemn, mulct
    • (Qal) to fine, punish
    • (Niphal) to be fined, be punished, be mulcted
