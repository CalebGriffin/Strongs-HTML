H8556 תִּמְנַת חֶרֶס / תמנת חרס

Timnath Chereç

(tim-nath kheh'-res | tim-NAHT heh-RES | teem-NAHT heh-RES)

Definition

proper locative noun; portion of the sun, abundant portion; or תִּמְנַת סֶרַח; from [H8553] and [H2775]; portion of (the) sun; Timnath-Cheres, a place in Palestine; Timnath-heres, Timnath-serah.

  • a city given to Joshua as a inheritance in the mountains of Ephraim on the north of the hill of Gaash where he was buried
