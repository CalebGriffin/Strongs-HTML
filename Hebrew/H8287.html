H8287 שָׁרוּחֶן / שרוחן

Shârûwchen

(shaw-roo-khen' | shaw-roo-HEN | sha-roo-HEN)

Definition

proper locative noun; refuge of grace; probably from [H8281] (in the sense of dwelling (compare [H8271]) and [H2580]; abode of pleasure; Sharuchen, a place in Palestine; Sharuhen.

  • a city in Judah allotted to Simeon
