H7759 שׁוּלַמִּית / שולמית

Shûwlammîyth

(shoo-lam-meeth' | shoo-lahm-MEET | shoo-la-MEET)

Definition

adjective; the perfect, the peaceful; from [H7999]; peaceful (with the article always prefixed, making it a pet name); the Shulammith, an epithet of Solomon's queen; Shulamite.

  • the heroine lover of the Song of Songs (of Solomon)
