H5761 עַוִּים / עוים

ʻAvvîym

(av-veem' | ah-WEEM | ah-WEEM)

Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; ruins; plural of [H5757]; Avvim (as inhabited by Avvites), a place in Palestine (with the article prefix); Avim.

  • a people among the early inhabitants of Palestine located in the southwest corner of the seacoast (noun proper masculine)
  • a city in Benjamin (noun proper locative)
