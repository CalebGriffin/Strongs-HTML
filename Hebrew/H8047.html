H8047 שַׁמָּה / שמה

shammâh

(sham-maw' | shahm-MAW | sha-MA)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H8074]; ruin; by implication, consternation; astonishment, desolate(-ion), waste, wonderful thing.

  • waste, horror, appalment
    • a waste (of land, city, etc)
    • appalment, horror
