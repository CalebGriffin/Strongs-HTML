H645 אֵפוֹ / אפו

ʼêphôw

(ay-fo' | ay-POH | ay-FOH)

Definition

particle of speech; or אֵפוֹא; from [H6311]; strictly a demonstrative particle, here; but used of time, now or then; here, now, where?.

  • then, now, so
    • (who) then, (what) then (with interrogative)
    • then (with imperative - i.e. know then)
    • if...then (with adverb)
