H6737 צָיַר / ציר

tsâyar

(tsaw-yar' | tsaw-YAHR | tsa-YAHR)

Definition

verb; a denominative from [H6735] in the sense of ambassador; to make an errand, i.e., betake oneself; make as if...had been ambassador.

  • ([lexicon-key|BDB]) (Hithpael) to supply oneself with provisions, take as one's provision
  • ([lexicon-key|CLBL]) to act as envoy
