H6621 פֶּתַע / פתע

pethaʻ

(peh'-thah | peh-TA | peh-TA)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning to open (the eyes); (compare [H6597]); a wink, i.e., moment (used only [with or without preposition] adverbially, quickly or unexpectedly); at an instant, suddenly, [lexicon-key|×] very.

  • suddenness, in an instant
