H7704 שָׂדֶה / שדה

sâdeh

(saw-deh' | saw-DEH | sa-DEH)

Definition

masculine noun; or שָׂדַי; from an unused root meaning to spread out; a field (as flat); country, field, ground, land, soil, [lexicon-key|×] wild.

  • field, land
    • cultivated field
    • of home of wild beasts
    • plain (opposed to mountain)
    • land (opposed to sea)
