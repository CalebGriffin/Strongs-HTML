H5780 עוּץ / עוץ

ʻÛwts

(oots | oots | oots)

Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; wooded; apparently from [H5779]; consultation; Uts, a son of Aram, also a Seirite, and the regions settled by them; Uz.

  • son of Aram and grandson of Seth (noun proper masculine)
  • son of Nahor by Milcah (noun proper masculine)
  • an Edomite, son of Dishan and grandson of Seir (noun proper masculine)
  • the country of Job; probably east and southeast of Palestine somewhere in the Arabian desert (noun proper locative)
