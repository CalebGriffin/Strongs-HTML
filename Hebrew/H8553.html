H8553 תִּמְנָה / תמנה

Timnâh

(tim-naw' | tim-NAW | teem-NA)

Definition

proper locative noun; portion; from [H4487]; a portion assigned; Timnah, the name of two places in Palestine; Timnah, Timnath, Thimnathah.

  • a town on the northern boundary of Judah later assigned to Dan
  • a town in the hill country of Judah
