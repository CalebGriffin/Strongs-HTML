H6121 עָקֹב / עקב

ʻâqôb

(aw-kobe' | aw-KOBE | ah-KOVE)

Definition

adjective; from [H6117]; in the original sense, a knoll (as swelling up); in the denominative sense; (transitive) fraudulent or (intransitive) tracked; crooked, deceitful, polluted.

  • deceitful, sly, insidious
    • deceitful, sly, insidious, slippery
    • foot-tracked
  • steep, hilly
