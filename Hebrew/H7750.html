H7750 שׂוּט / שוט

sûwṭ

(soot | soot | soot)

Definition

verb; or (by permutation) סוּט; a primitive root; to detrude, i.e., (intransitively and figuratively) become derelict (wrongly practise; namely, idolatry); turn aside to.

  • to swerve, fall away
    • (Qal)
      • to swerve, fall away
      • those falling away (participle)
