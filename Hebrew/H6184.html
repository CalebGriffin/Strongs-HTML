H6184 עָרִיץ / עריץ

ʻârîyts

(aw-reets' | aw-REETS | ah-REETS)

Definition

adjective; from [H6206]; fearful, i.e., powerful or tyrannical; mighty, oppressor, in great power, strong, terrible, violent.

  • awe-inspiring, terror-striking, awesome, terrifying, ruthless, mighty
