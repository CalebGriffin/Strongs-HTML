H3607 כָּלָא / כלא

kâlâʼ

(kaw-law' | kaw-LAW | ka-LA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to restrict, by act (hold back or in) or word (prohibit); finish, forbid, keep (back), refrain, restrain, retain, shut up, be stayed, withhold.

  • to restrict, restrain, withhold, shut up, keep back, refrain, forbid
    • (Qal)
      • to shut up
      • to restrain
      • to withhold
    • (Niphal) to be restrained
    • (Piel) to finish
