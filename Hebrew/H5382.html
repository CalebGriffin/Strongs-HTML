H5382 נָשָׁה / נשה

nâshâh

(naw-shaw' | naw-SHAW | na-SHA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to forget; figuratively, to neglect; causatively, to remit, remove; forget, deprive, exact.

  • to forget, deprive
    • (Qal) to forget
    • (Niphal) to be forgotten
    • (Piel) to cause to forget
    • (Hiphil) to cause to forget, allow to be forgotten
