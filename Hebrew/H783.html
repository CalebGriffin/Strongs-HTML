H783 אַרְתַּחְשַׁשְׁתָּא / ארתחששתא

ʼArtachshashtâʼ

(ar-takh-shash-taw' | ar-ta-shahsh-TAW | ar-tahk-shahsh-TA)

Definition

proper masculine noun; I will make the spoiled to boil, I will stir myself (in) winter; or אַרְתַּחְשַׁשְׁתְּא; or by permutation אַרְתַּחְשַׁסְתְּא; of foreign origin; Artachshasta (or Artaxerxes), a title (rather than name) of several Persian kings; Artaxerxes.

  • son and successor of Xerxes as emperor of Persia, 465-424 BC
