H6339 פָּזַז / פזז

pâzaz

(paw-zaz' | paw-DZAHDZ | pa-ZAHZ)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (identical with [H6338]); to solidify (as if by refining); also to spring (as if separating the limbs); leap, be made strong.

  • to bound, be agile, be supple
    • (Qal) to be nimble
    • (Piel) to leap, show agility
