H4529 מָסָה / מסה

mâçâh

(maw-saw' | maw-SAW | ma-SA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to dissolve; make to consume away, (make to) melt, water.

  • to melt, dissolve, be liquefied
    • (Hiphil)
      • to melt, cause to dissolve, consume, cause to vanish
      • to intimidate (figuratively)
