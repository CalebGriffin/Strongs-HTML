H8274 שֵׁרֵבְיָה / שרביה

Shêrêbᵉyâh

(shay-rayb-yaw' | shay-ray-beh-YAW | shay-ray-veh-YA)

Definition

proper masculine noun; Jehovah has scorched; from [H8273] and [H3050]; Jah has brought heat; Sherebjah, the name of two Israelites; Sherebiah.

  • a Levite who assisted Ezra at the reading of the Law and who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
