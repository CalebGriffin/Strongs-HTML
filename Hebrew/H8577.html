H8577 תַּנִּין / תנין

tannîyn

(tan-neen' | tahn-NEEN | ta-NEEN)

Definition

masculine noun; or תַּנִּים; (Ezekiel 29:3), intensive from the same as [H8565]; a marine or land monster, i.e., sea-serpent or jackal; dragon, sea-monster, serpent, whale.

  • dragon, serpent, sea monster
    • dragon or dinosaur
    • sea or river monster
    • serpent, venomous snake
