H884 בְּאֵר שֶׁבַע / באר שבע

Bᵉʼêr Shebaʻ

(be-ayr' sheh'-bah | beh-ARE sheh-BA | beh-ARE sheh-VA)

Definition

proper locative noun; well of the sevenfold oath; from [H875] and [H7651] (in the sense of [H7650]); well of an oath; Beer-Sheba, a place in Palestine; Beer-shebah.

  • a city at the south edge of Israel
