H7639 שְׂבָכָה / שבכה

sᵉbâkâh

(seb-aw-kaw' | seh-baw-KAW | seh-va-HA)

Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [H7638]; a net-work, i.e., (in hunting) a snare, (in architecture) a ballustrade; also a reticulated ornament to a pillar; checker, lattice, network, snare, wreath(-enwork).

  • network, lattice-work, net, netting
    • lattice
    • net-ornament (on pillars)
    • network, toils (for catching animals)
