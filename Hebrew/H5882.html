H5882 עֵין עֶגְלַיִם / עין עגלים

ʻÊyn ʻEglayim

(ane eg-lah'-yim | ane eɡ-la-YIM | ane eɡ-la-YEEM)

Definition

proper locative noun; fountain of the two calves; עַיִן and the dual of [H5695]; fountain of two calves; En-Eglajim, a place in Palestine; En-eglaim.

  • a place on the Dead Sea; site uncertain
