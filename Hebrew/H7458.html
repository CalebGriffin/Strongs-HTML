H7458 רָעָב / רעב

râʻâb

(raw-awb' | raw-AWB | ra-AV)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H7456]; hunger (more or less extensive); dearth, famine, [lexicon-key|+] famished, hunger.

  • famine, hunger
    • famine (in land, nation)
      • of Jehovah's word (figuratively)
    • hunger (of individuals)
