H2318 חָדַשׁ / חדש

châdash

(khaw-dash' | haw-DAHSH | ha-DAHSH)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be new; causatively, to rebuild; renew, repair.

  • to be new, renew, repair
    • (Piel)
      • to renew, make anew
      • to repair
    • (Hithpael) to renew oneself
