H2781 חֶרְפָּה / חרפה

cherpâh

(kher-paw' | her-PAW | her-PA)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H2778]; contumely, disgrace, the pudenda; rebuke, reproach(-fully), shame.

  • reproach, scorn
    • taunt, scorn (upon enemy)
    • reproach (resting upon condition of shame, disgrace)
    • a reproach (an object)
