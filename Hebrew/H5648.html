H5648 עֲבַד / עבד

ʻăbad

(ab-bad' | uh-BAHD | uh-VAHD)

Definition

verb; corresponding to [H5647]; to do, make, prepare, keep, etc; [lexicon-key|×] cut, do, execute, go on, make, move, work.

  • to make, do
    • (Peal)
      • to make, create
      • to do, perform
    • (Ithpeal)
      • to be made into
      • to be done, be wrought, be performed, be executed, be carried out
