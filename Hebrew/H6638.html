H6638 צָבָה / צבה

tsâbâh

(tsaw-baw' | tsaw-BAW | tsa-VA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to amass, i.e., grow turgid; specifically, to array an army against; fight swell.

  • to swell, swell up
    • (Qal) to swell (of adulteress' belly)
    • (Hiphil) to cause to swell (of adulteress' belly)
