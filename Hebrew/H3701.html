H3701 כֶּסֶף / כסף

keçeph

(keh'-sef | keh-SEP | keh-SEF)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H3700]; silver (from its pale color); by implication, money; money, price, silver(-ling).

  • silver, money
    • silver
      • as metal
      • as ornament
      • as colour
    • money, shekels, talents
