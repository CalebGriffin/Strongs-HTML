H7789 שׁוּר / שור

shûwr

(shoor | shoor | shoor)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (identical with through the idea of going round for inspection); to spy out, i.e., (generally) survey, (for evil) lurk for, (for good) care for; behold, lay wait, look, observe, perceive, regard, see.

  • to see, behold, observe, regard
    • (Qal)
      • to behold, observe
      • to regard
      • to watch, watch (stealthily), lie in wait
