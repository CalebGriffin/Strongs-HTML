H67 אָבֵל מִצְרַיִם / אבל מצרים

ʼÂbêl Mitsrayim

(aw-bale' mits-rah'-yim | aw-BALE mits-ra-YIM | ah-VALE meets-ra-YEEM)

Definition

proper locative noun; meadow of Egypt; from [H58] and [H4714]; meadow of Egypt; Abel-Mitsrajim, a place in Palestine; Abel-mizraim.

  • a place east of the Jordan (perhaps "as fertile as Egypt")
