H7677 שַׁבָּתוֹן / שבתון

shabbâthôwn

(shab-baw-thone' | shahb-baw-TONE | sha-ba-TONE)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H7676]; a sabbatism or special holiday; rest, sabbath.

  • Sabbath observance, sabbatism
    • of weekly sabbath
    • day of atonement
    • sabbatical year
    • of Feast of Trumpets
    • of the 1st and last days of the Feast of Tabernacles
