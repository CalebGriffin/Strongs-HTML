H868 אֶתְנַן / אתנן

ʼethnan

(eth-nan' | et-NAHN | et-NAHN)

Definition

masculine noun; the same as [H866]; a gift (as the price of harlotry or idolatry); hire, reward.

  • hire of prostitute, price
    • hire (of harlot)
    • of idolatrous Israel, Jerusalem, Tyre (figuratively)
