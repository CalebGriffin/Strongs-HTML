H8259 שָׁקַף / שקף

shâqaph

(shaw-kaf' | shaw-KAHP | sha-KAHF)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to lean out (of a window), i.e., (by implication) peep or gaze (passively, be a spectacle); appear, look (down, forth, out).

  • to overlook, look down or out, overhang, look out and down
    • (Niphal) to lean over (and look), look down
    • (Hiphil) to look down, look down upon
