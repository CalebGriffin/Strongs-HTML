H6634 צְבָא / צבא

tsᵉbâʼ

(tseb-aw' | tseh-BAW | tseh-VA)

Definition

verb; corresponding to [H6623] in the figurative sense of summoning one's wishes; to please; will, would.

  • to desire, be inclined, be willing, be pleased
    • (Peal)
      • to desire
      • to be pleased
      • to will (without hindrance) (of God)
