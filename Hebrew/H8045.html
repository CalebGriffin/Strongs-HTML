H8045 שָׁמַד / שמד

shâmad

(shaw-mad' | shaw-MAHD | sha-MAHD)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to desolate; destory(-uction), bring to nought, overthrow, perish, pluck down, [lexicon-key|×] utterly.

  • to destroy, exterminate, be destroyed, be exterminated
    • (Niphal)
      • to be annihilated, be exterminated
      • to be destroyed, be devastated
    • (Hiphil)
      • to annihilate, exterminate
      • to destroy
