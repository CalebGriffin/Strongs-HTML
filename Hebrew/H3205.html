H3205 יָלַד / ילד

yâlad

(yaw-lad' | yaw-LAHD | ya-LAHD)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to bear young; causatively, to beget; medically, to act as midwife; specifically, to show lineage; bear, beget, birth(-day), born, (make to) bring forth (children, young), bring up, calve, child, come, be delivered (of a child), time of delivery, gender, hatch, labour, (do the office of a) midwife, declare pedigrees, be the son of, (woman in, woman that) travail(-eth, -ing woman).

  • to bear, bring forth, beget, gender, travail
    • (Qal)
      • to bear, bring forth
        • of child birth
        • of distress (simile)
        • of wicked (behaviour)
      • to beget
    • (Niphal) to be born
    • (Piel)
      • to cause or help to bring forth
      • to assist or tend as a midwife
      • midwife (participle)
    • (Pual) to be born
    • (Hiphil)
      • to beget (a child)
      • to bear (figuratively - of wicked bringing forth iniquity)
    • (Hophal) day of birth, birthday (infinitive)
    • (Hithpael) to declare one's birth (pedigree)
