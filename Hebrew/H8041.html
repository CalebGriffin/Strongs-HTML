H8041 שָׂמַאל / שמאל

sâmaʼl

(saw-mal' | saw-MAHL | sa-MAHL)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (denominative from [H8040]); to use the left hand or pass in that direction; (go, turn) (on the, to the) left.

  • to take the left, go to the left
    • (Hiphil)
      • to go to the left
      • to turn (aside) to the left (figuratively)
      • to use the left hand
