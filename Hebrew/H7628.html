H7628 שְׁבִי / שבי

shᵉbîy

(sheb-ee' | sheh-BEE | sheh-VEE)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H7618]; exiled; captured; as noun, exile (abstractly or concretely and collectively); by extension, booty; captive(-ity), prisoners, [lexicon-key|×] take away, that was taken.

  • captivity, captives (noun masculine)
    • (state of) captivity
    • (act of) capture
    • captives
  • captive (noun feminine)
