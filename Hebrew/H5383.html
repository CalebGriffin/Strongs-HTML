H5383 נָשָׁה / נשה

nâshâh

(naw-shaw' | naw-SHAW | na-SHA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (rather identical with [H5382], in the sense of [H5378]); to lend or (by reciprocity) borrow on security or interest; creditor, exact, extortioner, lend, usurer, lend on (taker on) usury.

  • to lend, be a creditor
    • (Qal)
      • to lend
      • creditor (participle) (substantive)
    • (Hiphil) to lend
