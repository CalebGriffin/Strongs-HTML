H7551 רָקַם / רקם

râqam

(raw-kam' | raw-KAHM | ra-KAHM)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to variegate color, i.e., embroider; by implication, to fabricate; embroiderer, needlework, curiously work.

  • to variegate, mix colours
    • (Qal) variegator (worker in colours) (participle)
    • (Pual) to be skilfully wrought or woven
