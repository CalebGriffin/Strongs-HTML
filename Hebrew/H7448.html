H7448 רֶסֶן / רסן

reçen

(reh'-sen | reh-SEN | reh-SEN)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning to curb; a halter (as restraining); by implication, the jaw; bridle.

  • something that restrains, halter, jaw
    • halter
      • of restraint thrown off (figuratively)
    • jaw (of crocodile)
