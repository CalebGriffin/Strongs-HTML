H3960 לָשַׁן / לשן

lâshan

(law-shan' | law-SHAHN | la-SHAHN)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to lick; but used only as a denominative from [H3956]; to wag the tongue, i.e., to calumniate; accuse, slander.

  • to use the tongue, slander
    • (Poel) to slander
    • (Piel) to slander
    • (Hiphil) to accuse
