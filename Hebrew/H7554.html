H7554 רָקַע / רקע

râqaʻ

(raw-kah' | raw-KA | ra-KA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to pound the earth (as a sign of passion); by analogy to expand (by hammering); by implication, to overlay (with thin sheets of metal); beat, make broad, spread abroad (forth, over, out, into plates), stamp, stretch.

  • to beat, stamp, beat out, spread out, stretch
    • (Qal)
      • to stamp, beat out
      • one who beats out (participle)
    • (Piel) to overlay, beat out (for plating)
    • (Pual) beaten out (participle)
    • (Hiphil) to make a spreading (of clouds)
