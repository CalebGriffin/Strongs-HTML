H227 אָז / אז

ʼâz

(awz | awdz | az)

Definition

adverb; a demonstrative adverb; at that time or place; also as a conjunction, therefore; beginning, for, from, hitherto, now, of old, once, since, then, at which time, yet.

  • then, at that time
    • temporal expressions
      • then (past)
      • then, if...then (future)
      • earlier
    • logical expressions
      • in that case
      • that (being so)
