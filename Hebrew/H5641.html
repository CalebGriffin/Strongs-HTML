H5641 סָתַר / סתר

çâthar

(saw-thar' | saw-TAHR | sa-TAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to hide (by covering), literally or figuratively; be absent, keep close, conceal, hide (self), (keep) secret, [lexicon-key|×] surely.

  • to hide, conceal
    • (Niphal)
      • to hide oneself
      • to be hidden, be concealed
    • (Piel) to hide carefully
    • (Pual) to be hidden carefully, be concealed
    • (Hiphil) to conceal, hide
    • (Hithpael) to hide oneself carefully
