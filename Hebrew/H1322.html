H1322 בֹּשֶׁת / בשת

bôsheth

(bo'-sheth | boh-SHET | boh-SHET)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H954]; shame (the feeling and the condition, as well as its cause); by implication (specifically) an idol; ashamed, confusion, [lexicon-key|+] greatly, (put to) shame(-ful thing).
