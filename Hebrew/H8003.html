H8003 שָׁלֵם / שלם

shâlêm

(shaw-lame' | shaw-LAME | sha-LAME)

Definition

adjective; from [H7999]; complete (literally or figuratively); especially friendly; full, just, made ready, peaceable, perfect(-ed), quiet, Shalem (by mistake for a name), whole.

  • complete, safe, peaceful, perfect, whole, full, at peace
    • complete
      • full, perfect
      • finished
    • safe, unharmed
    • peace (of covenant of peace, mind)
      • perfect, complete (of keeping covenant relation)
