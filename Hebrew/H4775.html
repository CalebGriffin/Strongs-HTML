H4775 מָרַד / מרד

mârad

(maw-rad' | maw-RAHD | ma-RAHD)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to rebel; rebel(-lious).

  • to rebel, revolt, be rebellious
    • (Qal) to rebel, revolt
      • against human king
      • against God
      • against light (poetic)
