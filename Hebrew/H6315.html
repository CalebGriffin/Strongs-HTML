H6315 פּוּחַ / פוח

pûwach

(poo'akh | POO-ah | POO-ak)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to puff, i.e., blow with the breath or air; hence, to fan (as a breeze), to utter, to kindle (a fire), to scoff; blow (upon), break, puff, bring into a snare, speak, utter.

  • to breathe, blow
    • (Qal) to breathe
    • (Hiphil)
      • to cause to exhale or breathe
      • to puff, snort
      • to excite, inflame
      • to puff, pant for it
      • to breathe out, utter
      • to blow, blast
