H520 אַמָּה / אמה

ʼammâh

(am-maw' | am-MAW | ah-MA)

Definition

feminine noun; prolonged from [H517]; properly, a mother (i.e., unit of measure, or the fore-arm (below the elbow), i.e., a cubit; also a door-base (as a bond of the entrance); cubit, [lexicon-key|+] hundred (by exchange for [H3967]), measure, post.

  • cubit - a measure of distance (the forearm), roughly 18 inches (.5 meters). There are several cubits used in the OT, the cubit of a man or common cubit ([Deuteronomy 3#11|Deuteronomy 3:11]), the legal cubit or cubit of the sanctuary ([Ezekiel 40#5|Ezekiel 40:5]) plus others. See a Bible Dictionary for a complete treatment.
