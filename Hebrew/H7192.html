H7192 קְשִׂיטָה / קשיטה

qᵉsîyṭâh

(kes-ee-taw' | keh-see-TAW | keh-see-TA)

Definition

feminine noun; from an unused root (probably meaning to weigh out); an ingot (as definitely estimated and stamped for a coin); piece of money (silver).

  • a unit of unknown value
    • perhaps weight, money
