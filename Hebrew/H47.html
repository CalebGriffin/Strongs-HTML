H47 אַבִּיר / אביר

ʼabbîyr

(ab-beer' | ab-BEER | ah-BEER)

Definition

masculine adjective; for [H46]; a valiant one; angel, bull, chiefest, mighty (one), stout(-hearted), strong (one), valiant.

  • mighty, valiant
    • of men
    • of angels
    • of animals
    • (metaphorically)
      • of enemies
      • of princes
      • of sacrificial objects
    • obstinate (figuratively)
