H5599 סָפִיחַ / ספיח

çâphîyach

(saw-fee'-akh | saw-PEE-ah | sa-FEE-ak)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H5596]; something (spontaneously) falling off, i.e., a self-sown crop; figuratively, a freshet; (such) things as (which) grow (of themselves), which groweth of its own accord (itself).

  • outpouring
  • growth from spilled kernels, after-growth, volunteer plants
