H724 אֲרוּכָה / ארוכה

ʼărûwkâh

(ar-oo-kaw' | uh-roo-KAW | uh-roo-HA)

Definition

feminine noun; or אֲרֻכָה; feminine passive participle of [H748] (in the sense of restoring to soundness); wholeness (literally or figuratively); health, made up, perfected.

  • healing, restoration
