H5782 עוּר / עור

ʻûwr

(oor | oor | oor)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (rather identical with through the idea of opening the eyes); to wake (literally or figuratively); (a-)wake(-n) (up), lift up (self), [lexicon-key|×] master, raise (up), stir up (self).

  • to rouse oneself, awake, awaken, incite
    • (Qal) to rouse oneself, awake
    • (Niphal) to be roused
    • (Polel) to stir up, rouse, incite
    • (Hithpolel) to be excited, be triumphant
    • (Hiphil)
      • to rouse, stir up
      • to act in an aroused manner, awake
