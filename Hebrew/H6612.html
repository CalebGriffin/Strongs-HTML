H6612 פְּתִי / פתי

pᵉthîy

(peth-ee' | peh-TEE | peh-TEE)

Definition

adjective; or פֶּתִי; or פְּתָאִי; from [H6601]; silly (i.e., seducible); foolish, simple(-icity) (one).

  • simplicity, naivete (noun feminine)
  • simple, foolish, open-minded (adjective)
