H5803 עַזְגָּד / עזגד

ʻAzgâd

(az-gawd' | adz-ɡAWD | az-ɡAHD)

Definition

proper masculine noun; Gad is mighty; from [H5794] and [H1409]; stern troop; Azgad, an Israelite; Azgad.

  • ancestor of a family of laymen who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
  • a man who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
