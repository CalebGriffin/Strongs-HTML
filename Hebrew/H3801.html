H3801 כְּתֹנֶת / כתנת

kᵉthôneth

(keth-o'-neth | keh-toh-NET | keh-toh-NET)

Definition

feminine noun; or כֻּתֹּנֶת; from an unused root meaning to cover (compare [H3802]); a shirt; coat, garment, robe.

  • tunic, under-garment
    • a long shirt-like garment usually of linen
