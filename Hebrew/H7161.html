H7161 קֶרֶן / קרן

qeren

(keh'-ren | keh-REN | keh-REN)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H7160]; a horn (as projecting); by implication, a flask, cornet; by resemblance. an elephant's tooth (i.e., ivory), a corner (of the altar), a peak (of a mountain), a ray (of light); figuratively, power; [lexicon-key|×] hill, horn.

  • horn (noun feminine)
    • horn
    • of strength (figuratively)
    • flask (container for oil)
    • horn (as musical instrument)
    • horn (of horn-like projections on the altar)
    • of rays of light
    • hill
  • a place conquered by Israel probably in Bashan (noun proper locative)
