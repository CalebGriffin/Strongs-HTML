H1819 דָּמָה / דמה

dâmâh

(daw-maw' | daw-MAW | da-MA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to compare; by implication, to resemble, liken, consider; compare, devise, (be) like(-n), mean, think, use similitudes.

  • to be like, resemble
    • (Qal) to be like, resemble
    • (Piel)
      • to liken, compare
      • to imagine, think
    • (Hithpael) to make oneself like
    • (Niphal)
