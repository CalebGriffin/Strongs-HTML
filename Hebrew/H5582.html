H5582 סָעַד / סעד

çâʻad

(saw-ad' | saw-AD | sa-AD)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to support (mostly figurative); comfort, establish, hold up, refresh self, strengthen, be upholden.

  • to support, sustain, stay, establish, strengthen, comfort
    • (Qal) to support, sustain
      • to sustain, stay (of heart)
      • to support, uphold (of throne)
