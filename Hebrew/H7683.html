H7683 שָׁגַג / שגג

shâgag

(shaw-gag' | shaw-ɡAHɡ | sha-ɡAHɡ)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to stray, i.e., (figuratively) sin (with more or less apology); [lexicon-key|×] also for that, deceived, err, go astray, sin ignorantly.

  • to go astray, err, commit sin or error
    • (Qal)
      • to err (mentally)
      • to sin (ignorantly or inadvertently)
