H7965 שָׁלוֹם / שלום

shâlôwm

(shaw-lome' | shaw-LOME | sha-LOME)

Definition

masculine noun; or שָׁלֹם; from [H7999]; safe, i.e., (figuratively) well, happy, friendly; also (abstractly) welfare, i.e., health, prosperity, peace; [lexicon-key|×] do, familiar, [lexicon-key|×] fare, favour, [lexicon-key|+] friend, [lexicon-key|×] great, (good) health, ([lexicon-key|×] perfect, such as be at) peace(-able, -ably), prosper(-ity, -ous), rest, safe(-ty), salute, welfare, ([lexicon-key|×] all is, be) well, [lexicon-key|×] wholly.

  • completeness, soundness, welfare, peace
    • completeness (in number)
    • safety, soundness (in body)
    • welfare, health, prosperity
    • peace, quiet, tranquillity, contentment
    • peace, friendship
      • of human relationships
      • with God especially in covenant relationship
    • peace (from war)
    • peace (as adjective)
