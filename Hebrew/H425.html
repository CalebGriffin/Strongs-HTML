H425 אֵלָה / אלה

ʼÊlâh

(ay-law' | ay-LAW | ay-LA)

Definition

proper noun; An oak; the same as [H424]; Elah, the name of an Edomite, of four Israelites, and also of a place in Palestine; Elah.

  • an Edomite chief
  • a king of Israel for two years, son of Baasha
  • the father of king Hoshea of Israel
  • a son of Caleb
  • son of Uzzi
