H5399 נֶשֶׁף / נשף

nesheph

(neh'-shef | neh-SHEP | neh-SHEF)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H5398]; properly, a breeze, i.e., (by implication) dusk (when the evening breeze prevails); dark, dawning of the day (morning), night, twilight.

  • twilight
    • evening twilight
    • morning twilight
