H6255 עַשְׁתְּרֹת קַרְנַיִם / עשתרת קרנים

ʻAshtᵉrôth Qarnayim

(ash-ter-oth' kar-nah'-yim | ash-teh-ROTE kahr-na-YIM | ash-teh-ROTE kahr-na-YEEM)

Definition

proper locative noun; Ashtoreth of the two horns, peaks; from [H6252] and the dual of [H7161]; Ashtaroth of (the) double horns (a symbol of the deity); Ashteroth-Karnaim, a place East of the Jordan; Ashtoreth Karnaim.

  • a city in Bashan east of the Jordan given to Manasseh
