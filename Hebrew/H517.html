H517 אֵם / אם

ʼêm

(ame | ame | ame)

Definition

feminine noun; a primitive word; a mother (as the bond of the family); in a wide sense (both literally and figuratively [like father]); dam, mother, [lexicon-key|×] parting.

  • mother
    • of humans
    • of Deborah's relationship to the people (figuratively)
    • of animals
  • point of departure or division
