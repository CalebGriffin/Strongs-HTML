H2522 חָלַשׁ / חלש

châlash

(khaw-lash' | haw-LAHSH | ha-LAHSH)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to prostrate; by implication, to overthrow, decay; discomfit, waste away, weaken.

  • to be weak, be prostrate
    • (Qal) to be prostrate
  • to weaken, disable, prostrate
    • (Qal) to disable, prostrate
