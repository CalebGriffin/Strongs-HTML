H1168 בַּעַל / בעל

Baʻal

(bah'-al | ba-AL | ba-AL)

Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; lord; the same as [H1167]; Baal, a Phoenician deity; Baal, (plural) Baalim.

  • supreme male divinity of the Phoenicians or Canaanites (noun proper masculine)
  • a Reubenite (noun proper masculine)
  • the son of Jehiel and grandfather of Saul (noun proper masculine)
  • a town of Simeon, probably identical to Baalath-beer (noun proper locative)
