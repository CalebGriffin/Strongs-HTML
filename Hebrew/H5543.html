H5543 סַלּוּ / סלו

Çallûw

(sal-loo' | sahl-LOO | sa-LOO)

Definition

proper masculine noun; weighed; or סַלּוּא; or סָלוּא; or סַלַּי; from [H5541]; weighed; Sallu or Sallai, the name of two Israelites; Sallai, Sallu, Salu.

  • a priest in the time of Zerubbabel
  • father of Zimri, the prince of the Simeonites, who was slain by Phinehas
  • a post exilic Benjamite
