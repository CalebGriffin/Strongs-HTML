H7106 קָצַע / קצע

qâtsaʻ

(kaw-tsah' | kaw-TSA | ka-TSA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to strip off, i.e., (partially) scrape; by implication, to segregate (as an angle); cause to scrape, corner.

  • to scrape, scrape off, cut off
    • (Hiphil) to scrape
  • to be cornered, be set in corners
    • (Hophal) to cut off places, make corners
