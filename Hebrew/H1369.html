H1369 גְּבוּרָה / גבורה

gᵉbûwrâh

(gheb-oo-raw' | ɡeh-boo-RAW | ɡeh-voo-RA)

Definition

feminine noun; feminine passive participle from the same as [H1368]; force (literally or figuratively); by implication, valor, victory; force, mastery, might, mighty (act, power), power, strength.

  • strength, might
    • strength
    • might, valour, bravery
    • might, mighty deeds (of God)
