H3663 כְּנַנְיָה / כנניה

Kᵉnanyâh

(ken-an-yaw' | keh-nahn-YAW | keh-nahn-YA)

Definition

proper masculine noun; Jehovah establishes; or כְּנַנְיָהוּ; from [H3661] and [H3050]; Jah has planted; Kenanjah, an Israelite; Chenaniah.

  • a Levite song leader who assisted in the procession of transporting the ark
  • an Izharites officer of David
