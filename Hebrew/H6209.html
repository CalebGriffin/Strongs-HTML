H6209 עָרַר / ערר

ʻârar

(aw-rar' | aw-RAHR | ah-RAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to bare; figuratively, to demolish; make bare, break, raise up (perhaps by clerical error for raze), [lexicon-key|×] utterly.

  • to strip, make bare, strip oneself
    • (Qal) to strip, strip oneself
    • (Poel) to lay bare
    • (Hithpalpel) to be utterly stripped, be utterly laid bare
    • (Pilpel) break
