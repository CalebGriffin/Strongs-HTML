H8432 תָּוֶךְ / תוך

tâvek

(taw'-vek | taw-WEK | ta-VEK)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning to sever; a bisection, i.e., (by implication) the center; among(-st), [lexicon-key|×] between, half, [lexicon-key|×] (there-, where-)in(-to), middle, mid(-night), midst (among), [lexicon-key|×] out (of), [lexicon-key|×] through, [lexicon-key|×] with(-in).

  • midst, middle
    • midst, middle
    • into, through (after verbs of motion)
    • among (of a number of persons)
    • between (of things arranged by twos)
    • from among (as to take or separate etc)
