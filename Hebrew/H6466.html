H6466 פָּעַל / פעל

pâʻal

(paw-al' | paw-AL | pa-AL)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to do or make (systematically and habitually), especially to practise; commit, (evil-)do(-er), make(-r), ordain, work(-er).

  • to do, make
    • (Qal)
      • to do
      • to make
