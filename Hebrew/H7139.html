H7139 קָרַח / קרח

qârach

(kaw-rakh' | kaw-RA | ka-RAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to depilate; make (self) bald.

  • to be bald, make bald
    • (Qal) to make bald, make a baldness
    • (Niphal) to make oneself bald
    • (Hiphil) to make oneself bald
    • (Hophal) to make bald
