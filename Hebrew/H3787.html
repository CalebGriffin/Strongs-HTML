H3787 כָּשֵׁר / כשר

kâshêr

(kaw-share' | kaw-SHARE | ka-SHARE)

Definition

verb; a primitive root properly, to be straight or right; by implication, to be acceptable; also to succeed or prosper; direct, be right, prosper.

  • to succeed, please, be suitable, be proper, be advantageous, be right and proper to
    • (Qal) to please, be proper
    • (Hiphil) to give success
