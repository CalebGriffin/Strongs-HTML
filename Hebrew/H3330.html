H3330 יַצִּיב / יציב

yatstsîyb

(yats-tseeb' | yahts-SEEB | ya-TSEEV)

Definition

adjective; from [H3321]; fixed, sure; concretely, certainty; certain(-ty), true, truth.

  • the truth (noun)
  • surely, reliably, truly (adjective)
  • reliable, true, certain, sure (adjective)
