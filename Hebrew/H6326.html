H6326 פּוּעָה / פועה

Pûwʻâh

(poo-aw' | poo-AW | poo-AH)

Definition

proper feminine noun; splendid; from an unused root meaning to glitter; brilliancy; Puah, an Israelitess; Puah.

  • one of the 2 midwives of the Hebrews ordered by Pharaoh to kill all the male children born to Israel; time of Moses
