H3957 לִשְׁכָּה / לשכה

lishkâh

(lish-kaw' | lish-KAW | leesh-KA)

Definition

feminine noun; from an unused root of uncertain meaning; a room in a building (whether for storage, eating, or lodging); chamber, parlour.

  • room, chamber, hall, cell

Note

Compare [H5393].
