H6279 עָתַר / עתר

ʻâthar

(aw-thar' | aw-TAHR | ah-TAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (rather denominative from [H6281]); to burn incense in worship, i.e., intercede (reciprocally, listen to prayer); intreat, (make) pray(-er).

  • to pray, entreat, supplicate
    • (Qal) to pray, entreat
    • (Niphal) to be supplicated, be entreated
    • (Hiphil) to make supplication, plead
