H6529 פְּרִי / פרי

pᵉrîy

(per-ee' | peh-REE | peh-REE)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H6509]; fruit (literally or figuratively); bough, (first-)fruit(-ful), reward.

  • fruit
    • fruit, produce (of the ground)
    • fruit, offspring, children, progeny (of the womb)
    • fruit (of actions) (figuratively)
