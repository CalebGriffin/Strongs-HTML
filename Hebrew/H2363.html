H2363 חוּשׁ / חוש

chûwsh

(koosh | hoosh | hoosh)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to hurry; figuratively, to be eager with excitement or enjoyment; (make) haste(-n), ready.

  • to haste, make haste, hurry
    • (Qal) to make haste
    • (Hiphil)
      • to show haste, act quickly, hasten, come quickly
      • to enjoy, be excited
