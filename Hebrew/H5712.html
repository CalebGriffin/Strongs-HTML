H5712 עֵדָה / עדה

ʻêdâh

(ay-daw' | ay-DAW | ay-DA)

Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [H5707] in the original sense of fixture; a stated assemblage (specifically, a concourse, or generally, a family or crowd); assembly, company, congregation, multitude, people, swarm.

  • congregation, gathering

Note

Compare [H5713].
