H7879 שִׂיחַ / שיח

sîyach

(see'-akh | SEE-ah | SEE-ak)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H7878]; a contemplation; by implication, an utterance; babbling, communication, complaint, meditation, prayer, talk.

  • meditation, complaint, musing
    • plaint, complaint
    • following are dubious
      • musing
      • anxiety, trouble
      • talk
