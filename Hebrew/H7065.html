H7065 קָנָא / קנא

qânâʼ

(kaw-naw' | kaw-NAW | ka-NA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be (causatively, make) zealous, i.e., (in a bad sense) jealous or envious; (be) envy(-ious), be (move to, provoke to) jealous(-y), [lexicon-key|×] very, (be) zeal(-ous).

  • to envy, be jealous, be envious, be zealous
    • (Piel)
      • to be jealous of
      • to be envious of
      • to be zealous for
      • to excite to jealous anger
    • (Hiphil) to provoke to jealous anger, cause jealousy
