H5247 נִמְרָה / נמרה

Nimrâh

(nim-raw' | nim-RAW | neem-RA)

Definition

proper locative noun; limpid, pure; from the same as [H5246]; clear water; Nimrah, a place East of the Jordan; Nimrah.

  • a place on the east of the Jordan, 10 miles (16 kilometers) north of the Dead Sea and 3 miles (5 kilometers) east of the Jordan
