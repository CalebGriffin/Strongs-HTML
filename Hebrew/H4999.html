H4999 נָאָה / נאה

nâʼâh

(naw-aw' | naw-AW | na-AH)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H4998]; a home; figuratively, a pasture; habitation, house, pasture, pleasant place.

  • pasture, abode, abode of shepherd, habitation, meadow
