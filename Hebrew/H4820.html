H4820 מִרְמָה / מרמה

mirmâh

(meer-maw' | mir-MAW | meer-MA)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H7411] in the sense of deceiving; fraud; craft, deceit(-ful, -fully), false, feigned, guile, subtilly, treachery.

  • deceit, treachery
