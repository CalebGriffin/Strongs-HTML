H6552 פִּרְעָתוֹן / פרעתון

Pirʻâthôwn

(pir-aw-thone' | pir-aw-TONE | peer-ah-TONE)

Definition

proper locative noun; princely; from [H6546]; chieftaincy; Pirathon, a place in Palestine; Pirathon.

  • a town in Ephraim in the mount of the Amalekite
    • perhaps modern 'Ferata' located - 5 miles (- 5 kilometers) south from Jaffa on the road to Nablus
