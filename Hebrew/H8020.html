H8020 שַׁלְמַן / שלמן

Shalman

(shal-man' | shahl-MAHN | shahl-MAHN)

Definition

proper masculine noun; fire-worshipper; of foreign derivation; Shalman, a king apparently of Assyria; Shalman.

  • a contraction for Shalmaneser king of Assyria
    • maybe an obscure Assyrian king, predecessor of Pul

Note

Compare [H8022].
