H7741 שָׁוֵה קִרְיָתַיִם / שוה קריתים

Shâvêh Qiryâthayim

(shaw-vay' kir-yawthah'-yim | shaw-WAY kir-yaw-ta-YIM | sha-VAY keer-ya-ta-YEEM)

Definition

proper masculine noun; plain of the double, two city; from the same as [H7740] and the dual of [H7151]; plain of a double city; Shaveh-Kirjathajim, a place East of the Jordan; Shaveh Kiriathaim.

  • the place attacked by Chedorlaomer
