H3518 כָּבָה / כבה

kâbâh

(kaw-baw' | kaw-BAW | ka-VA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to expire or (causatively) to extinguish (fire, light, anger); go (put) out, quench.

  • to quench, put out, be put out, be quenched, be extinguished
    • (Qal) to be quenched, be extinguished
    • (Piel) to quench, extinguish
