H85 אַבְרָהָם / אברהם

ʼAbrâhâm

(ab-raw-hawm' | ab-raw-HAWM | av-ra-HAHM)

Definition

proper masculine noun; father of a multitude, chief of multitude; contracted from [H1] and an unused root (probably meaning to be populous); father of a multitude; Abraham, the later name of Abram; Abraham.

  • friend of God and founder of Hebrew nation via God's elective covenant
