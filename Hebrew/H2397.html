H2397 חָח / חח

châch

(khawkh | haw | hahk)

Definition

masculine noun; once (Ezekiel 29:4) חָחִי; from the same as [H2336]; a ring for the nose (or lips); bracelet, chain, hook.

  • hook, ring, fetter, brooch
    • hook, ring (in nose of captive)
    • hook, ring (as jewellery)
      • nose ring
      • bracelet
