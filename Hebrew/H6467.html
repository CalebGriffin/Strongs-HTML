H6467 פֹּעַל / פעל

pôʻal

(po'-al | poh-AL | poh-AL)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H6466]; an act or work (concretely); act, deed, do, getting, maker, work.

  • work, deed, doing
    • deed, thing done
    • work, thing made
    • wages of work
    • acquisition (of treasure)
