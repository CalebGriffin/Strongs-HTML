H8528 תֵּל מֶלַח / תל מלח

Têl Melach

(tale meh'-lakh | tale meh-LA | tale meh-LAHK)

Definition

proper locative noun; mound of salt; from [H8510] and [H4417]; mound of salt; Tel-Melach, a place in Babylonia; Tel-melah.

  • a place in Babylon from which some exiles of unknown parentage came; returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel
