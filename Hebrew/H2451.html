H2451 חׇכְמָה / חכמה

chokmâh

(khok-maw' | hoke-MAW | hoke-MA)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H2449]; wisdom (in a good sense); skilful, wisdom, wisely, wit.

  • wisdom
    • skill (in war)
    • wisdom (in administration)
    • shrewdness, wisdom
    • wisdom, prudence (in religious affairs)
    • wisdom (ethical and religious)
