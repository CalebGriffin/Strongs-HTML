H5878 עֵין חֲרֹד / עין חרד

ʻÊyn Chărôd

(ane khar-ode' | ane huh-RODE | ane huh-RODE)

Definition

proper locative noun; spring of Herod; from [H5869] and a derivative of [H2729]; fountain of trembling; En-Charod, a place in Palestine; well of Harod.

  • a camping place of Gideon and Israel near the hill of Moreh
