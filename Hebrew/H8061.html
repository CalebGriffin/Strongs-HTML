H8061 שְׁמִידָע / שמידע

Shᵉmîydâʻ

(shem-ee-daw' | sheh-mee-DAW | sheh-mee-DA)

Definition

proper masculine noun; wise; apparently from [H8034] and [H3045]; name of knowing; Shemida, an Israelite; Shemida, Shemidah.

  • a son of Gilead, grandson of Manasseh, and progenitor of a family in Manasseh
