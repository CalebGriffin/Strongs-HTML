H7834 שַׁחַק / שחק

shachaq

(shakh'-ak | sha-HAHK | sha-HAHK)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H7833]; a powder (as beaten small); by analogy, a thin vapor; by extension, the firmament; cloud, small dust, heaven, sky.

  • dust, cloud
    • fine dust
    • (thin) cloud
