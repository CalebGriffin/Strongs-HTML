H5472 סוּג / סוג

çûwg

(soog | sooɡ | sooɡ)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to flinch, i.e., (by implication) to go back, literally (to retreat) or figuratively (to apostatize); backslider, drive, go back, turn (away, back).

  • to move, go, turn back, move away, backslide
    • (Qal) to backslide, prove recreant to
    • (Niphal)
      • to turn oneself away, turn back
      • to be turned or driven back, be repulsed
