H8065 שָׁמַיִן / שמין

shâmayin

(shaw-mah'-yin | shaw-ma-YIN | sha-ma-YEEN)

Definition

masculine noun; corresponding to [H8064]; the sky (as aloft; the dual perhaps alluding to the visible arch in which the clouds move, as well as to the higher ether where the celestial bodies revolve); heaven.

  • heaven, heavens, sky
    • visible sky
    • Heaven (as abode of God)
