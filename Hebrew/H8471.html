H8471 תַּחְפַּנְחֵס / תחפנחס

Tachpanchêç

(takh-pan-khace' | ta-pahn-HASE | tahk-pahn-HASE)

Definition

proper locative noun; thou will fill hands with pity; or תְּחַפְנְחֵס; (Ezekiel 30:18), or תַּחְפְּנֵס; (Jeremiah 2:16), of Egyptian derivation; Tachpanches, Techaphneches or Tachpenes, a place in Egypt; Tahapanes, Tahpanhes, Tehaphnehes.

  • a city in Egypt; modern 'Tel Defenneh' or 'Tel Defneh' located approx 18 miles (29 kilometers) east southeast from Tanis
