H8070 שְׁמִירָמוֹת / שמירמות

Shᵉmîyrâmôwth

(shem-ee-raw-moth' | sheh-mee-raw-MOTE | sheh-mee-ra-MOTE)

Definition

proper masculine noun; name of heights; or שְׁמָרִימוֹת; probably from [H8034] and plural of [H7413]; name of heights; Shemiramoth, the name of two Israelites; Shemiramoth.

  • a Merarite Levite in the second rank in the choir of David
  • a Levite in the time of king Jehoshaphat of Judah
