H6264 עָתִיד / עתיד

ʻâthîyd

(aw-theed' | aw-TEED | ah-TEED)

Definition

adjective; from [H6257]; prepared; by implication, skilful; feminine plural the future; also treasure; things that shall come, ready, treasures.

  • ready, prepared
    • ready
    • ready, skilled
    • prepared, impending
    • prepared, stored up, treasure
