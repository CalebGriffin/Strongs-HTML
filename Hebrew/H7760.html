H7760 שׂוּם / שום

sûwm

(soom | soom | soom)

Definition

verb; or שִׂים; a primitive root; to put (used in a great variety of applications, literal, figurative, inferentially, and elliptically); [lexicon-key|×] any wise, appoint, bring, call (a name), care, cast in, change, charge, commit, consider, convey, determine, [lexicon-key|+] disguise, dispose, do, get, give, heap up, hold, impute, lay (down, up), leave, look, make (out), mark, [lexicon-key|+] name, [lexicon-key|×] on, ordain, order, [lexicon-key|+] paint, place, preserve, purpose, put (on), [lexicon-key|+] regard, rehearse, reward, (cause to) set (on, up), shew, [lexicon-key|+] stedfastly, take, [lexicon-key|×] tell, [lexicon-key|+] tread down, (over-)turn, [lexicon-key|×] wholly, work.

  • to put, place, set, appoint, make
    • (Qal)
      • to put, set, lay, put or lay upon, lay (violent) hands on
      • to set, direct, direct toward
        • to extend (compassion) (figuratively)
      • to set, ordain, establish, found, appoint, constitute, make, determine, fix
      • to set, station, put, set in place, plant, fix
      • to make, make for, transform into, constitute, fashion, work, bring to pass, appoint, give
    • (Hiphil) to set or make for a sign
    • (Hophal) to be set
