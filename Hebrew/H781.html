H781 אָרַשׂ / ארש

ʼâras

(aw-ras' | aw-RAHS | ah-RAHS)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to promise to marry; betroth, espouse.

  • to betroth, engage
    • (Piel) to betroth (man or woman)
    • (Pual) to be betrothed

Note

Unlike modern day engagement, betrothal always ends in marriage. Consider how Joseph intended to put Mary away privately instead of calling off the marriage (Matthew 1:19).
