H7278 רֶגֶם מֶלֶךְ / רגם מלך

Regem Melek

(reh'-gem meh'-lek | reh-ɡEM meh-LEK | reh-ɡEM meh-LEK)

Definition

proper masculine noun; king's heap; from [H7276] and [H4428]; king's heap; Regem-Melek, an Israelite; Regem-melech.

  • a man sent by some of the captivity to make enquiries at the temple concerning fasting
