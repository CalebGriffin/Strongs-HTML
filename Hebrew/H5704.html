H5704 עַד / עד

ʻad

(ad | ad | ad)

Definition

preposition; properly, the same as [H5703] (used as a preposition, adverb or conjunction; especially with a preposition); as far (or long, or much) as, whether of space (even unto) or time (during, while, until) or degree (equally with); against, and, as, at, before, by (that), even (to), for(-asmuch as), (hither-)to, [lexicon-key|+] how long, into, as long (much) as, (so) that, till, toward, until, when, while, ([lexicon-key|+] as) yet.

  • as far as, even to, until, up to, while, as far as (preposition)
    • of space
      • as far as, up to, even to
    • in combination
      • from...as far as, both...and (with 'min' - from)
    • of time
      • even to, until, unto, till, during, end
    • of degree
      • even to, to the degree of, even like
  • until, while, to the point that, so that even (conjunction)
