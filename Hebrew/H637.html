H637 אַף / אף

ʼaph

(af | ap | af)

Definition

particle of speech; a primitive particle; meaning accession (used as an adverb or conjunction); also or yea; adversatively though; also, [lexicon-key|+] although, and (furthermore, yet), but, even, [lexicon-key|+] how much less (more, rather than), moreover, with, yea.

  • also, yea, though, so much the more
  • furthermore, indeed
