H740 אֲרִיאֵל / אריאל

ʼĂrîyʼêl

(ar-ee-ale' | uh-ree-ALE | uh-ree-ALE)

Definition

proper noun; lion of God, lioness of God; the same as [H739]; Ariel, a symbolical name for Jerusalem, also the name of an Israelite; Ariel.

  • a name applied to Jerusalem
  • the name of a chief of the returning exiles
