H5298 נֶפֶג / נפג

Nepheg

(neh'-feg | neh-PEɡ | neh-FEɡ)

Definition

proper masculine noun; sprout; from an unused root probably meaning to spring forth; a sprout; Nepheg, the name of two Israelites; Nepheg.

  • one of sons of Izhar and grandson of Kohath of the tribe of Levi in the time of the exodus
  • one of the sons of David born to him at Jerusalem
