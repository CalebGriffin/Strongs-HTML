H3905 לָחַץ / לחץ

lâchats

(law-khats' | law-HAHTS | la-HAHTS)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to press, i.e., (figuratively) to distress; afflict, crush, force, hold fast, oppress(-or), thrust self.

  • to squeeze, press, oppress
    • (Qal)
      • to squeeze, press
      • to oppress
    • (Niphal) to squeeze oneself
