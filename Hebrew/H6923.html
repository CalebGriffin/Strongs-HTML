H6923 קָדַם / קדם

qâdam

(kaw-dam' | kaw-DAHM | ka-DAHM)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to project (one self), i.e., precede; hence, to anticipate, hasten, meet (usually for help); come (go, (flee)) before, disappoint, meet, prevent.

  • to meet, come or be in front, confront, go before
    • (Piel)
      • to meet, confront, come to meet, receive
      • to go before, go in front, be in front
      • to lead, be beforehand, anticipate, forestall
    • (Hiphil)
      • to come in front
      • to confront, anticipate
