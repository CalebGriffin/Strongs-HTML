H6078 עׇפְנִי / עפני

ʻOphnîy

(of-nee' | ope-NEE | ofe-NEE)

Definition

proper locative noun; mouldy; from an unused noun (denoting a place in Palestine; from an unused root of uncertain meaning); an Ophnite (collectively) or inhabitants of Ophen; Ophni.

  • a town of Benjamin located - 5 (- 5 kilometers) miles northwest of Bethel; modern 'Jifna'
