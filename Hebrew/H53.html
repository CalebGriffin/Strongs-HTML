H53 אֲבִישָׁלוֹם / אבישלום

ʼĂbîyshâlôwm

(ab-ee-shaw-lome' | uh-bee-shaw-LOME | uh-vee-sha-LOME)

Definition

proper masculine noun; my father is peace; or (shortened) אַבְשָׁלוֹם ; from [H1] and [H7965]; father of peace (i.e., friendly); Abshalom, a son of David; also (the fuller form) a later Israelite; Abishalom, Absalom.

  • father-in-law of Rehoboam
  • third son of David, killer of first-born son Amnon, also leader of revolt against his father - David
