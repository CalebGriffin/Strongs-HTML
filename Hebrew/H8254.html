H8254 שָׁקַל / שקל

shâqal

(shaw-kal' | shaw-KAHL | sha-KAHL)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to suspend or poise (especially in trade); pay, receive(-r), spend, [lexicon-key|×] throughly, weigh.

  • to weigh, weigh out, pay out
    • (Qal)
      • to weigh
      • to weigh out (a price)
      • of grief (figuratively)
    • (Niphal)
      • to be weighed
      • to be weighed out
