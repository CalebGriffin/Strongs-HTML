H851 אֶשְׁתְּמֹעַ / אשתמע

ʼEshtᵉmôaʻ

(esh-tem-o'-ah | esh-teh-MOH-ah | esh-teh-MOH-ah)

Definition

proper noun; I will make myself heard, I shall cause my own ruin, fire of astonishment, I shall soar aloft; or אֶשְׁתְּמוֹעַ; or אֶשְׁתְּמֹה; from [H8085] (in the sense of obedience); Eshtemoa or Eshtemoh, a place in Palestine; Eshtemoa, Eshtemoh.

  • a Levitical city in Judah
  • a man of Judah

Note

Eshtemoa means "I will make myself heard," and Eshtemoh means "I shall cause my own ruin," "fire of astonishment," or "I shall soar aloft."
