H7154 קִרְיַת בַּעַל / קרית בעל

Qiryath Baʻal

(keer-yath' bah'-al | kir-YAHT ba-AL | keer-YAHT ba-AL)

Definition

proper locative noun; city of Baal; from [H7151] and [H1168]; city of Baal; Kirjath-Baal, a place in Palestine; Kirjathbaal.

  • a city on the northern boundary of Judah and on the western and southern boundaries of Benjamin
    • also 'Kirjath-jearim' and 'Baalah'
