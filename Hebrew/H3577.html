H3577 כָּזָב / כזב

kâzâb

(kaw-zawb' | kaw-DZAWB | ka-ZAHV)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H3576]; falsehood; literally (untruth) or figuratively (idol); deceitful, false, leasing, liar, lie, lying.

  • a lie, untruth, falsehood, deceptive thing
