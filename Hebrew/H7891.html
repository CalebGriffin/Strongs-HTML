H7891 שִׁיר / שיר

shîyr

(sheer | sheer | sheer)

Definition

verb; or (the original form) שׁוּר; (1 Samuel 18:6), a primitive root (rather identical with [H7788] through the idea of strolling minstrelsy); to sing; behold (by mistake for [H7789]), sing(-er, -ing man, -ing woman).

  • to sing
    • (Qal)
      • to sing
      • singer, songstresses (participle)
    • (Polel)
      • to sing
      • singer, songstress (participle)
    • (Hophal) to be sung
