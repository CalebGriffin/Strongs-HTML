H5822 עׇזְנִיָּה / עזניה

ʻoznîyâh

(oz-nee-yaw' | ohdz-nih-YAW | oze-nee-YA)

Definition

feminine noun; probably feminine of [H5797]; probably the sea-eagle (from its strength); ospray.

  • an unclean bird of prey
    • osprey, black eagle, buzzard
    • perhaps an extinct bird, exact meaning unknown
