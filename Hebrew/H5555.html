H5555 סֶלַע הַמַּחְלְקוֹת / סלע המחלקות

Çelaʻ ham-machlᵉqôwth

(seh'-lah hammakh-lek-oth' | seh-LA hahm-ma-leh-KOTE | seh-LA ha-mahk-leh-KOTE)

Definition

proper locative noun; the cliff of escapes, the cliff of divisions; from [H5553] and the plural of [H4256] with the article interposed; rock of the divisions; Sela-ham-Machlekoth, a place in Palestine; Sela-hammalekoth.

  • a rock or cliff in the wilderness of Maon, southeast of Hebron, the scene of an escape of David from Saul
