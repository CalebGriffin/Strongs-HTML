H8548 תָּמִיד / תמיד

tâmîyd

(taw-meed' | taw-MEED | ta-MEED)

Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning to stretch; properly, continuance (as indefinite extension); but used only (attributively as adjective) constant (or adverbially, constantly); elliptically the regular (daily) sacrifice; alway(-s), continual (employment, -ly), daily, (n-)ever(-more), perpetual.

  • continuity, perpetuity, to stretch
    • continually, continuously (as adverb)
    • continuity (substantive)
