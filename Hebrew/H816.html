H816 אָשַׁם / אשם

ʼâsham

(aw-sham' | aw-SHAHM | ah-SHAHM)

Definition

verb; or אָשֵׁם; a primitive root; to be guilty; by implication to be punished or perish; [lexicon-key|×] certainly, be(-come) (made) desolate, destroy, [lexicon-key|×] greatly, be(-come) (found, hold) guilty, offend (acknowledge offence), trespass.

  • to offend, be guilty, trespass
    • (Qal)
      • to do wrong, offend, trespass, commit an offense, do injury
      • to be or become guilty
      • to be held guilty
      • to be incriminated
    • (Niphal) to suffer punishment
    • (Hiphil) to declare guilty
  • ([lexicon-key|TWOT]) to be desolate, acknowledge offense
