H8492 תִּירוֹשׁ / תירוש

tîyrôwsh

(tee-roshe' | tee-ROHSH | tee-ROHSH)

Definition

masculine noun; or תִּירֹשׁ; from [H3423] in the sense of expulsion; must or fresh grape-juice (as just squeezed out); by implication (rarely) fermented wine; (new, sweet) wine.

  • wine, fresh or new wine, must, freshly pressed wine
