H6152 עֲרָב / ערב

ʻĂrâb

(ar-awb' | uh-RAWB | uh-RAHV)

Definition

proper noun; or עֲרַב; from [H6150] in the figurative sense of sterility; Arab (i.e., Arabia), a country East of Palestine; Arabia.

  • steppe-dwellers
    • the people inhabiting the country east and south of Canaan, the nomadic desert Bedouins
    • Arabians, Arabs
