H6149 עָרֵב / ערב

ʻârêb

(aw-rabe' | aw-RABE | ah-RAVE)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (rather identical with [H6148] through the idea of close association); to be agreeable; be pleasant(-ing), take pleasure in, be sweet.

  • (Qal) to be pleasant, be sweet, be pleasing
    • ([lexicon-key|TWOT]) sweet, pleasant
