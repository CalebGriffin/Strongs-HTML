H8668 תְּשׁוּעָה / תשועה

tᵉshûwʻâh

(tesh-oo-aw' | teh-shoo-AW | teh-shoo-AH)

Definition

feminine noun; or תְּשֻׁעָה; from [H7768] in the sense of [H3467]; rescue (literal or figurative, persons, national or spiritual); deliverance, help, safety, salvation, victory.

  • salvation, deliverance
    • deliverance (usually by God through human agency)
    • salvation (spiritual in sense)
