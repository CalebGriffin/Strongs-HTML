H6413 פְּלֵיטָה / פליטה

pᵉlêyṭâh

(pel-ay-taw' | peh-lay-TAW | peh-lay-TA)

Definition

feminine noun; or פְּלֵטָה; feminine of [H6412]; deliverance; concretely, an escaped portion; deliverance, (that is) escape(-d), remnant.

  • escape, deliverance
    • escape, deliverance
    • escaped remnant
