H2247 חָבָה / חבה

châbâh

(khaw-bah' | haw-BAW | ha-VA)

Definition

verb; a primitive root (compare [H2245]); to secrete; hide (self).

  • to withdraw, hide, hide oneself
    • (Qal) to withdraw
    • (Niphal) to hide oneself, remain hidden, withdraw
