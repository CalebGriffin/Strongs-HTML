H6596 פֹּת / פת

pôth

(pohth | pote | pote)

Definition

feminine noun; or פֹּתָה; (Ezekiel 13:19), from an unused root meaning to open; a hole, i.e., hinge or the female pudenda; hinge, secret participle.

  • sockets, hinges, secret parts (meaning dubious)
    • sockets
    • secret parts
