H2714 חֵקֶר / חקר

chêqer

(khay'-ker | hay-KER | hay-KER)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H2713]; examination, enumeration, deliberation; finding out, number, (un-)search(-able, -ed, out, -ing).

  • a search, investigation, searching, enquiry, thing to be searched out
