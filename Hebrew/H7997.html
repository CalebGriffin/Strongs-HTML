H7997 שָׁלַל / שלל

shâlal

(shaw-lal' | shaw-LAHL | sha-LAHL)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to drop or strip; by implication, to plunder; let fall, make self a prey, [lexicon-key|×] of purpose, (make a, (take)) spoil.

  • (Qal) to draw out
  • to spoil, plunder, take spoil
    • (Qal) to spoil, plunder
    • (Hithpolel) to be spoiled, be plundered
