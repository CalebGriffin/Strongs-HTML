H7153 קִרְיַת אַרְבַּע / קרית ארבע

Qiryath ʼArbaʻ

(keer-yath' ar-bah' | kir-YAHT ar-BA | keer-YAHT ar-BA)

Definition

proper locative noun; city of Arba; or (with the article interposed) קִרְיַת הָאַרְבַּע; (Nehemiah 11:25), from [H7151] and [H704] or [H702]; city of Arba, or city of the four (giants); Kirjath-Arba or Kirjath-ha-Arba, a place in Palestine; Kirjath-arba.

  • early name of the city which after the conquest was called 'Hebron'
