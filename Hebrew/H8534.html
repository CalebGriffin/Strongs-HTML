H8534 תַּלְתַּל / תלתל

taltal

(tal-tal' | tahl-TAHL | tahl-TAHL)

Definition

feminine noun; by reduplication, from [H8524] through the idea of vibration; a trailing bough (as pendulous); bushy.

  • wavy, branchy (used of woman's hair - figuratively)
    • meaning uncertain

Note

The exact meaning is considered uncertain.
