H6099 עָצוּם / עצום

ʻâtsûwm

(aw-tsoom' | aw-TSOOM | ah-TSOOM)

Definition

adjective; or עָצֻם; passive participle of [H6105]; powerful (specifically, a paw); by implication, numerous; [lexicon-key|+] feeble, great, mighty, must, strong.

  • mighty, vast, numerous
    • mighty, strong (in number)
    • numerous, countless
