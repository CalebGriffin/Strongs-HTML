H8559 תָּמָר / תמר

Tâmâr

(taw-mawr' | taw-MAWR | ta-MAHR)

Definition

proper noun; palm-tree; the same as [H8558]; Tamar, the name of three women and a place; Tamar.

  • widow of Er, the son of Judah; fiancee of Shelah, another son of Judah; wife of Judah and mother of Pharez and Zerah (noun proper feminine)
  • daughter of David by Maacah, sister of Absalom, and rape victim of Amnon her half brother, another son of David (noun proper feminine)
  • daughter of Absalom (noun proper feminine)
  • a place on the southern border of Gad (noun proper locative)
