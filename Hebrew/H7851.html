H7851 שִׁטִּים / שטים

Shiṭṭîym

(shit-teem' | shit-TEEM | shee-TEEM)

Definition

proper locative noun; the acacias; the same as the plural of [H7848]; acacia trees; Shittim, a place East of the Jordan; Shittim.

  • place of Israel's encampment between the conquest of the transjordanic region and crossing the Jordan into Canaan
  • a place west of Jerusalem
