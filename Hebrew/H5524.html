H5524 סֻכּוֹת בְּנוֹת / סכות בנות

Çukkôwth bᵉnôwth

(sook-kohth' ben-ohth' | sook-KOTE beh-NOTE | soo-KOTE beh-NOTE)

Definition

noun; the daughter's booth; from [H5523] and the (irregular) plural of [H1323]; booths of (the) daughters; brothels, i.e., idoalatrous tents for impure purpose; Succothbenoth.

  • Assyrian or Babylonian deity worshipped by the Babylonians in Samaria
