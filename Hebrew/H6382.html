H6382 פֶּלֶא / פלא

peleʼ

(peh'-leh | peh-LEH | peh-LEH)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H6381]; a miracle; marvellous thing, wonder(-ful, -fully).

  • wonder, marvel
    • wonder (extraordinary, hard to understand thing)
    • wonder (of God's acts of judgment and redemption)
