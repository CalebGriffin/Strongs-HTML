H6491 פָּקַח / פקח

pâqach

(paw-kakh' | paw-KA | pa-KAHK)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to open (the senses, especially the eyes); figuratively, to be observant; open.

  • to open (the eyes)
    • (Qal)
      • to open (the eyes)
      • to open (the ears)
    • (Niphal) to be opened
