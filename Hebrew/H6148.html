H6148 עָרַב / ערב

ʻârab

(aw-rab' | aw-RAHB | ah-RAHV)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to braid, i.e., intermix; technically, to traffic (as if by barter); also or give to be security (as a kind of exchange); engage, (inter-)meddle (with), mingle (self), mortgage, occupy, give pledges, be(-come) (put in) surety, undertake.

  • to pledge, exchange, mortgage, engage, occupy, undertake for, give pledges, be or become surety, take on pledge, give in pledge
    • (Qal)
      • to take on pledge, go surety for
      • to give in pledge
      • to exchange
      • to pledge
    • (Hithpael)
      • to exchange pledges
      • to have fellowship with, share
