H3206 יֶלֶד / ילד

yeled

(yeh'-led | yeh-LED | yeh-LED)

Definition

masculine noun; from [H3205]; something born, i.e., a lad or offspring; boy, child, fruit, son, young man (one).

  • child, son, boy, offspring, youth
    • child, son, boy
    • child, children
    • descendants
    • youth
    • apostate Israelites (figuratively)
