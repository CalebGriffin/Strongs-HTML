H8033 שָׁם / שם

shâm

(shawm | shawm | shahm)

Definition

adverb; a primitive particle (rather from the relative pronoun, [H834]); there (transferring to time) then; often thither, or thence; in it, [lexicon-key|+] thence, there(-in), [lexicon-key|+] there(-of, -out), [lexicon-key|+] thither, [lexicon-key|+] whither.

  • there, thither
    • there
    • thither (after verbs of motion)
    • from there, thence
    • then (as an adverb of time)
