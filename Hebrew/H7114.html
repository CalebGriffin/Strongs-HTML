H7114 קָצַר / קצר

qâtsar

(kaw-tsar' | kaw-TSAHR | ka-TSAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to dock off, i.e., curtail (transitive or intransitive, literal or figurative); especially to harvest (grass or grain); [lexicon-key|×] at all, cut down, much discouraged, grieve, harvestman, lothe, mourn, reap(-er), (be, wax) short(-en, -er), straiten, trouble, vex.

  • to be short, be impatient, be vexed, be grieved
    • (Qal) to be short
    • (Piel) to shorten
    • (Hiphil) to shorten
  • to reap, harvest
    • (Qal) to reap, harvest
    • (Hiphil) to reap, harvest
