H1534 גַּלְגַּל / גלגל

galgal

(gal-gal' | ɡahl-ɡAHL | ɡahl-ɡAHL)

Definition

masculine noun; by reduplication from [H1556]; a wheel; by analogy, a whirlwind; also dust (as whirled); heaven, rolling thing, wheel.

  • wheel, whirl, whirlwind, whirling
    • wheel
    • whirl (of dust, chaff)
