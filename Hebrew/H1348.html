H1348 גֵּאוּת / גאות

gêʼûwth

(gay-ooth' | ɡay-OOT | ɡay-OOT)

Definition

feminine noun; from [H1342]; the same as H1346; excellent things, lifting up, majesty, pride, proudly, raging.

  • majesty
    • a rising up (of column of smoke)
    • a swelling (of sea)
    • majesty (of God)
    • pride
