H6934 קַדְמִיאֵל / קדמיאל

Qadmîyʼêl

(kad-mee-ale' | kahd-mee-ALE | kahd-mee-ALE)

Definition

proper masculine noun; God is the ancient one; from [H6924] and [H410]; presence of God; Kadmiel, the name of three Israelites; Kadmiel.

  • a Levite, descendant of Hodaviah, and head of a family of returning exiles; also oversaw the work on the temple and also was one of the leaders of the people who led the people in the public confession
