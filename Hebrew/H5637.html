H5637 סָרַר / סרר

çârar

(saw-rar' | saw-RAHR | sa-RAHR)

Definition

verb; a primitive root; to turn away, i.e., (morally) be refractory; [lexicon-key|×] away, backsliding, rebellious, revolter(-ing), slide back, stubborn, withdrew.

  • to rebel, be stubborn, be rebellious, be refractory
    • (Qal)
      • to be stubborn
      • stubborn, rebel (substantive)
