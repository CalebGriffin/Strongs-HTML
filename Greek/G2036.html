G2036 ἔπω

épō

(ep'-o | EP-oh | APE-oh)

Definition

a primary verb (used only in the definite past tense, the others being borrowed from [G2046], [G4483], and [G5346]); to speak or say (by word or writing); answer, bid, bring word, call, command, grant, say (on), speak, tell.

  • to speak, say

Note

Compare [G3004].
