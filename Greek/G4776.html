G4776 συγκαθίζω

synkathízō

(soong-kath-id'-zo | soong-ka-THEE-zoh | syoong-ka-THEE-zoh)

Definition

from [G4862] and [G2523]; to give (or take) a seat in company with; (make) sit (down) together.

  • to cause to sit down together, place together
  • to sit down together

See also

[G2523], [G4862]
