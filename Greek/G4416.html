G4416 πρωτότοκος

prōtótokos

(pro-tot-ok'-os | proh-TOH-toh-kose | proh-TOH-toh-kose)

Definition

from [G4413] and the alternate of [G5088]; first-born (usually as noun, literally or figuratively); firstbegotten(-born).

  • the firstborn
  • of man or beast
  • of Christ, the first born of all creation
