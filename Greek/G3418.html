G3418 μνῆμα

mnēma

(mnay'-mah | m-NAY-ma | m-NAY-ma)

Definition

from [G3415]; a memorial, i.e., sepulchral monument (burial-place); grave, sepulchre, tomb.

  • a monument or memorial to perpetuate the memory of any person or thing
  • a sepulchral monument
  • a sepulcher or tomb
