G784 ἄσπιλος

áspilos

(as'-pee-los | AH-spee-lose | AH-spee-lose)

Definition

from [G1] (as a negative particle) and [G4695]; unblemished (physically or morally); without spot, unspotted.

  • spotless
  • metaphorically
  • free from censure, irreproachable
  • free from vice, unsullied

See also

[G1], [G4695]
