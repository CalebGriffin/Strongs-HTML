G1893 ἐπεί

epeí

(ep-i' | ep-EE | ape-EE)

Definition

from [G1909] and [G1487]; thereupon, i.e., since (of time or cause); because, else, for that (then, -asmuch as), otherwise, seeing that, since, when.

  • when, since
  • of time: after
  • of cause: since, seeing that, because
