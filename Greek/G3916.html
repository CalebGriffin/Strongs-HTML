G3916 παραχρῆμα

parachrēma

(par-akh-ray'-mah | pa-ra-HRAY-ma | pa-ra-HRAY-ma)

Definition

from [G3844] and [G5536] (in its original sense); at the thing itself, i.e., instantly; forthwith, immediately, presently, straightway, soon.

  • immediately, forthwith, instantly
