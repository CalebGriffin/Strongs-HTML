G4848 συμπορεύομαι

symporeúomai

(soom-por-yoo'-om-ahee | soom-poh-REV-oh-meh | syoom-poh-RAVE-oh-may)

Definition

from [G4862] and [G4198]; to journey together; by implication, to assemble; go with, resort.

  • to go or journey together
  • to come together, to assemble

See also
