G2592 καρποφορέω

karpophoréō

(kar-pof-or-eh'-o | kahr-poh-foh-REH-oh | kahr-poh-foh-RAY-oh)

Definition

from [G2593]; to be fertile (literally or figuratively); be (bear, bring forth) fruit(-ful).

  • to bear fruit
  • to bear, bring forth, deeds
  • to bear fruit of one's self
