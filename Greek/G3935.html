G3935 παρίημι

paríēmi

(par-ee'-ay-mi | pa-REE-ay-mee | pa-REE-ay-mee)

Definition

from [G3844] and hiemi (to send); to let by, i.e., relax; hang down.

  • to let pass
  • to pass by, neglect
  • to disregard, omit
  • to relax, loose, let go
  • relaxed, unstrung, weakened, exhausted
