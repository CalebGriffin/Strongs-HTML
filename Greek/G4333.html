G4333 προσεργάζομαι

prosergázomai

(pros-er-gad'-zom-ahee | prose-er-GA-zoh-meh | prose-are-GA-zoh-may)

Definition

from [G4314] and [G2038]; to work additionally, i.e., (by implication) acquire besides; gain.

  • to work besides
  • by working or trading to make or gain besides

See also

[G2038], [G4314]
