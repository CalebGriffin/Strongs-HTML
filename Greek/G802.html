G802 ἀσύνθετος

asýnthetos

(as-oon'-thet-os | ah-SOON-theh-tose | ah-SYOON-thay-tose)

Definition

from [G1] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [G4934]; properly, not agreed, i.e., treacherous to compacts; covenant-breaker.

  • uncompounded, simple
  • covenant breaking, faithless
