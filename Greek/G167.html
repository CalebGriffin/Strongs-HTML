G167 ἀκαθαρσία

akatharsía

(ak-ath-ar-see'-ah | ah-ka-thahr-SEE-ah | ah-ka-thahr-SEE-ah)

Definition

from [G169]; impurity (the quality), physically or morally; uncleanness.

  • uncleanness
  • physical
  • in a moral sense: the impurity of lustful, luxurious, profligate living
    • of impure motives
