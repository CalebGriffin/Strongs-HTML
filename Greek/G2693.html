G2693 καταστρώννυμι

katastrṓnnymi

(kat-as-trone'-noo-mee | ka-ta-STRONE-noo-mee | ka-ta-STRONE-nyoo-mee)

Definition

from [G2596] and [G4766]; to strew down, i.e., (by implication) to prostrate (slay); overthrow.

  • to strew over (the ground)
  • to prostrate (compare "to lay low"), slay

See also

[G2596], [G4766]
