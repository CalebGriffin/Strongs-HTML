G2875 κόπτω

kóptō

(kop'-to | KOH-ptoh | KOH-ptoh)

Definition

a primary verb; to "chop"; specially, to beat the breast in grief; cut down, lament, mourn, (be-)wail.

  • to cut, strike, smite
  • to cut from, cut off
  • to beat one's breast for grief

Note

Compare the base of [G5114].
