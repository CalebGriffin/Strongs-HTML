G1064 γαστήρ

gastḗr

(gas-tare' | ga-STARE | ga-STARE)

Definition

of uncertain derivation; the stomach; by analogy, the matrix; figuratively, a gourmand; belly, [lexicon-key|+] with child, womb.

  • the belly
  • the womb
  • the stomach
  • a glutton, gormandiser, a man who is as it were all stomach
