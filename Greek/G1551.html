G1551 ἐκδέχομαι

ekdéchomai

(ek-dekh'-om-ahee | ek-THEH-hoh-meh | ake-THAY-hoh-may)

Definition

from [G1537] and [G1209]; to accept from some source, i.e., (by implication) to await; expect, look (tarry) for, wait (for).

  • to receive, accept
  • to look for, expect, wait for, await

See also

[G1209], [G1537]
