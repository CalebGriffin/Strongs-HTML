G5563 χωρίζω

chōrízō

(kho-rid'-zo | hoh-REE-zoh | hoh-REE-zoh)

Definition

from [G5561]; to place room between, i.e., part; reflexively, to go away; depart, put asunder, separate.

  • to separate, divide, part, put asunder, to separate one's self from, to depart
  • of divorce
  • to depart, go away
