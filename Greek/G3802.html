G3802 παγιδεύω

pagideúō

(pag-id-yoo'-o | pa-gee-THEV-oh | pa-gee-THAVE-oh)

Definition

from [G3803]; to ensnare (figuratively); entangle.

  • to ensnare, entrap
  • of birds
  • metaphorically of the attempt to elicit from one some remark which can be turned into an accusation against him
