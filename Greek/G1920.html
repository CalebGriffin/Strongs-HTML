G1920 ἐπιγίνομαι

epigínomai

(ep-ig-in'-om-ahee | eh-pee-GEE-noh-meh | ay-pee-GEE-noh-may)

Definition

from [G1909] and [G1096]; to arrive upon, i.e., spring up (as a wind); blow.

  • to become or happen afterwards
  • to be born after
  • to come to, arrive: of time
  • to arise, spring up, come on

See also
