G4205 πόρνος

pórnos

(por'-nos | PORE-nose | PORE-nose)

Definition

from πέρνημι (to sell; akin to the base of [G4097]); a (male) prostitute (as venal), i.e., (by analogy) a debauchee (libertine); fornicator, whoremonger.

  • a man who prostitutes his body to another's lust for hire
  • a male prostitute
  • a man who indulges in unlawful sexual intercourse, a fornicator
