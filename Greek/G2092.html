G2092 ἕτοιμος

hétoimos

(het-oy'-mos | HEH-too-mose | AY-too-mose)

Definition

from an old noun ἐτεός (fitness); adjusted, i.e., ready; prepared, (made) ready(-iness) (to our hand).

  • prepare ready
  • of things
    • ready at hand
    • opportune, seasonable
  • of persons
    • ready prepared
    • to do something
    • to receive one coming
