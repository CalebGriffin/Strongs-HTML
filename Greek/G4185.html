G4185 πολυτελής

polytelḗs

(pol-oo-tel-ace' | poh-loo-teh-LASE | poh-lyoo-tay-LASE)

Definition

from [G4183] and [G5056]; extremely expensive; costly, very precious, of great price.

  • precious
  • requiring very great outlay, very costly
  • excellent, of surpassing value
