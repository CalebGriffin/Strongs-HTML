G4939 σύντροφος

sýntrophos

(soon'-trof-os | SOON-troh-fose | SYOON-troh-fose)

Definition

from [G4862] and [G5162] (in a passive sense); a fellow-nursling, i.e., comrade; brought up with.

  • nourished with one
  • brought up with one
  • companion of one's childhood and youth

See also

[G4862], [G5162]
