G5037 τέ

té

(teh | teh | tay)

Definition

a primary particle (enclitic) of connection or addition; both or also (properly, as correlation of [G2532]); also, and, both, even, then, whether.

  • not only ... but also
  • both ... and
  • as ... so

Note

Often used in composition, usually as the latter participle.
