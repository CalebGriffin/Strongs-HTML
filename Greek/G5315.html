G5315 φάγω

phágō

(fag'-o | FA-goh | FA-goh)

Definition

a primary verb (used as an alternate of [G2068] in certain tenses); to eat (literally or figuratively); eat, meat.

  • to eat
  • to eat (consume) a thing
  • to take food, eat a meal
  • metaphorically to devour, consume
