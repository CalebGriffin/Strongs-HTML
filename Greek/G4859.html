G4859 σύμφωνος

sýmphōnos

(soom'-fo-nos | SOOM-foh-nose | SYOOM-foh-nose)

Definition

from [G4862] and [G5456]; sounding together (alike), i.e., (figuratively) accordant (neuter as noun, agreement); consent.

  • harmonious, accordant, agreeing
  • thing agreed upon, compact

See also

[G4862], [G5456]
