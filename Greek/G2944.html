G2944 κυκλόω

kyklóō

(koo-klo'-o | koo-KLOH-oh | kyoo-KLOH-oh)

Definition

from the same as [G2945]; to encircle, i.e., surround; compass (about), come (stand) round about.

  • to go around, lead around
  • to surround, encircle, encompass
  • of persons standing around
  • of besiegers
