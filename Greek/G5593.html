G5593 ψυχρός

psychrós

(psoo-chros' | psoo-HROSE | psyoo-HROSE)

Definition

from [G5592]; chilly (literally or figuratively); cold.

  • cold, cool
  • of cool water
  • metaphorically
  • cold i.e., sluggish, inert
  • in mind: of one destitute of warm Christian faith and the desire for holiness
