G4645 σκληρύνω

sklērýnō

(sklay-roo'-no | sklay-ROO-noh | sklay-RYOO-noh)

Definition

from [G4642]; to indurate, i.e., (figuratively) render stubborn; harden.

  • to make hard, harden
  • metaphorically
  • to render obstinate, stubborn
  • to be hardened
  • to become obstinate or stubborn
