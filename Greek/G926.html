G926 βαρύς

barýs

(bar-ooce' | va-ROOS | va-RYOOS)

Definition

from the same as [G922]; weighty, i.e., (fig) burdensome, grave; grievous, heavy, weightier.

  • heavy in weight
  • metaphorically
  • burdensome
  • severe, stern
  • weighty
    • of great moment
  • violent, cruel, unsparing
