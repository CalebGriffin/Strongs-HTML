G4881 συναπόλλυμι

synapóllymi

(soon-ap-ol'-loo-mee | soon-ah-POLE-loo-mee | syoon-ah-POLE-lyoo-mee)

Definition

from [G4862] and [G622]; to destroy (middle voice or passively, be slain) in company with; perish with.

  • to destroy together
  • to perish together (to be slain along with)

See also

[G622], [G4862]
