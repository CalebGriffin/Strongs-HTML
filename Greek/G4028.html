G4028 περικαλύπτω

perikalýptō

(per-ee-kal-oop'-to | peh-ree-ka-LOO-ptoh | pay-ree-ka-LYOO-ptoh)

Definition

from [G4012] and [G2572]; to cover all around, i.e., entirely (the face, a surface); blindfold, cover, overlay.

  • to cover all around, to cover up, cover over

See also

[G2572], [G4012]
