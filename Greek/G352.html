G352 ἀνακύπτω

anakýptō

(an-ak-oop'-to | ah-na-KOO-ptoh | ah-na-KYOO-ptoh)

Definition

from [G303] (in the sense of reversal) and [G2955]; to unbend, i.e., rise; figuratively, be elated; lift up, look up.

  • to raise or lift one's self up
  • one's body
  • one's soul
    • to be elated or exalted
