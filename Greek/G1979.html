G1979 ἐπισιτισμός

episitismós

(ep-ee-sit-is-mos' | eh-pee-see-tee-SMOSE | ay-pee-see-tee-SMOSE)

Definition

from a compound of [G1909] and a derivative of [G4621]; a provisioning, i.e., (concretely) food; victuals.

  • foraging, providing food
  • supplies, provisions, food

See also

[G1909], [G4621]
