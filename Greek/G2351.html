G2351 θόρυβος

thórybos

(thor'-oo-bos | THOH-roo-vose | THOH-ryoo-vose)

Definition

from the base of [G2360]; a disturbance; tumult, uproar.

  • a noise, tumult, uproar
  • of persons wailing
  • of a clamorous and excited multitude
  • of riotous persons
  • a tumult, as a breach of public order
