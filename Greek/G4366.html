G4366 προσρήγνυμι

prosrḗgnymi

(pros-rayg'-noo-mee | prose-RAY-gnoo-mee | prose-RAY-gnyoo-mee)

Definition

from [G4314] and [G4486]; to tear towards, i.e., burst upon (as a tempest or flood); beat vehemently against (upon).

  • to break against, break by dashing against

See also

[G4314], [G4486]
