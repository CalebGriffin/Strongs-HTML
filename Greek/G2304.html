G2304 θεῖος

theîos

(thi'-os | THEE-ose | THEE-ose)

Definition

from [G2316]; godlike (neuter as noun, divinity); divine, godhead.

  • a general name of deities or divinities as used by the Greeks
  • spoken of the only and true God, trinity
  • of Christ
  • Holy Spirit
  • the Father
