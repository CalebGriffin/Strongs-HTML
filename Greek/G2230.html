G2230 ἡγεμονεύω

hēgemoneúō

(hayg-em-on-yoo'-o | hay-geh-moh-NEV-oh | ay-gay-moh-NAVE-oh)

Definition

from [G2232]; to act as ruler; be governor.

  • to be leader, to lead the way
  • to rule, command
  • of a province, to be governor of a province
  • said of a proconsul, of a procurator
