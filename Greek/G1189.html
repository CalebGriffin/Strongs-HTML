G1189 δέομαι

déomai

(deh'-om-ahee | THEH-oh-meh | THAY-oh-may)

Definition

middle voice of [G1210]; to beg (as binding oneself), i.e., petition; beseech, pray (to), make request.

  • to want, lack
  • to desire, long for
  • to ask, beg
  • the thing asked for
  • to pray, make supplications

Note

Compare [G4441].
