G5001 τάγμα

tágma

(tag'-mah | TAHG-ma | TAHG-ma)

Definition

from [G5021]; something orderly in arrangement (a troop), i.e., (figuratively) a series or succession; order.

  • that which has been arranged, thing placed in order
  • a body of soldiers, a corps
  • band, troop, class
