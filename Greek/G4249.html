G4249 πρίζω

prízō

(prid'-zo | PREE-zoh | PREE-zoh)

Definition

a strengthened form of a primary prio (to saw); to saw in two; saw asunder.

  • to saw, to cut in two with a saw To be "sawn asunder" was a kind of punishment among the Hebrews, which according to tradition was inflicted on the prophet Isaiah
