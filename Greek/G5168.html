G5168 τρυμαλιά

trymaliá

(troo-mal-ee-ah' | troo-ma-lee-AH | tryoo-ma-lee-AH)

Definition

from a derivative of τρύω (to wear away; akin to the base of [G5134], [G5147] and [G5176]); an orifice, i.e., needle's eye; eye.

  • a hole, (eye of the needle)

Note

Compare [G5169].
