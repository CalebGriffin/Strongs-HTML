G2214 ζήτησις

zḗtēsis

(dzay'-tay-sis | ZAY-tay-sees | ZAY-tay-sees)

Definition

from [G2212]; a searching (properly, the act), i.e., a dispute or its theme; question.

  • a seeking
  • enquiry
  • a questioning, debate
  • a subject of questioning or debate, matter of controversy
