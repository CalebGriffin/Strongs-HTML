G4911 συνεφίστημι

synephístēmi

(soon-ef-is'-tay-mee | soon-eh-FEE-stay-mee | syoon-ay-FEE-stay-mee)

Definition

from [G4862] and [G2186]; to stand up together, i.e., to resist (or assault) jointly; rise up together.

  • to place over or appoint together
  • to rise up together
  • against one

See also

[G2186], [G4862]
