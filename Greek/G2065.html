G2065 ἐρωτάω

erōtáō

(er-o-tah'-o | eh-roh-TA-oh | ay-roh-TA-oh)

Definition

apparently from [G2046] (compare [G2045]); to interrogate; by implication, to request; ask, beseech, desire, intreat, pray.

  • to question
  • to ask
  • to request, entreat, beg, beseech

Note

Compare [G4441].
