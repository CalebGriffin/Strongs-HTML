G206 ἄκρον

ákron

(ak'-ron | AH-krone | AH-krone)

Definition

neuter of an adjective probably akin to the base of [G188]; the extremity; one end... other, tip, top, uttermost participle.

  • the farthest bounds, uttermost parts, end, highest, extreme
  • of the earth
  • of heaven
