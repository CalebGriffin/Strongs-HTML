G61 ἄγρα

ágra

(ag'-rah | AH-gra | AH-gra)

Definition

from [G71]; (abstractly) a catching (of fish); also (concretely) a haul (of fish); draught.

  • a catching, hunting
  • that which is taken in hunting, the booty, prey
  • of birds or beasts, game
  • of fish, a draught or haul
