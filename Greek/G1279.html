G1279 διαπορεύομαι

diaporeúomai

(dee-ap-or-yoo'-om-ahee | thee-ah-poh-REV-oh-meh | thee-ah-poh-RAVE-oh-may)

Definition

from [G1223] and [G4198]; to travel through; go through, journey in, pass by.

  • to cause to pass through a place
  • to carry across
  • to journey through a place, go through
  • to travel through

See also

[G1223], [G4198]
