G2601 καταβιβάζω

katabibázō

(kat-ab-ib-ad'-zo | ka-ta-vee-VA-zoh | ka-ta-vee-VA-zoh)

Definition

from [G2596] and a derivative of the base of [G939]; to cause to go down, i.e., precipitate; bring (thrust) down.

  • to cause to go down
  • to bring down
  • to cast down, thrust down

See also

[G939], [G2596]
