G4769 στῦλος

stŷlos

(stoo'-los | STOO-lose | STYOO-lose)

Definition

from στύω (to stiffen; properly akin to the base of [G2476]); a post ("style"), i.e., (figuratively) support; pillar.

  • a pillar
  • a column
  • pillars of fire i.e., flames rising like pillars
  • a prop or support
