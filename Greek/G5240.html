G5240 ὑπερεκχύνω

hyperekchýnō

(hoop-er-ek-khoo'-no | hoo-per-ek-HOO-noh | yoo-pare-ake-HYOO-noh)

Definition

from [G5228] and the alternate form of [G1632]; to pour out over, i.e., (passively) to overflow; run over.

  • to pour out beyond measure
  • to overflow, run over
