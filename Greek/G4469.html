G4469 ῥακά

rhaká

(rhak-ah' | ra-KA | ra-KA)

Definition

of Chaldee origin (compare [H7386]); O empty one, i.e., thou worthless (as a term of utter vilification); Raca.

  • empty, i.e., a senseless, empty headed man
  • a term of reproach used among the Jews in the time of Christ
