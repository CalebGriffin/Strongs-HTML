G3859 παραδιατριβή

paradiatribḗ

(par-ad-ee-at-ree-bay' | pa-ra-thee-ah-tree-VAY | pa-ra-thee-ah-tree-VAY)

Definition

from a compound of [G3844] and [G1304]; misemployment, i.e., meddlesomeness; perverse disputing.

  • useless occupation, empty business, misemployment
