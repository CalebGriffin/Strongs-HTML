G5003 ταλαιπωρέω

talaipōréō

(tal-ahee-po-reh'-o | ta-leh-poh-REH-oh | ta-lay-poh-RAY-oh)

Definition

from [G5005]; to be wretched, i.e., realize one's own misery; be afflicted.

  • to toil heavily, to endure labors and hardships
  • to be afflicted
  • to feel afflicted and miserable
  • to afflict
