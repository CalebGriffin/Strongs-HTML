G1547 ἐκγαμίζω

ekgamízō

(ek-gam-id'-zo | ek-ga-MEE-zoh | ake-ga-MEE-zoh)

Definition

from [G1537] and a form of [G1061] (compare [G1548]); to marry off a daughter; give in marriage.

  • to give away in marriage: a daughter
  • to marry, to be given in marriage

See also

[G1061], [G1537], [G1548]
