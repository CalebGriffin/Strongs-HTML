G180 ἀκατάπαυστος

akatápaustos

(ak-at-ap'-ow-stos | ah-ka-TA-pav-stose | ah-ka-TA-paf-stose)

Definition

from [G1] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [G2664]; unrefraining; that cannot cease.

  • unable to stop, unceasing
  • not quieted, that cannot be quieted
