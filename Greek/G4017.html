G4017 περιβλέπω

periblépō

(per-ee-blep'-o | peh-ree-VLEH-poh | pay-ree-VLAY-poh)

Definition

from [G4012] and [G991]; to look all around; look (round) about (on).

  • to look around
  • to look around about one's self
  • to look round on one (i.e., to look for one's self at one near by)

See also

[G991], [G4012]
