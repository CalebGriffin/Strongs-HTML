G1439 ἐάω

eáō

(eh-ah'-o | eh-AH-oh | ay-AH-oh)

Definition

of uncertain affinity; to let be, i.e., permit or leave alone; commit, leave, let (alone), suffer.

  • to allow, permit, let
  • to allow one to do as he wishes, not to restrain, to let alone
  • to give up, let go, leave
