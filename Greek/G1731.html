G1731 ἐνδείκνυμι

endeíknymi

(en-dike'-noo-mee | en-THEE-knoo-mee | ane-THEE-knyoo-mee)

Definition

from [G1722] and [G1166]; to indicate (by word or act); do, show (forth).

  • to point out
  • to show, demonstrate, prove, whether by arguments or by acts
  • to manifest, display, put forth

See also

[G1166], [G1722]
