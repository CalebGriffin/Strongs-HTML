G3782 ὀφειλή

opheilḗ

(of-i-lay' | oh-fee-LAY | oh-fee-LAY)

Definition

from [G3784]; indebtedness, i.e., (concretely) a sum owed; figuratively, obligation, i.e., (conjugal) duty; debt, due.

  • that which is owed
  • a debt
  • metaphorically dues: specifically of conjugal duty
