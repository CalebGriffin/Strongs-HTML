G3745 ὅσος

hósos

(hos'-os | HOH-sose | OH-sose)

Definition

by reduplication from [G3739]; as (much, great, long, etc.) as; all (that), as (long, many, much) (as), how great (many, much), (in-)asmuch as, so many as, that (ever), the more, those things, what (great, -soever), wheresoever, wherewithsoever, which, [lexicon-key|X] while, who(-soever).

  • as great as, as far as, how much, how many, whoever
