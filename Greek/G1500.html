G1500 εἰκῆ

eikē

(i-kay' | ee-KAY | ee-KAY)

Definition

probably from [G1502] (through the idea of failure); idly, i.e., without reason (or effect); without a cause, (in) vain(-ly).

  • inconsiderably, without purpose, without just cause
  • in vain
  • without success or effort
