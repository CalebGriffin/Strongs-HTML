G214 ἀλαλάζω

alalázō

(al-al-ad'-zo | ah-la-LA-zoh | ah-la-LA-zoh)

Definition

from ἀλαλή ( a shout, "halloo"); to vociferate, i.e., (by implication) to wail; figuratively, to clang; tinkle, wail.

  • to repeat frequently the cry "alala" as soldiers used to do on entering into battle
  • to utter a joyful sound
  • to wail, lament
  • to ring loudly, to clang
