G3739 ὅς

hós

(hos | hose | ose)

Definition

probably a primary word (or perhaps a form of the article [G3588]); the relatively (sometimes demonstrative) pronoun, who, which, what, that; one, (an-, the) other, some, that, what, which, who(-m, -se), etc.

  • who, which, what, that
