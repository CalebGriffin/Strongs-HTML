G3068 λούω

loúō

(loo'-o | LOO-oh | LOO-oh)

Definition

a primary verb; to bathe (the whole person; whereas [G3538] means to wet a part only, and [G4150] to wash, cleanse garments exclusively); wash.

  • to bathe, wash
  • of a dead person
  • washing to cleanse blood out of wounds

See also

[G3538], [G4150]
