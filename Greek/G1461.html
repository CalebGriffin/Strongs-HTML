G1461 ἐγκεντρίζω

enkentrízō

(eng-ken-trid'-zo | eng-ken-TREE-zoh | ayng-kane-TREE-zoh)

Definition

from [G1722] and a derivative of [G2759]; to prick in, i.e., ingraft; graff in(-to).

  • to cut into for the sake of inserting a scion
  • to inoculate, ingraft, graft in

See also

[G1722], [G2759]
