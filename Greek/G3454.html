G3454 μῦθος

mŷthos

(moo'-thos | MOO-those | MYOO-those)

Definition

perhaps from the same as [G3453] (through the idea of tuition); a tale, i.e., fiction ("myth"); fable.

  • a speech, word, saying
  • a narrative, story
  • a true narrative
  • a fiction, a fable
    • an invention, a falsehood
