G2168 εὐχαριστέω

eucharistéō

(yoo-khar-is-teh'-o | ev-ha-ree-STEH-oh | afe-ha-ree-STAY-oh)

Definition

from [G2170]; to be grateful, i.e., (actively) to express gratitude (towards); specially, to say grace at a meal; (give) thank(-ful, -s).

  • to be grateful, feel thankful
  • give thanks
