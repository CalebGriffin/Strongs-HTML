G2704 καταφθείρω

kataphtheírō

(kat-af-thi'-ro | ka-ta-FTHEE-roh | ka-ta-FTHEE-roh)

Definition

from [G2596] and [G5351]; to spoil entirely, i.e., (literally) to destroy; or (figuratively) to deprave; corrupt, utterly perish.

  • to corrupt, deprave
  • corrupted in mind
  • to destroy
  • to be destroyed, to perish

See also

[G2596], [G5351]
