G5104 τοί

toí

(toy | too | too)

Definition

probably for the dative case of [G3588]; an enclitic particle of asseveration by way of contrast; in sooth; (used only with other particles in the comparative, as [G2544], [G3305], [G5105], [G5106], etc.).

  • certainly, truly
