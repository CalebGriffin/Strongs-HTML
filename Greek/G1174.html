G1174 δεισιδαιμονέστερος

deisidaimonésteros

(dice-ee-dahee-mon-es'-ter-os | thee-see-theh-moh-NEH-steh-rose | thee-see-thay-moh-NAY-stay-rose)

Definition

the compound of a derivative of the base of [G1169] and [G1142]; more religious than others; too superstitious.

  • in a good sense
  • reverencing god or the gods, pious, religious
  • in a bad sense
  • superstitious

See also

[G1142], [G1169]
