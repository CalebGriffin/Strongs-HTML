G597 ἀποθησαυρίζω

apothēsaurízō

(ap-oth-ay-sow-rid'-zo | ah-poh-thay-sev-REE-zoh | ah-poh-thay-sa-REE-zoh)

Definition

from [G575] and [G2343]; to treasure away; lay up in store.

  • to put away, lay by in store, to treasure away
  • to store up abundance for future use
