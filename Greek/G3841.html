G3841 παντοκράτωρ

pantokrátōr

(pan-tok-rat'-ore | pahn-toh-KRA-tore | pahn-toh-KRA-tore)

Definition

from [G3956] and [G2904]; the all-ruling, i.e., God (as absolute and universal sovereign); Almighty, Omnipotent.

  • he who holds sway over all things
  • the ruler of all
  • almighty: God

See also

[G2904], [G3956]
