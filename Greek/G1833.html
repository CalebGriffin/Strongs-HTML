G1833 ἐξετάζω

exetázō

(ex-et-ad'-zo | eks-eh-TA-zoh | ayks-ay-TA-zoh)

Definition

from [G1537] and ἐτάζω (to examine); to test thoroughly (by questions), i.e., ascertain or interrogate; ask, enquire, search.

  • to search out
  • to examine strictly, enquire
  • enquire of someone
