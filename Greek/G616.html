G616 ἀποκυέω

apokyéō

(ap-ok-oo-eh'-o | ah-poh-koo-EH-oh | ah-poh-kyoo-A-oh)

Definition

from [G575] and the base of [G2949]; to breed forth, i.e., (by transference) to generate (figuratively); beget, produce.

  • to bring forth
  • from the womb
  • give birth to
  • produce

See also

[G575], [G2949]
