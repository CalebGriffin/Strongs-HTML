G4303 προμαρτύρομαι

promartýromai

(prom-ar-too'-rom-ahee | proh-mahr-TOO-roh-meh | proh-mahr-TYOO-roh-may)

Definition

from [G4253] and [G3143]; to be a witness in advance i.e., predict; testify beforehand.

  • antetestor
  • to testify beforehand
  • to make known, predict

See also

[G3143], [G4253]
