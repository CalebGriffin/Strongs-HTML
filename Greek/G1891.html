G1891 Ἐπαφρόδιτος

Epaphróditos

(ep-af-rod'-ee-tos | ep-ah-FROH-thee-tose | ape-ah-FROH-thee-tose)

Definition

lovely; from [G1909] (in the sense of devoted to) and Ἀφροδίτη (Venus); Epaphroditus, a Christian; Epaphroditus.
