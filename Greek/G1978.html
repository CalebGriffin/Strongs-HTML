G1978 ἐπίσημος

epísēmos

(ep-is'-ay-mos | eh-PEE-say-mose | ay-PEE-say-mose)

Definition

from [G1909] and some form of the base of [G4591]; remarkable, i.e., (figuratively) eminent; notable, of note.

  • having a mark on it, marked, stamped, coined
  • marked
  • in a good sense
    • of note, illustrious
  • in a bad sense
    • notorious, infamous

See also

[G1909], [G4591]
