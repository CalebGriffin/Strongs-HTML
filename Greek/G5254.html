G5254 ὑπέχω

hypéchō

(hoop-ekh'-o | hoo-PEH-hoh | yoo-PAY-hoh)

Definition

from [G5259] and [G2192]; to hold oneself under, i.e., endure with patience; suffer.

  • to hold under, to put under, place underneath
  • metaphorically to sustain, undergo
  • suffer punishment

See also
