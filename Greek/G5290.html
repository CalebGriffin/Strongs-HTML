G5290 ὑποστρέφω

hypostréphō

(hoop-os-tref'-o | hoo-poh-STREH-foh | yoo-poh-STRAY-foh)

Definition

from [G5259] and [G4762]; to turn under (behind), i.e., to return (literally or figuratively); come again, return (again, back again), turn back (again).

  • to turn back
  • to turn about
  • to return

See also

[G4762], [G5259]
