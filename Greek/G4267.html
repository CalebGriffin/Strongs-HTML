G4267 προγινώσκω

proginṓskō

(prog-in-oce'-ko | proh-gee-NOH-skoh | proh-gee-NOH-skoh)

Definition

from [G4253] and [G1097]; to know beforehand, i.e., foresee; foreknow (ordain), know (before).

  • to have knowledge before hand
  • to foreknow
  • of those whom God elected to salvation
  • to predestinate

See also

[G1097], [G4253]
