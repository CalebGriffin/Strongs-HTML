G1175 δεισιδαιμονία

deisidaimonía

(dice-ee-dahee-mon-ee'-ah | thee-see-theh-moh-NEE-ah | thee-see-thay-moh-NEE-ah)

Definition

from the same as [G1174]; religion; superstition.

  • in a good sense
  • reverencing god or the gods, pious, religious
  • in a bad sense
  • superstitious
  • religious
