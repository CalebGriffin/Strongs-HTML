G1114 γόης

góēs

(go'-ace | GOH-ase | GOH-ase)

Definition

from γοάω (to wail); properly, a wizard (as muttering spells), i.e., (by implication) an imposter; seducer.

  • a wailer, a howler
  • a juggler, enchanter (because incantations used to be uttered in a kind of howl)
  • a deceiver, imposter
