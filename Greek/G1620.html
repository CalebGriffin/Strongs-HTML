G1620 ἐκτίθημι

ektíthēmi

(ek-tith'-ay-mee | ek-TEE-thay-mee | ake-TEE-thay-mee)

Definition

from [G1537] and [G5087]; to expose; figuratively, to declare; cast out, expound.

  • to place or set out, put outside, expose
  • to set up, exhibit
  • metaphorically to set forth, declare, expound
