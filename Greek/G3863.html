G3863 παραζηλόω

parazēlóō

(par-ad-zay-lo'-o | pa-ra-zay-LOH-oh | pa-ra-zay-LOH-oh)

Definition

from [G3844] and [G2206]; to stimulate alongside, i.e., excite to rivalry; provoke to emulation (jealousy).

  • to provoke to
  • to provoke to jealously or rivalry
  • to provoke to anger

See also

[G2206], [G3844]
