G3833 πανοπλία

panoplía

(pan-op-lee'-ah | pa-noh-PLEE-ah | pa-noh-PLEE-ah)

Definition

from a compound of [G3956] and [G3696]; full armor ("panoply"); all (whole) armour.

  • full armour, complete armour
  • includes shield, sword, lance, helmet, greaves, and breastplate

See also

[G3696], [G3956]
