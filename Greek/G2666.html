G2666 καταπίνω

katapínō

(kat-ap-ee'-no | ka-ta-PEE-noh | ka-ta-PEE-noh)

Definition

from [G2596] and [G4095]; to drink down, i.e., gulp entire (literally or figuratively); devour, drown, swallow (up).

  • to drink down, swallow down
  • to devour
  • to swallow up, destroy

See also

[G2596], [G4095]
