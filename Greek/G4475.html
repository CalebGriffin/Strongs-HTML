G4475 ῥάπισμα

rhápisma

(hrap'-is-mah | RA-pee-sma | RA-pee-sma)

Definition

from [G4474]; a slap; ([lexicon-key|+] strike with the) palm of the hand, smite with the hand.

  • a blow with a rod or staff or a scourge
  • a blow with the flat of the hand, a slap in the face, box the ear
