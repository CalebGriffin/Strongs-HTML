G4884 συναρπάζω

synarpázō

(soon-ar-pad'-zo | soon-ar-PA-zoh | syoon-ar-PA-zoh)

Definition

from [G4862] and [G726]; to snatch together, i.e., seize; catch.

  • to seize by force
  • to catch or lay hold of (one so that he is no longer his own master)
  • to seize by force and carry away

See also

[G726], [G4862]
